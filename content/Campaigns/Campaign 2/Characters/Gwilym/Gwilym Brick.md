> [!info] Brick (Owl Familiar)
> **HP:** 1/1 | **AC:** 11 | **Speed:** 5 ft, Fly 60 ft
> **Passives:** Perception: `dice: 1d20+3` | Stealth: `dice: 1d20+3` | **Senses:** Darkvision 120 ft

**Actions & Traits**
* **Help (Action):** Brick dive-bombs an enemy to distract them, giving you or an ally Advantage on your next attack roll against that enemy. 
* **Flyby:** Brick doesn't provoke opportunity attacks when he flies out of an enemy's reach. (Use this to fly in, Help, and fly safely away).
* **Keen Hearing and Sight:** Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or sight.
* **Telepathy:** You can communicate telepathically with Brick while he is within 100 feet of you. As an action, you can see through his eyes and hear what he hears until the start of your next turn.
