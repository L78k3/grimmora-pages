## 🧬 Magic Reference (The "Protest Medic" Toolkit)
* **Spell Attack Bonus:** +5
* **Spell Save DC:** 13
* **Mage Armor (1st Level):** Base AC becomes 13 + DEX modifier (15 total). Lasts 8 hours.
* **Sleep (1st Level):** Roll 5d8; put that many hit points worth of creatures to sleep. No saving throw. Non-lethal crowd control.
* **Minor Illusion (Cantrip):** Create a sound or an image of an object within 30 feet. Used to hide basement doors or distract guards during a sweep.
* **Message (Cantrip):** Point your finger and whisper a message to a target within 120 feet. Used to quietly tip off worshippers of approaching danger.
* **Mage Hand (Cantrip):** A spectral, floating hand that can manipulate objects up to 30 feet away.
* **Ritual Caster:** Can cast *Detect Magic*, *Find Familiar* (Brick), and *Identify* without using spell slots if an extra 10 minutes is taken.
