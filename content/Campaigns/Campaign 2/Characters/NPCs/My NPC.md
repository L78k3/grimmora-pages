**Bishop Greggan** is a fanatical Inquisitor for the Disciples of War, ruling over the town of Hjemmen with an iron fist. 

## Biography & Personality
Unlike most Inquisitors who might ignore minor infractions or harmless worshippers (such as those of Sune), Greggan is a zealot. He actively hunts down heretics of any stripe and is known for orchestrating brutal sweeps through the streets of Hjemmen. He believes entirely in the rigid hierarchy and totalitarian control of the Triumvirate. 

He is responsible for the systematic rounding up of street urchins in Hjemmen, sending them off to state institutions to be broken down and trained as clerics. He represents everything Gwilym despises about the state.

## Campaign History
* **Pre-Campaign:** Arrested and "disappeared" the families of several hidden worshippers in Hjemmen. Led the street sweep that resulted in the capture of Gwilym and the scattering of his found family.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Gwilym|Gwilym Lackmount]]:** Greggan is entirely unaware of Gwilym's double life, but serves as the primary architect of Gwilym's childhood trauma.

## Notable Equipment
* **Ledgers of the Inquisition:** Likely possesses records of where he sends the captured urchins and heretics.
