**The Mentor** is an older street urchin from [[Hjemmen]] who acted as a surrogate sibling and protector to Gwilym before the Inquisitor sweeps tore them apart.

## Biography & Personality
A hardened but fiercely loyal street survivor. They taught Gwilym how to navigate the alleys of Hjemmen, how to steal to survive, and how to stay out of the sight of the Disciples of War. Their ultimate fate is unknown - whether they were killed, captured and sent to an institution, or managed to escape the sweep that took Gwilym.

## Campaign History
* **Pre-Campaign:** Protected Gwilym on the streets. Disappeared during the sweep orchestrated by Bishop Greggan.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Gwilym|Gwilym Lackmount]]:** Acted as Gwilym's protector and older sibling figure. Gwilym is actively searching for clues to their fate.
