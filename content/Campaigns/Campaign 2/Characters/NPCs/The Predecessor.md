**The Predecessor** is Master Drev's beloved former apprentice who died under mysterious circumstances, leaving a massive shadow that Gwilym is forced to live in.

## Biography & Personality
Very little is currently known about her personality or origins, other than the fact that she was highly competent and deeply respected by Master Drev. She had trained under the irascible half-orc for long enough that she was nearly ready to take over his duties and responsibilities regarding his abjuration studies and his restricted library. 

Her death occurred under suspicious circumstances. Master Drev mourns her deeply and considers her utterly irreplaceable, a fact he makes abundantly clear to his new apprentice, Gwilym. 

## Campaign History
* **Pre-Campaign:** Trained extensively under Master Drev. Died suspiciously before Gwilym was captured and brought to the tower.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Master Drev]]:** Her former master, who held her in incredibly high regard and still grieves her loss.
* **[[Gwilym|Gwilym Lackmount]]:** Her reluctant replacement. Gwilym is constantly compared to her, but he actively uses Drev's lingering grief - and resulting low expectations of Gwilym - as a convenient cover for his subversive activities.
