# Brick

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Fey Spirit (Owl Form)
> 
> **Class:** Familiar
> 
> **Affiliation:** [[Gwilym]]
> 
> **Location:** [[Master Drev's Tower]]
> 
> **Status:** Alive

**Brick** is an ancient, grumpy fey spirit summoned by [[Gwilym]] via the *Find Familiar* spell to act as a scout and companion.

## Biography & Personality
Taking the physical form of a massive, scruffy Great Horned Owl, Brick is completely loyal to [[Gwilym]] and his quiet resistance against the Triumvirate. However, he expresses this support entirely through exhausted compliance, heavy sighs, and slow blinking. He relies on his keen sight and hearing to act as a scout, and is capable of telepathically communicating with his summoner.

## Campaign History
* **Pre-Campaign:** Summoned by [[Gwilym]] to assist in his double life, scouting ahead during excursions to nearby towns.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Gwilym]]:** His summoner. Brick assists him in his dangerous endeavors, though seemingly with great reluctance and annoyance.

## Notable Equipment
* **None:** As a summoned spirit in animal form, Brick carries no equipment.
