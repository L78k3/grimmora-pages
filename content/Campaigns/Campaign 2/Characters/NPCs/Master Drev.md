**Master Drev** is a cantankerous, 69-year-old scholar of magical wards who reluctantly took Gwilym on as his apprentice after an Inquisitor sweep.

## Biography & Personality
Drev is highly advanced in years and nearing the end of his life. His name carries significant weight in scholarly circles, particularly regarding his knowledge of wards and magical shields written in obscure tongues. He has a reputation for being fiercely irascible and prefers the isolation of his tower over the politics of the capital. 

He is deeply grieving the loss of his previous apprentice, who was killed in suspicious circumstances just as she was ready to take over his duties. He views Gwilym as a pale imitation and a brainwashed state stooge, often treating him poorly. Ironically, Drev's own scrying spell is what caused Gwilym to be captured by the Inquisition in the first place, though Drev subsequently pulled rank to claim the boy before he could be institutionalized as a cleric.

## Campaign History
* **Pre-Campaign:** Cast a scrying spell that distracted Gwilym during an Inquisitor sweep in Hjemmen, leading to the boy's capture. Intervened to claim Gwilym as his apprentice.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Gwilym|Gwilym Lackmount]]:** His current apprentice. Drev resents him but relies on him to scout nearby towns for rare texts.
* **[[The Predecessor]]:** His former apprentice, whom he mourns deeply and considers irreplaceable.

## Notable Equipment
* **Restricted Library Keys:** Drev holds access to texts deemed highly secret or dangerous by the Triumvirate.
