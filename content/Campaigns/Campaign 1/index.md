# Rime of the Soul-Stolen

> [!abstract] Campaign Overview
> A continent-spanning struggle against a rising necromantic order marked by the skull-and-diamond-eye symbol. What began as a rescue mission for a missing noblewoman has evolved into a journey to prevent environmental sabotage and liberate a trapped goddess.

---

## 📜 Narrative Movements

### Movement I: The Vanishing at Durvish
The party forms at a low-end inn in [[Durvish City]] to clear a room of spectres. They witness the dramatic abduction of [[Lady Bromelia]] from her wedding to [[Lord Requiem]] by a man in black who escapes on a griffin.
* **Key Milestone:** Tracking the griffin north past Avalon.

### Movement II: The Champion’s Ascent
The party faces the trials of [[The Frost]], discovering that the "plague" in [[Mightrest]] is a [[Vampire Lord]] using the town as a larder. High in the mountains, they face the trials of the goddess [[Auriel]].
* **Key Milestone:** [[Lavender]] becomes the champion of Auriel after a sacrificial trial in the amphitheatre.

### Movement III: The Architect of Shadows
The party discovers [[The Displaced]], a rebel group led by the "kidnapped" Bromelia. They learn that Lavender’s brother, [[Orris Locke]], is a high-ranking member of the [[Necromancers]] in [[Arcadia]].
* **Key Milestone:** A tense parley with Orris followed by an undead ambush in the Arcadia town square.

### Movement IV: The Imperial Thread (Current)
While escorting [[Mary-Ann]] and rescuing a cursed courier, the party intercepts five [[Encoded Letters]] revealing a vast conspiracy involving "Operation Green Spill" and the hunt for "Winter's Core".
* **Key Milestone:** Learning that the [[Ring of Orris]] likely contains the essence of the goddess Auriel.
