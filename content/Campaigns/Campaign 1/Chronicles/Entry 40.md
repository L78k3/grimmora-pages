# Day 29

The whole party awakens looking a little green. [[Lavender]] is the only one who is fine. [[Mary-Ann]] has tea and goes back to bed. [[Theren]] is very peaky.

[[Captain Voss|Voss]] speaks to [[Lavender]], telling her the wind has dropped and the oars need to be used. The journey might take a little longer, as the sea is like a millpond. [[Lavender]] is concerned at how unnatural the calm seems.

[[Temerity]] stumbles onto deck and offers to give the boat a boost. [[Lavender]] cringes. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] is unsure if this will be a good idea. [[Temerity]] offers to turn into a whale and pull the ship. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] is taken aback by this. She declines and tells [[Temerity]] he should save his magic. [[Temerity]] looks over the side of the boat but cannot see any wildlife due to the glare off the water. He sits against the mast to try and alleviate the rocking and provides some ambience music. [[Lavender]] wanders to the rail and admires the horizon. [[Gage]] practises his knots.

Much of the day passes. Later in the afternoon, as the sun is lowering, most of the party have recovered. [[Theren]] stands on deck taking in the sea air. [[Mary-Ann]] and [[Gage]] and are interfering in the galley. [[Lavender]] is running drills for the sailors. [[Temerity]] is climbing up the mast but his hooves make it difficult – he doesn’t manage to get up there. 

A shout from the crow’s nest tells them that a ship is approaching on the starboard side. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] looks through her telescope – the colour drains from her face. The Frozen Wraith, a sunken ship, has been raised – it sank 3 weeks ago. The ship pulls alongside – it is dark wood covered in ice with torn sails, but filled with an unnatural wind. Twelve undead figures, plus the captain, are moving jerkily around on deck.

A voice colder than the grave calls out – the Master of Ash orders them to hand over the [[Ring of Orris|ring]] containing the [[Winter's Core]]. 

[[Gage]] climbs up the rigging but gets tangled in the rope. He casts hunter’s mark on the captain of the enemy ship. He fires his bow at the captain but the arrow falls into the water. The [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]] crew man their stations – two manage the sails, two navigate and two on emergency repairs. The navigators do not manage to plot the course; the sails are prepped effectively; the repairers stand by for damage. [[Lavender]] watches the enemy crew girding for war. She gets [[Anaphel]] to carry her over to the enemy ship. The captain points at [[Gage]] and an undead spew sprays from his finger, but a gust of wind blows it aside. He shouts something unintelligible in undercommon. Some of the enemy crew start boarding the ship with grappling hooks. [[Temerity]] delivers a rousing speech to the crew and bolsters their fortitude. He provides bardic inspiration to [[Lavender]]. [[Anaphel]] deftly picks up [[Lavender]] and flies her across to the Frozen Wraith. She lands beside the mainmast. She uses channel divinity. One of the zombie crew disintegrates, three others turn and the two who have boarded the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]] turn. [[Anaphel]] mutters and huge icy shards grow out of the ground, forming spirit guardians. Three of the zombies take damage from the guardians, one of which is impaled by an ice spike and explodes. [[Runcible]] is dispelled, [[Theren]] takes damage and is restrained by ice. All of the crew, including [[Captain Voss|Voss]], take damage. [[Mary-Ann]] hides behind the gunwale and sneak attacks the captain, hitting him with her shortbow. It thuds into his shoulder but it doesn’t hurt him as much as it should. The zombie behind [[Anaphel]] swings a rusted cutlass at [[Anaphel]] but misses. The zombie by [[Lavender]] swings a rusted cutlass at her. [[Temerity]] uses cutting words but flubs it. The cutlass hurts [[Lavender]]. The turned zombies all run to the edges of the boat. The zombie at the stern throws a grapple and boards the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]] and swings at [[Theren]], but misses. The two zombies to the bow fire cannon at the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]], smashing into its deck. [[Theren]] uses [Raulothim’s psychic lance](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#raulothim's%20psychic%20lance_ftd) on the enemy captain. It pierces his mind and incapacitates him. [[Theren]] uses cloak of flies and the zombie beside him grins as the poison damage does not affect it. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] bellows out orders to the crew and inspires [[Temerity]], [[Mary-Ann]], [[Gage]], [[Lavender]] and [[Anaphel]]. [[Lavender]] uses the boost to attack the zombie in front of her, who explodes. 

[[Gage]] stealths and shoots his bow at the captain. An arrow thuds into him but doesn’t do as much damage as it should. The crew man their stations. The navigators still cannot plot a course; the sails continue to be prepped well; the repairers do not manage to repair the ship. [[Lavender]] moves past the zombies manning the cannon and approaches the captain, using reckless attack with divine smite. The captain remains incapacitated. [[Temerity]] uses guiding bolt. [[Chikatika]]’s collar glows, imbuing [[Temerity]] with energy and he smacks the captain with radiant damage. The captain explodes and covers [[Lavender]] in putrid liquid. The other zombies disanimate and fall to the fall. [[Temerity]] collapses to the floor. The Frozen Wraith begins to sink. [[Anaphel]] swoops up [[Lavender]] and flies her back over to the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]]. 

[[Lavender]] smiles and congratulates [[Anaphel]], who reciprocates. 

[[Lavender]] looks over at [[Captain Voss|Voss]] and wonders if they can continue. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] thinks that it can make it to [[Adalens]]. They can patch it up there. [[Temerity]] smiles and asks if they would like a lift now. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] is unnerved and gives up. She tells [[Temerity]] to go ahead if he feels like it.

[[Temerity]] runs to the front of the ship, grabs the massive towing rope and dives into the water, polymorphing into an orca whale. [[Theren]] throws up as the boat rocks from the huge wave sweeping the ship. [[Temerity]] begins to swim towards [[Adalens]] for the next hour.

[[Mary-Ann]] sleeps in her hammock. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] approaches [[Lavender]]. She knew there would be trouble, but not as much as this. She thanks them for their help.

[[Lavender]] asks for more information. The Frozen Wraith were pirates who terrorised this area. They were supposedly brought down by a kraken, or a ship that took them on, or perhaps something else that was not of the sea. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] believes now that they must be cautious now, or they will suffer the same fate. [[Lavender]] side-eyes [[Gage]]. She asks [[Captain Voss|Voss]] if [[Temerity]] knows where he is going. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] gets [[Gage]] to direct [[Temerity]] telepathically. [[Theren]] resummons [[Runcible]].

[[Mary-Ann]] awakens and heads to the kitchen to meddle. 

[[Gage]] asks the crew to train him in nautical manoeuvres. The crew feels offended by [[Gage]]’s attitude – [[Gage]] feels that sailing a boat cannot be that difficult. They put him through his paces until he is utterly exhausted. 

As [[Temerity]]’s polymorph ends, he releases the rope, leaps out of the water, changes back and splats onto the deck. 

[[Lavender]] is below deck considering the phrases Master of Ash and [[Winter's Core]]. She wonders if it is the [[Ring of Orris|ring]]. 

The sky suddenly darkens. The wind picks up. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] shouts. A magical storm approaches. It is very unnatural. Ash is scented on the wind. 

Lightning strikes the crew, causing severe injuries throughout. [[Mary-Ann]] tries to see what is going on in the cloud, but can see nothing. She tries to repair the ship, but cannot. The crew tries to secure the deck, but fail. They try to navigate through the storm and find a safe route. [[Gage]] assists with the navigation successfully. [[Temerity]] tries to inspire the crew. He delivers a rousing speech. [[Temerity]] pulls on the [[Cloak of Billowing|strange cloak]] he acquired to shield him from the rain. Magic swirls around him. His cloak glows brightly. They stare in anticipation. He shakes it out. The cloak billows behind [[Temerity]], regardless of the wind. He has activated his Cloak of Billowing. A huge wave shakes the side of the ship. [[Gage]], [[Mary-Ann]], [[Temerity]] and [[Theren]] are knocked down. [[Captain Voss|Voss]] tries to navigate through the storm and succeeds. [[Lavender]] struggles through the wind and manages to secure the cargo. [[Theren]] uses magic to shield the ship from the storm. 

Another wave smashes the ship, knocking everyone except [[Mary-Ann]] down. [[Temerity]] and [[Gage]] somehow manage to avoid being thrown overboard. [[Theren]] collapses unconscious to the deck. [[Mary-Ann]] tries to repair the ship again and this time she succeeds. 

The sails fill with wind and the storm is weathered. The dark clouds continue to rage behind them, seeming almost angry that they have escaped.

[[Temerity]] stabilises [[Theren]]. 

The ship limps away across the water, still in one piece.
