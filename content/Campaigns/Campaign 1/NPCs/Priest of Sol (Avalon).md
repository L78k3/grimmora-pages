# Priest of Sol (Avalon)

> [!info] Status: Alive (Coerced)
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 28]]
> **Location:** [[Temple of Sol]] in [[Avalon]]

The **Priest of Sol** is the head of the temple in [[Avalon]].

## Interactions

When [[Gage]] and [[Theren]] were caught snooping in the temple, the priest confronted them. While initially defensive, he eventually admitted (after Gage mentioned the necromancers) that he was terrified and being forced to work with them.

He allied himself with the party's goal to take down the necromancers and dismissed his monks to speak with the party privately.

## Abilities

* **Healing/Blessing:** He identified that [[Theren]]'s headaches were caused by a "fey enchantment." While he stated only a fey could break it, he was able to offer a blessing that mitigated the effects.
