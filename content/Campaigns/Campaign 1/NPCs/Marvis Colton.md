**Marvis Colton** is an older human merchant and the proprietor of [[Silken and Spindle]], a high-quality textile shop located in the shopping district of [[Adalens]].

## Biography & Personality
Marvis is an older human who runs a highly successful textile business. His shop, [[Silken and Spindle]], offers high-quality clothing and significantly outperforms his main competitor, [[Vex Silks]]. He is protective of his staff, demonstrating great concern and generosity when his sharp-minded clerk, [[Petra Greenfield]], was abducted. When thrilled, he is willing to pay extravagantly, rewarding the party with 500 gold pieces for Petra's safe return—far exceeding his initially posted bounty.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 43]] (The Missing Clerk):** Marvis posted a job notice regarding his kidnapped clerk, [[Petra Greenfield]]. When the party arrived at his shop, he shared his suspicions about his rival, [[Theron Vex]], and directed them to investigate the warehouse district. After the party rescued Petra and returned her to the shop, Marvis was thrilled and paid them a massive 500 gold reward.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Petra Greenfield]]:** His young, mathematically sharp clerk. Marvis values her highly and immediately sought out mercenaries for her rescue.
* **[[Theron Vex]]:** His primary business rival and the proprietor of [[Vex Silks]]. Marvis's financial success drove Vex to orchestrate the kidnapping of Marvis's clerk.
* **[[Lavender]]:** The party member who initially presented the job notice and led the successful rescue operation.

## Notable Equipment
* **Business Ledgers:** Marvis maintains detailed ledgers that document his robust sales and interactions with figures like [[Captain Holloway]].
