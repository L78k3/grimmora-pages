**Captain Holloway** is a smuggler ship captain operating in Adalens, known for her discretion and willingness to undertake illicit contracts.

## Biography & Personality
Holloway is a pragmatic and discreet ship captain involved in smuggling operations within the metropolis of Adalens. She commands a crew of thugs and is frequently recorded in local ledgers, indicating her active role in the city's black market and under-the-table logistics. 

She is highly mercenary in her ethics; while she is willing to kidnap civilians for a business rival, she is equally willing to abandon a contract—and the hostage—if a better or less lethal financial offer presents itself.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 43]] (The Mill Confrontation):** Hired by [[Vex Silks]] to abduct the clerk [[Petra Greenfield]], she held the girl captive in the derelict [[Old Mill]]. When confronted by the party, Holloway aimed her crossbow but ultimately accepted a counter-bribe of 22 gold from [[Mary-Ann]] to abandon the contract and leave the premises with her injured thug.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Mary-Ann]]:** Mary-Ann knew of Holloway's reputation for discretion and successfully haggled with her to resolve the hostage situation peacefully.
* **[[Theron Vex]]:** Holloway was hired by his company, [[Vex Silks]], to sabotage a business rival.
* **[[Marvis Colton]]:** Holloway frequently appears as the final daily entry in the ledgers of Colton's business, [[Silken and Spindle]], though her exact dealings with him prior to the kidnapping are unclear.

## Notable Equipment
* **Crossbow:** She wielded this weapon when confronting the party as she descended the stairs in the derelict mill.
