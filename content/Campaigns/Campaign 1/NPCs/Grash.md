# Grash

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 33]]
> **Role:** Sailor on the [[Paddle Steamer]]

**Grash** is a hulking orc sailor working on the [[Paddle Steamer]].

## Interactions

When [[Temerity]] polymorphed into a "massive demonic rabbit," Grash was ordered by [[Kevin (Sailor)|Kevin]] to carry the creature to the brig. Grash petted the giant rabbit affectionately during transport.

When he later returned to the brig and found the rabbit missing (because Temerity dropped the form), Grash was described as "devastated".
