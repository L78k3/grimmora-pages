# Guido

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Species:** Rat
> **Companion:** [[Temerity]]
> **Traits:** Alcoholic, gluttonous, grumpy, surprisingly useful scout

## Overview

Guido is a rat who travels in [[Temerity]]'s satchel. Originally a city rat from [[Durvish City]], he was recruited by the party with bribes of cheese and alcohol. He is defined by his vices - he is almost always drunk, hungover, or eating - but he frequently serves as a crucial scout for the party.

## Personality & Traits

* **Vice-Ridden:** Guido loves beer, cheese, and sleep. He is frequently described as being "drunk as a lord" or nursing a hangover. He once stole a bottle of mead (which contained the [[Ring of Orris]]) and was furious when it was taken away.
* **Grumpy but Loyal:** He often complains and bites [[Temerity]], but he consistently follows orders (usually for a bribe).
* **Scout:** Despite his state, he is an effective spy. He can speak to other rats to gather information and is small enough to infiltrate locked rooms.

## History

### Recruitment
The party met Guido in [[Durvish City]] (Entry 1). Temerity bribed him with ale and cheese to track a mysterious black-cloaked man. Later, they used him as live bait to catch a hawk, staking him out in a field after drugging him with absinthe-spiked ale. He survived the hawk attack thanks to a healing potion and slept through the whole ordeal.

### Key Moments
* **The [[Ring of Orris]]:** In [[Midward]], Guido stole a bottle of mead during a bar brawl. The bottle turned out to contain [[Lavender]]'s brother's ring. Guido was launched out of the bottle by [[Runcible]]'s fire breath, landing with singed whiskers.
* **[[The Tavern]]:** In [[Mightrest]], he helped Temerity fight the [[Gnome Cook]] by biting him (though he complained the gnome tasted bad). He was later thrown out of the tavern by [[Gorg]] after a prank.
* **[[Dragon's Nest]]:** He suffered severe food poisoning after eating [[Temerity]]'s undercooked [[Dragon Meat]], belching constantly for a day.
* **[[Arcadia]]:** He was sent out to gather information in the necromancer-occupied city while the party planned their infiltration.

## Relationships

* **[[Temerity]]:** His "employer." Their relationship is transactional, based on a steady supply of cheese and alcohol.
* **[[Korako]]:** His travel companion. They are often left together in safe spots while the party fights.
* **[[Runcible]]:** The pseudodragon has carried Guido to safety on occasion, though he also once blasted him out of a bottle with fire.
