**Azuul** is the professional and observant proprietor of [[The Snoring Jackal]] tavern in [[Midward]].

## Biography & Personality
Azuul is a tall dragonborn known for her striking mirror-blue scales. She runs a raucous and popular establishment, managing both the lodgings and the bar with a steady hand. She is helpful to travellers and keeps a close eye on the goings-on in her inn, often acting as a primary point of contact for the party during their stay in Midward.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 18]] (Arrival in Midward):** Azuul greeted the party upon their arrival and arranged for five rooms to accommodate the group for their stay.
* **[[Entry 23]] (The Drow Liaison):** As the party prepared to head to the casino, Azuul informed Lavender that Temerity had retreated upstairs with a drow companion, facilitating the party's efforts to gather their group.
* **[[Entry 24]] (Missing Bard):** When Temerity failed to answer his door for a job involving missing livestock, Gage consulted Azuul to check on the bard’s status.
* **[[Entry 25]] (Morning Departure):** Azuul served Gage mead as he waited for the rest of the party to wake. She later processed Temerity's key return, noting that he had left the tavern exceptionally early to head toward the southern gate.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** Azuul maintains a respectful, professional relationship with Lavender, who handles the party's logistics and payments.
* **[[Gage]]:** Azuul has been helpful to Gage, providing him with drinks and information regarding the movements of his companions.

## Notable Equipment
* **Innkeeper's Ledger and Keys:** Used to manage the many guests of The Snoring Jackal.
