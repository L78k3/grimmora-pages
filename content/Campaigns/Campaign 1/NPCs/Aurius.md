# Aurius

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Snow Fox
> 
> **Affiliation:** [[Temerity]]
> 
> **Location:** [[Tidecaller (Ship)]]
> 
> **Status:** Alive

**Aurius** is a hyperactive snow fox cub who serves as a loyal companion to [[Temerity]] and a scout for the party, possessing a unique sensitivity to the voice of the goddess [[Auriel]].

## Biography & Personality
Aurius is a small, energetic snow fox cub with a tragic past, having witnessed the death of his parent at the claws of [[Ice Wolves]] in the [[Alpine Forest]]. Despite this trauma, he maintains a relentlessly enthusiastic and innocent demeanour, famously declaring [[Temerity]] his "best friend" almost immediately upon meeting him. He is highly excitable, prone to zooming around camp, and has a naive understanding of the world, once referring to [[Korako]] as "[[Entry 06#^0a7005|cock-a-doodle-doo]]".

While often serving as a source of levity, Aurius has demonstrated a surprising spiritual connection. During the party's ascent of the mountains near [[Mightrest]], he acted as a conduit for the goddess [[Auriel]], translating her whispers which guided the party through her trials. He is deeply attached to Temerity's "menagerie," often seen playing with [[Korako]] and [[Chikatika]], or hiding behind Temerity's legs when danger threatens.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 06]] (Meeting the Party):** Encountered the party in the [[Alpine Forest]]. He enthusiastically befriended [[Temerity]] and guided the group to the [[Ice Wolves]]' cave, where he witnessed his parent's carcass during the ensuing battle.
* **[[Entry 07]] (Gifts & Guiding):** Received a custom [[Cloak Adorned with Snowflakes]] made by Temerity. He successfully guided the party through the mountains towards the [[Dragon's Nest]].
* **[[Entry 08]] (The Goddess's Voice):** At [[Auriel's Wall]], Aurius heard the voice of "the soft lady" ([[Auriel]]), relaying key words like "adventure," "journey," and "trust" to the party, which were essential for solving the mural puzzle and entering [[The Trial Chamber]].
* **[[Entry 09]] (The Trial):** During the trial within the [[The Gladiator's Amphitheatre|amphitheatre]] and subsequent caverns, Aurius continued to hear the Lady's voice, relaying that she called [[Lavender]] "friend" and urged the party to "trust," leading to their escape on the invisible griffin [[Mathilda]].
* **[[Entry 15]] (The Rage Rope):** Assisted Temerity in a chaotic attempt to tie up [[Lavender]] with a rope to calm her rage outside [[Arcadia]].
* **[[Entry 18]] (Waiting Room):** Hid quaking behind Temerity's legs during a confrontation with guards in the waiting room of the [[Mayor's Mansion (Arcadia)]].
* **[[Entry 20]] (Meeting Anaphel):** Was presented to the aasimar [[Anaphel]].
* **[[Entry 22]] (Camp Life):** Seen running playfully around [[Chikatika]] back at camp after the party retreated from the [[Mausoleum (Alpine Forest)]].
* **[[Entry 33]] (The Ship):** While travelling on the [[Paddle Steamer]], he played with [[Korako]] and was briefly separated from Temerity when the bard was thrown in the brig.
* **[[Entry 34]] (The Investigation):** Sniffed out a suspicious door on the ship, sneezing and getting knocked back by the necrotic energy within, which helped the party locate the cursed courier [[Loraleia]]. He later led the group to the cargo hold.
* **[[Entry 35]] (The Lost Bag):** Successfully identified the scent of [[Mary-Ann Mayfield]]'s missing bag on the ship, leading to the party's introduction to her.
* **[[Entry 38]] (The Rescue):** Alerted [[Temerity]] that their friends were in trouble (communicating via [[Runcible]]), prompting the rescue mission at the [[Onasea Sea Cave]]. He rode atop Temerity (in giant crocodile form) during the escape.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Temerity]]:** His owner and "best friend." Aurius is fiercely loyal to the bard, often perching on his shoulder or head.
* **[[Korako]]:** A close companion within the menagerie. Aurius often plays with the rabbit, though he has been known to be confused by him.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Aurius seems fond of the paladin, having snuggled her for comfort after the battle with the ice wolves.
* **[[Auriel]]:** Aurius shares a mysterious connection to the goddess, able to hear and interpret her divine whispers when others cannot.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[Cloak Adorned with Snowflakes]]:** A small, custom-made fur cloak crafted by [[Temerity]] using off-cuts from the [[The Clothier|Clothier]] in [[Mightrest]].
