**Petra Greenfield** is a 17-year-old half-elf clerk who works at [[Silken and Spindle]] in [[Adalens]] and was the victim of a targeted kidnapping by a rival merchant.

## Biography & Personality
Petra is described as having very dark hair and very pale skin. She lives near the outskirts of the market square and had no known enemies prior to her abduction. 

Despite her young age, she is exceptionally sharp with numbers, making her an invaluable asset to her employer, [[Marvis Colton]]. She demonstrates remarkable composure; immediately after being rescued from being tied to a chair in a derelict mill, she composed herself and offered the party her professional assistance with decoding messages or understanding figures and contracts.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 43]] (The Kidnapped Clerk):** Abducted from the warehouse district by [[Captain Holloway]] on the orders of rival merchant [[Theron Vex]]. The party tracked her to the derelict [[Old Mill]], negotiated her release with her captors, and safely returned her to [[Marvis Colton]], earning a 500 gold reward.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Marvis Colton]]:** Her employer at [[Silken and Spindle]]. He values her highly, immediately posting a large reward for her safe and unharmed return.
* **[[Theron Vex]]:** A rival merchant from [[Vex Silks]] who hired thugs to kidnap her in an attempt to sabotage Colton's business.
* **[[Captain Holloway]]:** The smuggler ship captain hired to carry out the kidnapping.

## Notable Equipment
* **Satchel:** A bag she had on her person when abducted, containing 200 gold and plenty of material. It remained completely intact throughout her ordeal.
