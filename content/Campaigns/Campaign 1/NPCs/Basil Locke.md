Basil is the current head of House Locke. Whilst it is unusual his younger brother, Sorrel, leads the family's formidable forces, Basil found his strength in politics and the noble courts. This is not to say he isn't vicious in a fight, but as the saying goes, the brightest lights cast the longest shadows, and it appears the shadows are where Basil is most comfortable.
