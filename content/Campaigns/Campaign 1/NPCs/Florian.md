# Florian

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Fey
> **Role:** Master of the Scrying Pool
> **Affiliation:** [[Court of Summer and Winter]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport]] ([[fey realm]])
> **Status:** Alive

**Florian** is a fey mage residing in the magical city of [[Bridgeport]]. He serves the [[Court of Summer and Winter]] as the keeper and operator of the court's [[scrying pool]], an ornate divination tool made of white marble featuring a sculpture of an opening eye.

## Biography & Personality
Florian is a specialized arcane practitioner who facilitates powerful divination rituals for the eladrin royals. He is professional and guiding in his duties, overseeing the immersion of subjects into the pool and managing the resulting visions. Beyond his divinatory skills, he possesses practical utility magic, such as the ability to instantly dry individuals after they exit the water. He assisted the party during their mission to locate [[Lady Bromelia]], managing separate scrying attempts for [[Lavender]] and [[Temerity]].

## Key Relationships
* **[[Summer]] and [[Winter]]:** His royal patrons within the Court.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Provided her with access to a vision of her family through her grandmother's eyes.
* **[[Temerity]]:** Assisted him in a scrying attempt directed toward [[Orris]].

## Notable Equipment
* **[[scrying pool]]:** A white marble basin with an eye sculpture used for viewing distant places and people.
