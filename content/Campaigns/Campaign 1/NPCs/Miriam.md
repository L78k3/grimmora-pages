**Miriam** is an older half-elf healer alchemist stationed within the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]] of the [[Mages' Guild]] in [[Adalens]].

## Biography & Personality
Miriam is an older half-elf lady known for her smiling demeanour and her constant companion, a squirrel named [[Brian]]. She is described by her animal companion as the "only nice one" among the researchers in her lab. As a dedicated healer alchemist, she spends her time stirring, tasting, and meticulously modifying medicinal mixtures, recording the results of her experiments in a notebook.

She possesses significant knowledge regarding magical afflictions and advised [[Theren]] that fey enchantments are incredibly tricky to undo. Though she works in a building that was once a temple to a forgotten god, she claims to have no knowledge of its ancient history. In combat, she acts as a support specialist, utilizing healing tinctures and magical spells to revive fallen allies.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 44]] (The Sanctum Skirmish):** Met the party in the alchemists' lab and provided [[Theren]] with professional advice regarding his name curse. During the sudden betrayal by her colleague [[Felix]], she remained allied with [[Thomas]] and the party. She provided vital combat support by throwing healing tinctures at [[Temerity]], healing [[Lavender]] and [[Runcible]], and eventually using [Cure Wounds](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#cure%20wounds_phb) to revive an unconscious Theren. Following the battle, she complied with Lavender's request to wake Felix for interrogation.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Brian]]:** Her squirrel companion whom she rescued after he fell from a tree; she continues to treat his injuries and monitor his health.
* **[[Thomas]]:** A dwarven gemstone expert and her trusted ally within the [[Mages' Guild]] lab.
* **[[Felix]]:** A former laboratory colleague who was revealed to be a servant of the [[Master of Ash]].
* **[[Theren]]:** Provided him with medical consultation regarding his fey enchantment and revived him during the Sanctum battle.

## Notable Equipment
* **Alchemist's Notebook:** Used to record the results of healing experiments.
* **Healing Tinctures:** Potent alchemical fluids she can throw to mend wounds from a distance.
