# Brian

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** Highwayman
> **Affiliation:** [[Bandits]]
> **Location:** [[Bandit Clearing (Avalon Woods)]]
> **Status:** Deceased

**Brian** was a member of a small bandit raiding party operating in the woods north of [[Avalon]]. He is primarily remembered for his brief encounter with the party and a vital sighting he made prior to his death.

## Biography & Personality
Brian was a highwayman encountered by the party on the road to [[Frostpeak Pass]]. He served in a four-man crew led by an experienced [[bandit captain]] that targeted merchants traveling to the capital for seasonal celebrations. During an ambush in a forest clearing, Brian was the first of the bandits to be killed, decapitated by a single, rage-fueled blow from [[Lavender]]'s claymore.

Though his role was short-lived, Brian provided the party with one of their most critical early leads. During a subsequent interrogation, his surviving comrade [[Dave]] revealed that Brian had witnessed "something strange" earlier that day - a large, squawking, flying creature heading north. This sighting confirmed the flight path of the [[griffin]] carrying the kidnapped [[Lady Bromelia]], allowing the party to track her into the northern mountains.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** His executioner, who ended his life during the ambush in the forest clearing.
* **[[Dave]]:** A fellow bandit and survivor who relayed Brian's sighting of the griffin to the party.
* **[[Bandit Captain]]:** Brian's commanding officer during the highway robbery attempt.

## Notable Equipment
* **Bandit Gear:** Like his comrades, Brian was equipped with standard highwayman weaponry, including a scimitar and a light crossbow.
