**Bandits** are common highway robbers encountered by the party while traveling toward the capital.

## Biography & Personality
These individuals are typical highwaymen who survive by attacking travellers and merchants to steal their goods. They lack a formal organization beyond small raiding parties and are motivated purely by material gain.

## Campaign History
* **Road to Avalon Encounter:** The party encountered a specific group of four bandits. At the time, this group was actively targeting merchants who were traveling to **[[Avalon]]** for the seasonal celebrations.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Bandit Captain]]:** This specific group of four was led by a more experienced captain who directed their ambushes.

## Notable Equipment
* **Stolen Goods:** The group typically carries various wares and gold pilfered from merchants.
