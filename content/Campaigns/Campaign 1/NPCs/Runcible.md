# Runcible

> **Status:** Alive

**Runcible** is the emerald green pseudodragon familiar bonded to [[Theren]]. He serves as the party's primary aerial scout, a telepathic conduit for information, and a frequent - if sometimes reluctant - []()enforcer of party discipline.

## Biography & Personality
Runcible is a clever and shimmering green creature with a sting-tipped tail and a sharp mind. He shares a deep telepathic bond with his master, Theren, allowing the warlock to see through his eyes and communicate across distances. While highly competent in his duties, Runcible often displays a distinct personality: he is prone to hissing when annoyed, enjoys perching on Theren’s shoulder, and has a mischievous streak that usually manifests at the expense of [[Temerity]].

Though he is technically a summoned spirit, Runcible shows a high level of autonomy and protective instinct, particularly towards Theren. He is often tasked with carrying [[Guido]] the rat during reconnaissance missions, forming an effective, if unlikely, partnership with the party's smaller animal companions.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Haunted Inn):** Runcible was first summoned to scout a pitch-black room in Durvish City. His telepathic reports allowed the party to identify and defeat two spectres lurking within the unnatural darkness.
* **[[Entry 02]] (The Griffin Chase):** Following the kidnapping of [[Lady Bromelia]], Runcible scouted the temple from above and later tracked a griffin across Avalon until he lost its trail in the northern mountains.
* **[[Entry 03]] (Barge Infiltration):** Sent to investigate a barge filled with magical treasures, Runcible successfully reported the crew count but clumsily crashed into some boxes, forcing a hasty escape through a window.
* **[[Entry 04]] (The Vampire Mansion):** Acted as a distraction during the confrontation with the [[Vampire Lord]] by scratching the surface of a grand piano. 
* **[[Entry 06]] (The Ice Wolf Hunt):** Assisted in the battle against ice wolves by retrieving a healing potion from Theren's pack to revive his unconscious master.
* **[[Entry 09]] (The Floating Platform):** During [[Auriel]]'s trial, Runcible flew to a suspended platform and discovered a crawling Lavender, acting as a crucial link between the separated party members.
* **[[Entry 15]] (Arcadia Infiltration):** Provided a vital distraction at the city gates of Arcadia by grabbing a guard's spear and dragging him off the ground, allowing Lavender and Temerity to sneak inside.
* **[[Entry 16]] (Waking the Bard):** Instructed by Theren to scratch Temerity awake, Runcible ended up in a scuffle with the bard. He also used a burst of flame to launch Guido out of a mead bottle.
* **[[Entry 25]] (The Great Departure):** After Temerity abandoned the party in Midward, Runcible located him near the south gate and dive-bombed him, biting him as a reprimand before returning to Theren.
* **[[Entry 32]] (The Warehouse Battle):** During the fight with the possessed mason, Runcible was temporarily dispelled by a powerful psychic attack from a necromantic idol.
* **[[Entry 34]] (The Sickroom Recon):** On the paddle steamer, Runcible carried Guido to a porthole to peek inside the room of a cursed courier, revealing the location of the Imperial courier [[Loraleia]].

## Key Relationships
* **[[Theren]]:** His master and primary bond. Runcible is fiercely loyal to Theren and acts as his literal eyes and ears in dangerous situations.
* **[[Temerity]]:** A strained relationship. Theren frequently sends Runcible to bite, scratch, or harass the bard to force him into action.
* **[[Guido]]:** A frequent passenger. Runcible often carries the rat to high places for scouting, though he seems occasionally annoyed by the rat's antics.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Runcible appears to respect Lavender, once curling up to guard her while she slept in the aftermath of a battle.

## Notable Equipment
* **Venomous Stinger:** Runcible's primary natural weapon, capable of delivering a potent poison to enemies.
* **Telepathic Link:** A permanent bond that allows Theren to perceive through Runcible's senses and communicate silently.
