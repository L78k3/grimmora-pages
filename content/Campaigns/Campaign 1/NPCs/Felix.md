# Felix

**Felix** is a gaunt human flame alchemist working within the Arch-mage's Sanctum who was revealed to be a fanatical servant of the Master of Ash.

## Biography & Personality
Felix is a rake-thin man in his 50s with greyish streaks in his hair and a notably gaunt appearance. He wears dark robes stained with ash that appears out of place despite his professional work with fire. His movements are described as slow, precise, and measured. He is highly secretive and hostile toward [[Orris Locke]], whom he considers a "charlatan". As a devoted follower of the [[Master of Ash]], he believes his master sees everything and strives to maintain total secrecy.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 44]] (The Sanctum Skirmish):** Discovered working in the alchemists' lab within the [[Mages' Guild]] headquarters. After being confronted by [[Lavender]] regarding her brother and the [[Encoded Letters]], he attacked the party using a [Wall of Fire](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#wall%20of%20fire_phb) and alchemical explosives. He was eventually subdued and interrogated, revealing that [[Orris Locke]] had met with the [[Arch-mage]] three days prior.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Master of Ash]]:** His master and deity; he serves as a fanatical agent for the cult's interests in the capital.
* **[[Orris Locke]]:** Despises him, publicly branding the nobleman's brother a "charlatan".
* **[[Miriam]] and [[Thomas]]:** Research colleagues in the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum|Sanctum]] who were caught in his alchemical blast and appeared shocked by his sudden betrayal.

## Notable Equipment
* **Alchemical Vials:** Including flaming potions, caustic flasks, and alchemical bombs used during the Sanctum battle.
* **Shield Focus:** Utilized to generate a protective barrier against projectiles.
* **Flame Reagents:** Various liquids used to modify the properties and colors of open flames.
