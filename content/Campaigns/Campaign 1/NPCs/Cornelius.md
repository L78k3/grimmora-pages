**Cornelius** is a hairy man and rock-breaker encountered by the party in the city bathhouse of [[Adalens]].

## Biography & Personality
Very little is currently known about Cornelius's background or personality. He is described physically as a "hairy man" whose known occupation or hobby involves "breaking rocks." He appears to frequent the local bathhouse in the metropolis of [[Adalens]], specifically taking his leisure in the freezing temperatures of the ice pool.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 42]] (The Bathhouse Encounter):** While the party was securing lodgings and cleaning up after their harrowing sea voyage, [[Gage]] entered the bathhouse's ice pool and briefly encountered Cornelius.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Gage]]:** Briefly met Cornelius while the two were sharing the ice pool at the [[Adalens]] bathhouse.
