# Arrogant Mage

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** Tower Mage / Cartographer
> **Affiliation:** [[Mage's Tower (Durvish City)]]
> **Location:** [[Durvish City]]
> **Status:** Alive

The **Arrogant Mage** is a member of the magical order residing in the [[Mage's Tower (Durvish City)|Mage's Tower]] within [[Durvish City]]. He is defined by his dismissive attitude toward outsiders and his preoccupation with academic pursuits.

## Biography & Personality
The mage is characterized by a significant lack of hospitality and an inflated sense of self-importance. He prioritizes his personal research - specifically his work in cartography - over the urgent needs of visitors, even those on official city guard business. He appears to find interactions with non-magical individuals or lower-ranking petitioners to be a nuisance, and he is quick to delegate such tasks to others whom he considers more suitable for "minor" disruptions.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Tower Visit):** First encountered when the party visited the [[Mage's Tower (Durvish City)|Mage's Tower]] seeking assistance for a guard who had been subjected to a [[confoundment spell]]. True to his name, he was found preoccupied with his cartography and was far too arrogant to bother assisting the group personally. Instead, he dismissively suggested that the party speak to [[Ismay]] to resolve their problem.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Ismay]]:** A colleague at the tower whom he treats as a point of contact for tasks he deems beneath his attention.
* **[[Lavender]], [[Temerity]], & [[Theren]]:** The adventurers he briefly encountered and promptly ignored.

## Notable Equipment
* **Cartography Tools:** Utilized for his ongoing academic research within the tower.
