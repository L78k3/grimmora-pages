**Orion** is a disciplined monk of [[The Order of the Ancient Fable]] who joined the party in [[Mightrest]] and served as a stalwart companion until his disappearance during the approach to [[Arcadia]].

## Biography & Personality
Orion is an "incongruous" figure whose body is almost entirely covered in cloth wraps scribed with various languages and story phrases. As a member of his Order, he adheres to the mantra that "those who forget history are doomed to repeat it." He is a focused, serious, and often quiet individual who frequently spends his downtime meditating.

Though his introduction to the party was violent - culminating in him punching [[Temerity]] in the stomach - Orion eventually developed a deep bond with the group. He is a lethal combatant who utilizes "fists of fury" and stunning energy to incapacitate his foes, often acting as a front-line defender alongside [[Lavender]].

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (The Incongruous Monk):** Discovered in [[Mightrest]] investigating the disappearance of his fellow monks. After a brief confrontation with the party, he was captured and eventually agreed to join them when their goals aligned.
* **[[Entry 04]] (The Vampire's Fall):** Assisted in the assault on the [[Vampire Mansion]], using a flurry of blows to pummel the [[Vampire Lord]] and protect a wounded Temerity.
* **[[Entry 05]] (The Ice Troll Battle):** Aided the group in entering [[Auriel's Church]] and was mortally wounded during the confrontation with an ice troll.
* **[[Entry 06]] (The Wolf Hunt):** Participated in a grueling battle against massive [[Ice Wolves]] in the [[Alpine Forest]], nearly dying to their cold breath.
* **[[Entry 07]] (The Dragon's Nest):** Fought the juvenile [[Ice Dragon]], providing critical aid to a fallen [[Theren]] despite the creature's dangerous breath attacks.
* **[[Entry 08]] (The Gladiator's Trial):** Challenged the [[The Gladiator Wight|Gladiator Wight]] in the amphitheatre, suffering significant necrotic damage before Lavender’s disappearance.
* **[[Entry 09]] (Guidance in the Dark):** After Lavender's disappearance, Orion took charge in the absolute darkness of the trial tunnels, allowing Temerity to hold his tail to find the way forward.
* **[[Entry 10]] (The Spider Cave):** Assisted in clearing a chamber of massive spiders and witnessed the summoning of [[Chikatika]].
* **[[Entry 11]] (The Displaced):** Captured by "wandering souls" and brought to the hidden camp of [[The Displaced]], where he eventually escaped into the shadows to write to his order.
* **[[Entry 14]] (The Departure):** Rejoined the party and committed to traveling with them to [[Arcadia]]. He claimed he was "taking charge" because the group needed leadership, but he has not been seen since the group entered the city limits.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** Though originally captured by her, Orion grew to respect her as a formidable protector and the group's natural leader.
* **[[Temerity]]:** A tumultuous relationship that began with a physical altercation but softened after Orion guided the bard through the darkness of the Gladiator's Trial.
* **[[Theren]]:** Shared a quiet mutual respect with the warlock, who often mediated between Orion and the rest of the party.
* **[[Corvus]]:** Orion's brother and fellow monk, currently searching for missing members of their Order in the south-west.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[Blue Communication Stone]]:** A magical stone on a cord that allows for communication over distance; he provided it to Lavender while investigating Auriel's Church.
* **Scribed Wraps:** His Order's vestments, containing ancient languages and history that he is sworn to protect.
