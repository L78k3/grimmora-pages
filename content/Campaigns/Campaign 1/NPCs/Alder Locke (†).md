Alder, the previous head of [[House Locke]], died in battle approximately forty years ago, when both of his sons were young. His two brothers also perished with him, leaving a handful of nieces and nephews behind. His wife, Countess [[Myrtle Locke]], took over the house after his death, who passed it to their eldest son upon his marriage to the Lady Rosemary.
