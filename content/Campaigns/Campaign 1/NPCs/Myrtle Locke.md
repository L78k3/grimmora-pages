**Lady Myrtle Locke** is the 75-year-old matriarch of [[House Locke]] and the formidable grandmother of [[Lavender]].

## Biography & Personality
Lady Myrtle is the widow of the late Count [[Alder Locke (†)]]. Following her husband's death in battle approximately forty years ago, she took over leadership of the house until she eventually passed it to their eldest son, Count [[Basil Locke]], upon his marriage. She is the mother of both Basil and Lord Marshal [[Sorrel Locke]], and has two sisters and one brother. 

Myrtle is a stern, demanding, and highly perceptive noblewoman who does not tolerate weakness or a lack of ambition. She is an authoritative figure whose word carries immense weight within the family. It was her sharp intuition that recognized the martial potential in her unruly granddaughter, [[Lavender]]; Myrtle was the one who explicitly suggested that Lavender be taught the ways of battle and considered to lead the family's military forces. She remains a commanding presence, revered by Lavender as the ultimate "protector".

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (The Scrying Pool):** During a vision in the [[scrying pool]] in the [[fey realm]], [[Lavender]] saw the world through Myrtle's eyes. Myrtle was furiously berating her son, [[Basil Locke|Count Basil Locke]], demanding to know why Lavender was gone and criticizing his carelessness and lack of ambition.
* **[[Entry 28]] (Grandmother's Blessing):** [[Lavender]] returned to the [[House Locke]] manor in [[Avalon]] and met with Myrtle in her bright, airy study. Lavender revealed [[Orris Locke]]'s involvement with the [[necromancers]]. Myrtle instructed her granddaughter to be a soldier, do what she must, and try to pull Orris back from his path, formally giving Lavender her blessing.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** Her granddaughter. Myrtle recognized Lavender's martial potential early on. Lavender deeply respects her grandmother, drawing upon her teachings and memory for strength in moments of crisis.
* **[[Basil Locke]]:** Her eldest son and the current Count. Myrtle is highly critical of him, berating his lack of ambition and causing the stoic man to shrink under her reprimands.
* **[[Sorrel Locke]]:** Her younger son and the current Lord Marshal of the house's forces.
* **[[Orris Locke]]:** Her grandson. She authorized Lavender to do whatever is necessary regarding his involvement in necromancy.
* **[[Alder Locke (†)]]:** Her late husband, the former Count and Lord Marshal, who died in battle.
