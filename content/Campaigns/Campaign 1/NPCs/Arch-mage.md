The **Arch-mage** is the elected governing official of the metropolis of [[Adalens]] and the leader of the [[Mages' Guild]].

## Biography & Personality
The Arch-mage serves as the highest executive authority in the guild-run city of [[Adalens]]. Governing from a headquarters established in a repurposed massive ancient temple, the Arch-mage oversees the activities of the city's six primary guilds: Mages, Merchants, Artisans, Scholars, Navigators, and Hospitallers. The position is an elected one rather than a hereditary title. While the Arch-mage oversees the broad stability of the metropolis, they occupy the highly restricted [[Arch-mage's Sanctum|Inner Sanctum]] on the sixth floor of the guild academy, where the city's most significant and secretive research is conducted.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 44]] (The Secret Meeting):** Following the interrogation of the alchemist [[Felix]], it was revealed that the Arch-mage held a private meeting with [[Orris Locke]] in the Inner Sanctum three days prior to the party's infiltration of the lab.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Orris Locke]]:** A member of the [[Mages' Guild]] who secured a private audience with the Arch-mage shortly before being forced into hiding.
* **[[Felix]]:** A fire alchemist and cultist of the [[Master of Ash]] who operated within the Arch-mage's secure Sanctum.
* **[[Elara Mythin]]:** A mistress of one of the five ruling families who operates under the Arch-mage's guild-governed hierarchy.
