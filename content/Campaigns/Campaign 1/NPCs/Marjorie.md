# Marjorie

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 14]]
> **Location:** [[Displaced Camp]] (Housekeeper's Quarters)

**Marjorie** is an elderly gnome woman who serves as a housekeeper within the [[Displaced Camp]]. Despite her unassuming role, she holds significant wisdom and a surprising connection to [[Temerity]]'s past.

## Overview

We encountered Marjorie while staying in the Displaced Camp. She appears as a tiny, elderly gnome—standing only as high as [[Temerity]]'s crotch—often seen dragging a huge broom behind her. She lives in a small, cosy house within the cavern, decorated with window boxes and bunting.

She is strict but motherly, refusing to clean [[Temerity]]'s room until he put on underwear and forcing him to help scrub the walls.

## Key Interactions

### Connection to Temerity
While cleaning [[Temerity]]'s room, the two hummed a song together that Temerity had known since childhood. Marjorie revealed that she knew his mother well from their time as friends in a "big city many years ago".

* **True Name:** She addressed Temerity by his birth name, **Morthos**.
* **The Photograph:** She invited Temerity to her home for tea and gifted him a photo from a gilded album. The image depicted Temerity's parents, Marjorie, and her husband.

### Counsel to Theren
Marjorie interrupted [[Theren]] while he was reading to clean his room. They discussed magic and friendship. When Theren expressed conflict about joining [[The Displaced]] and leaving his party, she offered him sage advice:
* She explained that friends playing pranks is often a way of showing care.
* She reminded him that "no decision is fixed in stone," helping him realize he could change his mind about leaving his companions.
