# Kevin

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 30]]
> **Role:** City Guard (South Gate)

**Kevin** is a new and inexperienced member of the [[Avalon]] City Guard.

## Interactions

We encountered him at the guard post by the south gate. He was nervous, stuttering, and admitted it was only his second day on the job.

He confessed to [[Lavender]] that when he arrived for his shift, the previous guards were asleep/unconscious. Fearing headquarters would find out, he moved them to [[The Slippery Ostrich]] to stage a drunken stupor. [[Theren]] sensed that Kevin was honest, if somewhat incompetent.

[[Gage]] offered him life advice, suggesting he ignore his mother and find what he is good at.
