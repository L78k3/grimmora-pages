# Water Genasi Harbourmaster

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Water Genasi
> **Role:** Head of Port Authority
> **Affiliation:** [[Avalon]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport]] (Avalon)
> **Status:** Alive

The **Harbourmaster of Avalon** is a water genasi official who oversees the port authority within the [[Bridgeport]] district. She is known for being strictly professional and occasionally argumentative, though she possesses a significant and exploitable weakness for small, adorable creatures.

## Biography & Personality
The Harbourmaster is characterized by a personality that is frequently described as obtuse, argumentative, and resistant to becoming involved in city matters. She manages the docks with a focus on administrative records and fees, often demanding proper paperwork from those seeking her assistance. Despite her abrasive and uncooperative exterior, she is highly susceptible to the "cute effect" of animals, particularly rabbits. She is also easily offended by perceived slights or nicknames, having taken great umbrage at being referred to as a "watery lady."

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (The Smuggling Barge):** First encountered when the party attempted to report a barge carrying stolen magical treasures. She initially refused to help, but after [[Temerity]] presented [[Korako]], she agreed to "look the other way" during the party's investigation in exchange for time playing with the rabbit.
* **[[Entry 30]] (The Missing Horses):** Faced an unauthorized intrusion into her office by [[Lavender]] and [[Gage]] during their investigation into the disappearance of [[Soliana]]'s caravan horses. Although unhappy with the interruption, she identified a seal fragment found at [[The Slippery Ostrich]] as belonging to the shipping company that owns the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].
* **[[Entry 32]] (Securing Passage):** Met with [[Temerity]] once more following the raid on the warehouse. Under the influence of a [charm person](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#charm%20person_phb) spell cast by the bard, she reluctantly secured boat passage for the party to [[Durvish City]] for a fee of two silver pieces per person, expressing a fervent hope to never see the group again.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Korako]]:** The primary reason for her occasional cooperation; she finds the rabbit irresistible.
* **[[Temerity]]:** A source of great irritation. While she has secured passage for him, she remains insulted by his past descriptions of her and was only cooperative during their final meeting due to magical influence.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Respectful but wary of the paladin’s direct and forceful approach to "city guard business."

## Notable Equipment
* **Official Records:** Maintains the logs and registry for all vessels and shipping companies operating in the [[Avalon]] docks.
