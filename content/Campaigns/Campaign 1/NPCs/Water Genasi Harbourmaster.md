# Water Genasi Harbourmaster

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 3]], [[Entry 30]]
> **Location:** [[Harbourmaster's Office (Avalon)]]

The **Harbourmaster** of [[Avalon]] is a female water genasi who runs the port authority.

## Personality

She is generally described as obtuse, argumentative, and unwilling to get involved in city matters. However, she has a significant soft spot for rabbits.

## Interactions

### The Smuggling Barge (Day 3)
When the party first investigated the smuggling barge, she was unhelpful until [[Temerity]] presented [[Korako]]. She found the rabbit so adorable she agreed to look the other way while the party investigated the barge, in exchange for time playing with him. She took offense to Temerity calling her a "watery lady".

### The Missing Horses (Day 22)
[[Lavender]] and [[Gage]] forced their way into her office to investigate the seal found at [[The Slippery Ostrich]]. She was unhappy with the interruption but agreed to check the records. She identified the seal as belonging to the shipping company that owns the warehouse.
