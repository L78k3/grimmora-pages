# Barge Captain (Avalon)

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** River Trader / Smuggler
> **Affiliation:** Independent
> **Location:** [[Docks (Avalon)]]
> **Status:** Alive

The **Barge Captain** is a river trader encountered at the docks of [[Avalon]] who was unwittingly - or perhaps knowingly - transporting stolen magical goods.

## Biography & Personality
The captain is a professional mariner who operates a large river barge with a crew of at least six people. He appears to be motivated by profit, as he was identified as the individual who purchased stolen supplies from the [[bandit captain]]'s group for 60 gold pieces. While his direct level of involvement with the criminal element is unclear, he operates with enough discretion to offload sensitive cargo quickly upon reaching the city.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (The Smuggling Lead):** The party tracked his [[River Barge (Avalon)|barge]] from the forest clearing to the [[Avalon]] quayside after learning from the bandit [[Dave]] that the merchant's magical items had been sold to him.
* **[[Entry 03]] (The Docks):** By the time the party arrived at the docks to confront him, the captain and his [[First Mate (Avalon)|first mate]] had already disembarked. Most of the magical cargo had been offloaded, leaving only a skeleton crew and minimal goods remaining in the hold.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Bandits]]:** Purchased stolen magical inventory from the [[bandit captain]] for a significant sum of gold.
* **[[First Mate (Avalon)]]:** His primary subordinate who accompanied him into the city.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[River Barge (Avalon)]]:** A large transport vessel capable of carrying significant amounts of cargo.
