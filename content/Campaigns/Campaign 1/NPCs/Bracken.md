# Bracken

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Fey
> **Role:** Teleportation Specialist
> **Affiliation:** [[Court of Summer and Winter]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport]] ([[fey realm]])
> **Status:** Alive

**Bracken** is a fey creature residing in the [[fey realm]] who serves as a primary transporter for the court.

## Biography & Personality
Bracken acts as a magical facilitator for the rulers of the [[fey realm]], specialized in moving individuals across vast distances between locations in their world. While his personal temperament is not extensively documented, he operates with high efficiency under the direct authority of [[Summer]] and [[Winter]]. He is typically found within the [[Temple (Fey Realm)|Temple]] or the court's major hubs.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (Dimensional Transit):** First encountered by the party in the fey equivalent of [[Avalon]]. Following the party's meeting with the eladrin royals, Bracken utilized his magic to teleport the group to a dimly lit area of the [[Weakened Forest (Fey Realm)|forest]] to investigate a dimensional weakness.
* **[[Entry 03]] (Return Journey):** Waited for the party at a [[teleportation rune|transportation portal]] within the forest clearing. After the party defeated a [[dimensional sucker]], Bracken facilitated their return to the [[Temple (Fey Realm)|Temple]] for rest and recuperation.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Summer]] and [[Winter]]:** His royal superiors; he executes their orders regarding the movement of guests and subjects.
* **[[Lavender]], [[Temerity]], & [[Theren]]:** The adventurers he transported during their initial mission for the fey court.

## Notable Equipment
* **Teleportation Magic:** Possesses the innate ability or arcane knowledge to operate [[teleportation rune|teleportation runes]] and shift groups across the realm.
