# Bridgeport Mason

> [!danger] Status: Hostile
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 31]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]

The **Bridgeport Mason** is a well-built human male found working in the secret workshop within the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].

## Condition

When observed by [[Gage]], the mason appeared pale and was identified as being **possessed**.

## Abilities

He appears to be creating or modifying statues to act as vessels for necromantic energy. Upon being discovered by the party, the statues around him (including a large golem) began to glow with necromantic energy and animate to attack.
