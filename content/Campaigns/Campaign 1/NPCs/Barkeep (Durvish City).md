# Barkeep (Durvish City)

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** Innkeeper
> **Affiliation:** Independent
> **Location:** [[Durvish City]]
> **Status:** Alive

The **Barkeep of Durvish City** is the proprietor of a modest inn located in the southern district of the city. He is a pragmatic, business-oriented individual whose establishment served as the accidental meeting point for the original party members.

## Biography & Personality
The barkeep is primarily motivated by his livelihood, exhibiting a miserly streak that earned him the reputation of a "cheapskate" from [[Temerity]]. He is often overwhelmed by the bustle of city festivities and appears ill-equipped to handle supernatural disturbances, relying heavily on the direction of his more decisive wife. While he maintains a gruff, transactional exterior, he is easily intimidated by physical threats or high-status individuals.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Initial Altercation):** Engaged in a heated argument with [[Temerity]] over performance rights and room board. Attempted to price-gouge [[Lavender]] by charging 5 gold pieces for a single night's stay.
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Spectre Contract):** Following an intervention by his wife, he negotiated a deal with the party to provide three nights of free room and board in exchange for clearing an upstairs room of [[spectres]].
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Interrogation):** Faced a violent interrogation at knifepoint by [[Theren]]. He revealed that he had rented the haunted room to a man in a black, hooded cloak who paid with a large pouch of gold, asking no questions in return.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Barkeep's Wife (Durvish City)]]:** His spouse and business partner, who holds significant influence over his decisions and manages the more difficult aspects of the inn's operation.
* **[[Temerity]]:** A source of constant irritation; the two clashed immediately over the bard's attempt to stay for free.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Nearly incensed the paladin with his attempts to overcharge her, only backing down when physically seized by the collar.
* **[[Theren]]:** The target of the warlock's suspicion and physical intimidation during the hunt for the "Man in Black."

## Notable Equipment
* **Innkeeper's Ledger:** A record of the inn's guests, notably missing the names of certain high-paying, secretive clients.
