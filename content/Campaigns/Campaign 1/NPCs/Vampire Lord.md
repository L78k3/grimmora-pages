**The Vampire Lord** was the primary antagonist of the [[Mightrest]] arc, responsible for a "magical plague" that ravaged the village and used its citizens as a living larder.

## Biography & Personality
The Vampire Lord was a terrifying undead entity with gaunt, alien features and a skeletal face. He possessed long black hair styled in an intricate braid and massive fangs. Operating from the "Big House" overlooking Mightrest, he spoke in a chilling whisper and maintained an aura of eerie silence within his domain.

He was highly suspicious and protective of his residence, particularly a massive pipe organ located on the foyer landing. He displayed extreme cruelty, overseeing a "larder" of occupied hospital beds where victims were kept not to be turned, but to be slowly drained of their life force to feed his hunger.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 04]] (The Plague Investigation):** The party arrives in Mightrest to find a lethargic, pale population. [[Theren]] identifies the symptoms as vampirism. The group devises a plan to infiltrate the Vampire Mansion by having [[Lavender]] pose as a victim.
* **[[Entry 04]] (The Foyer Confrontation):** The Vampire Lord meets the party in the entrance foyer, attempting to separate Lavender from her companions. When the party resists, he leads them to the "larder" before returning to the foyer. A fierce battle erupts when Theren launches a fire bolt at the vampire's back.
* **[[Entry 04]] (The Battle at the Big House):** During the combat, the Vampire Lord bites [[Temerity]] and later clamps onto Lavender's jugular. Lavender retaliates by drinking a [potion of fire breath] and belching flames directly into his face. 
* **[[Entry 04]] (Death):** Sensing his end, the Vampire Lord launches a desperate final attack on Lavender. Supported by Temerity, Lavender plunges her sword through his chest, destroying him and causing his remaining spawn to disintegrate as sunlight began to filter into the hall.
* **[[Entry 05]] (Aftermath):** The party awakens in the mansion and realizes the "infirmary" is now a morgue. They loot the mansion for the victims' sentimental belongings and gold to return to the village.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** His slayer. He viewed her as a prime source of food, but she ultimately ended his reign by skewering his heart.
* **[[Temerity]]:** The Lord was distracted by the bard’s attempts to play his organ and later bit him during the melee.
* **[[Orion]]:** The monk used a flurry of fists to force the vampire off of Temerity during the battle.
* **[[Vampiric Spawn]]:** Numerous pale figures served as his guards and assistants in the feeding operation.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[Pipe Organ (Vampire Mansion)]]:** A massive, elevated instrument that the Lord was oddly protective of.
* **[[Larder (Vampire Mansion)]]:** A room filled with hospital beds used to store living victims for feeding.
* **[[Brass Door Knocker (Tiger)]]:** An ornate knocker shaped like a tiger that echoes throughout the mansion when struck.
