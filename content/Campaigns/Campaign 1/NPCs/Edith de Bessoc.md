Twin half-elves from House Bessoc, a vassal of House Locke. Alfred and Edith have served the House since their early years and Sorrel considers them his closest friends. Edith is Sorrel's second in command, whilst Alfred, a follower of Pellor, has retired from active service to train their healers. 

The twins were sent as wards from the middle of their childhood and were raised to be warriors.
