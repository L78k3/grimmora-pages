# Thumpy

> [!info] Status: Active
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 26]]
> **Location:** [[Durendal]] / [[Fairy Garden (Durendal)]]

**Thumpy** is a mysterious [[harengon]] encountered in the [[Durendal]] market.

## Interactions

We first saw him near the staff seller's stall. He looked at us, shook his head, and promptly transformed into an **almiraj** (a rabbit with a unicorn horn) before hopping away.

Later, unbeknownst to us at the time, he followed the party through the daisy portal into the [[Fairy Garden (Durendal)]].
