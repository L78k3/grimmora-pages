**The Alchemist** is a specialist merchant based in [[Mightrest]] who produces and sells high-grade healing draughts and other concoctions.

## Biography & Personality
The Alchemist operates a shop in the freezing village of [[Mightrest]]. He is a practical merchant who values rare ingredients over simple gold, often bartering his expensive potions for labour or hard-to-find botanical specimens. 

During his encounter with the party, he requested that they retrieve rare flowers from a clearing in the [[Alpine Forest]] - a location known to be guarded by fey spirits. He demonstrated a specific professional interest in these flora, offering to trade two potent healing potions in exchange for a collection of special yellow and purple flowers.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 07]] (The Floral Bargain):** The party visits the Alchemist in Mightrest to secure supplies for their journey. He offers them a deal: retrieve rare fey-guarded flowers from the forest in exchange for his services. The party successfully retrieves the yellow and purple flowers and completes the trade for two healing potions.

## Key Relationships
* **[[The Party]]:** The Alchemist utilized the group as temporary couriers to bypass the dangerous fey protections of the Alpine Forest.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[Healing Potions]]:** He maintains a stock of expensive medicinal draughts for trade.
* **Rare Flora:** He possesses (or seeks) exotic yellow and purple flowers with unique alchemical properties.
