# Captain Voss

> [!info] Status: Unknown (Not yet met)
> **Role:** Captain of the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]]
> **Location:** [[Onasea]] (Presumed)

**Captain Voss** is a ship captain and associate of [[Corbin]], known for running passengers that "the authorities don't ask about".

## Role

She is part of Corbin's information and smuggling network. Corbin vouched for her reliability, stating she is the contact who will take [[Lavender]] and the party to safety in [[Adalens]].

## The Arrangement

According to a vision experienced by [[Lavender]], [[Orris]] arranged for Voss to transport Lavender to safety if she was in danger. The signal to initiate this passage is the **[[Grey Pearl]]**; showing it to Voss is meant to secure passage with "no questions asked".
