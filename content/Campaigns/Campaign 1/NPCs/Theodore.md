**Theodore** is a scholar reportedly located in the city of [[Adalens]].

## Overview

We learned of Theodore from a severe-looking bookseller in [[Midward]]. When [[Theren]] inquired about books regarding powerful beings, the bookseller informed him that Theodore's life's work is the study of the extra-planar.

He represents a potential lead for Theren in his search for answers regarding his patron.
