# Barkeep's Wife (Durvish City)

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** Innkeeper / Decision Maker
> **Affiliation:** Independent
> **Location:** [[Durvish City]]
> **Status:** Alive

The **Barkeep's Wife** is the co-proprietor of a modest inn in the southern district of [[Durvish City]]. She is a decisive woman who acts as the practical force behind the inn's operations, particularly during crises.

## Biography & Personality
The barkeep's wife is characterized by her sharp temperament and practical nature, famously "shrieking" at her husband for his lack of foresight in allowing dangerous guests to stay at their establishment. Unlike her husband, who is primarily motivated by greed, she is focused on the safety and reputation of the inn. She demonstrated a keen ability to recognize professional talent, being the one to initiate negotiations with the party to clear their haunted room. While she manages the day-to-day chaos of the inn during festivities, she remains observant of her clientele.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Necromancer Revelation):** Interrupted a heated argument between her husband and the party to reveal that [[necromancers]] had been allowed to stay in the inn.
* **[[Entry 01]] (Hiring the Party):** Pressured her husband into offering the party free room and board in exchange for clearing an upstairs room of [[spectres]].
* **[[Entry 01]] (Client Information):** Provided the party with critical intelligence regarding a mysterious black-cloaked client, noting that he had left the inn around ten o'clock that morning.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Barkeep (Durvish City)]]:** Her husband and business partner, whom she frequently corrects and directs.
* **[[Lavender]], [[Temerity]], & [[Theren]]:** The adventurers she hired to resolve the supernatural disturbance in her establishment.
