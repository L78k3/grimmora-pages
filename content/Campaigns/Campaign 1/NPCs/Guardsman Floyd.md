# Guardsman Floyd

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Drow
> **Role:** City Guard
> **Affiliation:** [[Avalon City Guard]]
> **Location:** [[North City Gate]] (Avalon)
> **Status:** Alive

**Guardsman Floyd** is a drow member of the City Guard in [[Avalon]], currently stationed at the city's northern perimeter.

## Biography & Personality
Guardsman Floyd is a professional and observant sentry who maintains a stern exterior when dealing with travelers. Like many guards in the capital, he is initially unimpressed by the florid speech and eccentric behavior of adventurers. However, he possesses a soft spot for small creatures, particularly rabbits, which can be leveraged to gain his cooperation. Despite his professional demeanor, he maintains a healthy level of suspicion toward chaotic individuals.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (Gate Duty):** Stationed at the [[North City Gate]] of [[Avalon]] with a fellow drow guard. While his companion was driven to a "fit of delirium" and ran off into the forest after seeing [[Korako]], Floyd remained at his post. He initially resisted [[Temerity]]'s effusive manner but became cooperative once [[Lavender]] revealed her noble status.
* **[[Entry 03]] (Providing Intel):** Informed the party that stolen magical goods were being unloaded on the quayside. He also confirmed that he had seen a [[Tiefling Merchant (Avalon Road)|tiefling merchant]] pass through the gate heading toward the harbor. Before the party left, he pointedly warned [[Temerity]] that he would be "keeping an eye" on him.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** Respectful toward her due to her noble standing, which influenced his decision to provide assistance to the party.
* **[[Temerity]]:** Highly suspicious of the bard's antics and made a point to warn him of continued surveillance.
* **[[Korako]]:** Found the rabbit impressive enough to allow it as a sign of the party's trustworthiness, though he was less susceptible to its "cute effect" than his fellow guard.

## Notable Equipment
* **City Guard Armament:** Equipped with standard-issue gear for an [[Avalon]] sentry.
