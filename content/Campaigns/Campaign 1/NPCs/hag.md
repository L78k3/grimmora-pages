# Hag

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Fey
> 
> **Affiliation:** Independent
> 
> **Location:** [[fey realm]]
> 
> **Status:** Alive

**The Hag** is a dangerous [[fey]] creature who attempted to lure the party into a trap using a disembodied voice and [[will o' the wisps]] in the forest south of [[Avalon]]. 

## Biography & Personality
The Hag is a deceptive and predatory creature. She attempted to lure [[Theren]] deeper into the forest by asking to "play a game." When her ruse failed, she aggressively pursued him out of the treeline. She is later heard in [[Entry 03]] giggling from the woods, suggesting she enjoys stalking or mocking those who have escaped her.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 01]] (The Forest Trap):** Attempted to lure [[Theren]] into a [[fairy circle]] using a disembodied voice and [will o' the wisps](https://2014.5e.tools/bestiary.html#will-o'-wisp_mm). When he retreated, she burst from the trees in pursuit but was incapacitated by a [hideous laughter](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#tasha's%20hideous%20laughter_phb) spell, allowing the party to flee to [[Avalon]].
* **[[Entry 03]] (The Mocking Laughter):** Her distinct giggle was heard trailing the party through the forest between [[Avalon]] and [[Frostpeak Pass]], though she did not reveal herself. 

## Key Relationships
* **[[Theren]]:** She specifically attempted to lure and trap him when he entered the woods seeking to bargain with the fey.
