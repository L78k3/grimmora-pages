# Anaphel

> [!info] Status: Active Party Member
> **Origin:** Offspring of [[Auriel]]
> **Role:** Healer / Divine Caster

**Anaphel** is a celestial being (Aasimar) sent to the mortal realm 300 years ago as a missionary by the goddess [[Auriel]]. They currently travel with the party.

## Campaign Notes
### Loss of Connection
In **[[Entry 39]]**, while traveling to [[Onasea]], Anaphel confessed to [[Lavender]] that they no longer felt their connection to [[Auriel]], stating they "don't have something any more."

### Theological Research
Anaphel began researching the possibility of an adversary to Auriel—specifically relating to fire or heat. Working with [[Theren]] and [[Lavender]], they discussed deities like [[Sol]], [[Pellor]], and [[Sargonnas]]. Anaphel hopes to find answers in [[Adalens]].

### Origin & Nature
Anaphel revealed their true nature to [[Lavender]] in [[Pinechill]]. They appear as a tall figure with glowing white skin, white hair, and deep blue eyes. They are homesick for the immortal planes but cannot return until their duty is fulfilled.

### Relationship with Lavender
Anaphel and [[Lavender]] share a complex bond as servants of Auriel.
* **The Conflict:** Anaphel was horrified to learn Lavender possessed the **[[Ring of Orris]]**, believing that their mother, **[[Auriel]]**, is trapped inside it. This caused significant tension, though they eventually reconciled.
* **The Promise:** Lavender has sworn to free Auriel, leaving Anaphel looking "scared and vulnerable".

### Combat Role
Anaphel acts as the primary support and heavy caster, frequently using *healing word*, *greater restoration*, *toll the dead*, and *ice storm* to protect the party.
