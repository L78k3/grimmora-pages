**Oliver** is a halfling caravan driver employed by **[[Soliana]]** to transport goods for **[[Zadhir]]**'s operations.

## Biography & Personality
Oliver is a halfling distinguished by his ginger hair and freckles. He appears to have a penchant for luxury and excess, which occasionally interferes with his professional duties. 

During the caravan's stopover in **[[Durendal]]**, Oliver failed to report for duty, causing significant concern for his supervisor, Soliana. The party eventually tracked him to an expensive, well-furnished room at **[[The Sweet and Sour Skunk]]** inn. He was found in bed with a tiefling companion, having overindulged in alcohol the previous night. Once roused by **[[Lavender]]**, he hurriedly dressed and returned to the caravan just in time for its departure to **[[Avalon]]**. He was last seen arriving in Avalon shortly before the caravan's horses and manager went missing.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Soliana]]:** His direct supervisor; she was notably concerned by his absence and personally requested the party find him.
* **[[Lavender]]:** One of the individuals who forcibly retrieved him from his room in Durendal to ensure the caravan remained on schedule.
