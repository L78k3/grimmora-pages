# Oliver

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 27]]
> **Role:** Caravan Driver

**Oliver** is a halfling caravan driver working for [[Soliana]] (and by extension [[Zadhir]]).

## Appearance
He is a halfling with ginger hair and freckles.

## Events
During the stopover in [[Durendal]], Oliver went missing, delaying the caravan's departure. The party tracked him to a luxury room in [[The Sweet and Sour Skunk]], where he was found hungover in bed with a tiefling. He was hurriedly retrieved to ensure the caravan could leave for [[Avalon]].
