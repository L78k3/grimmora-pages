# Merisa

> [!warning] Status: Missing
> **Last Seen:** [[Durendal]] Market
> **Current Location:** [[Fairy Garden (Durendal)]]

**Merisa** is a young human girl from [[Durendal]] who went missing in the market.

## Disappearance

Her mother was found crying outside a tavern, clutching [[Merisa]]'s cloak. [[Runcible]] tracked her scent to a goblin vendor's stall. Under [[Lavender]]'s interrogation, the vendor admitted that Merisa "could not pay" for an item, so he took a "favour" instead - taking her to the "fairy garden".

We have entered the [[Fairy Garden (Durendal)]] to retrieve her.
