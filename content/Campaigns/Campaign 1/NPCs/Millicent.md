# Millicent

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Fey
> **Role:** Fey Court Mage
> **Affiliation:** [[Court of Summer and Winter]]
> **Location:** [[fey realm]]
> **Status:** Alive

**Millicent** is a fey mage serving the rulers of the [[fey realm]]. She is a skilled practitioner of transportation magic, responsible for facilitating travel between the mortal and fey planes.

## Biography & Personality
Millicent is a loyal agent of the fey court who operates under the authority of [[Summer]] and [[Winter]]. She appears to be an efficient and precise spellcaster, capable of performing complex trans-dimensional teleportation rituals. While her direct interactions with the party were brief, her magic was responsible for returning them to the mortal plane after their diplomatic mission to [[Bridgeport]].

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (Mortal Return):** Following the party's audience with the eladrin royals, Millicent was tasked with their return journey. She utilized her magic to teleport the group from the fey realm to a location just south of [[Mightrest]]. The party was shocked to find that despite their extensive time in the fey realm, Millicent's magic delivered them to the mortal plane only ten minutes after their initial disappearance.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Summer]] and [[Winter]]:** Her royal superiors who command her services.
* **[[Lavender]], [[Temerity]], & [[Theren]]:** The adventurers she returned to the mortal world.

## Notable Equipment
* **Teleportation Magic:** Possesses advanced knowledge of dimensional travel, including the ability to manipulate the time dilation between the fey and mortal realms.
