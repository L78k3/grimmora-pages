# Halsen

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Location:** [[Silvanus' Beard]] (near [[Avalon]])

**Halsen** is the powerful druid leader of the enclave at [[Silvanus' Beard]]. He is a close ally of [[Gage]] and the father of the three druid children (the raven, jackalope, and weasel).

## Campaign Notes

### Magical Analysis
The party brought several dark artefacts to Halsen for identification:
* **[[Cloudy Gem (Sol)]]:** He identified powerful binding magic on this gem and provided a **[[Rune Box]]** to shield its signature.
* **[[Corrupted Censer]]:** He determined this object had been perverted to cause sickness rather than cure it.
* **[[Chronometer Pocket Watch]]:** He identified this as an object of chronomancy.

### Advice
He advised the party to seek out a reclusive druid in **[[Crystalmine Village]]** who may have the power to deal with the binding magic of the Cloudy Gem.
