# Halsen

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 27]]
> **Location:** [[Silvanus' Beard]] (near [[Avalon]])

**Halsen** is a huge, muscular elf druid who leads the community at [[Silvanus' Beard]].

## Appearance & Personality
Halsen is described as a "huge, buff elf" who dominates the area with his presence. Despite his imposing physical stature, he is welcoming and knowledgeable, eager to discuss sustainable farming, fishing, and animal husbandry.

## Interactions
[[Gage]] sought him out while in [[Avalon]]. Halsen welcomed Gage, introduced him to other druids, and offered accommodation for the evening.

When Gage warned him about the necromantic devastation in [[Arcadia]], Halsen noted that while he had heard of necromantic tainting in druidic areas, they had not yet been targeted specifically in Avalon.

## Gifts
Impressed by Gage's commitment to the "word of green," Halsen gifted him two magical items:
* **Gage's Cloak:** A dark green cloak with embroidered leaves that boosts nature and survival abilities.
* **Moss-Covered Stone:** A magical stone said to give assistance when needed.
