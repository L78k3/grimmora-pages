**Halsen** is the powerful druid leader of the enclave at [[Silvanus' Beard]] and a close ally to [[Gage]]. He is also the father of the three druid children who frequent the city of [[Avalon]].

## Biography & Personality
Halsen is a "huge, buff elf" who oversees the druidic community at Silvanus' Beard. He is deeply knowledgeable about nature and sustainability, teaching methods of sustainable fishing and animal husbandry. He possesses a wise and protective demeanour, often seen playing with the children of the enclave despite his imposing presence.

He is empathetic toward those suffering from magical ailments but maintains a strict policy of non-interference regarding the politics of other religions, such as the [[Temple of Sol]]. He is highly respected within his grove and acts as a mentor figure to those seeking to protect the natural world.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 27]] (The Grove Leader):** Gage seeks out the leader of Silvanus' Beard to learn about the area. Halsen welcomes him, introduces him to the enclave's sustainable practices, and discusses reports of necromantic tainting in the region.
* **[[Entry 28]] (The Druid Children):** Although Halsen is not present in the park, his three children (in the forms of a raven, jackalope, and weasel) interact with Theren and Gage. Gage later mentions they will meet at Silvanus' Beard.
* **[[Entry 29]] (Analysing the Dark Artefact):** The party brings a dark, cloudy gem taken from the Temple of Sol to Halsen. He identifies powerful binding magic on the stone and provides a **[[Rune Box]]** to shield its magical signature. He advises the party to seek a reclusive druid in **[[Crystalmine Village]]** for further help.
* **[[Entry 32]] (The Bridgeport Artefacts):** The party returns to Halsen with artefacts found in the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]. He identifies that a **[[Corrupted Censer]]** has been perverted to cause sickness and notes that a pocket watch found by the party is an object of chronomancy.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Gage]]:** A close ally. Halsen gifted Gage his **[[Gage's Cloak of the Forest|Cloak of the Forest]]** and a mossy sending stone to aid his travels and survival.
* **[[Temerity]]:** Halsen is aware of the bard through his children, noting that they are fond of him.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Halsen assisted her by identifying the binding magic in her brother's ring and the Cloudy Gem.
* **The Druid Children:** Halsen is the father of the three druid children (human, elf, and tiefling) who play in Avalon's parks.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[Rune Box]]:** A small box carved with runes used to conceal the aura of powerful magical items.
* **[[Gage's Cloak of the Forest]]:** A magical dark green cloak embroidered with leaves that boosts the wearer's nature and survival skills.
