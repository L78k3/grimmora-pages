# Alaric

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 28]]
> **Role:** Butler for [[House Locke]]

**Alaric** is the butler of the [[House Locke]] manor in [[Avalon]].

## Interactions

He greeted [[Lavender]] upon her return to her childhood home. He recognized her immediately and informed her of her family's current status (her sisters are courting, her grandmother was out, and her father was in his study).
