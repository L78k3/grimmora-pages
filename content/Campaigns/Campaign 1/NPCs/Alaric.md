**Alaric** is the butler of the [[House Locke]] manor in [[Avalon]].

## Biography & Personality
Alaric is a loyal and professional servant who manages the daily operations of the Locke family estate in [[Avalon]]. He is well-acquainted with the family's history and members, immediately recognizing [[Lavender]] upon her return to the manor after an eighteen-month absence. 

Maintaining a helpful and courteous demeanour, Alaric serves as an initial point of contact for the household. During Lavender's visit, he provided essential updates regarding the family, noting that her younger sisters were currently busy with courtship, her grandmother was attending a special dinner engagement, and her father was occupied within his study.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** Alaric recognized the returning noble immediately and provided her with updates on her family's current status.
* **[[House Locke]]:** He serves as the primary butler for the household, attending to the needs of the family patriarch and the grandmother.
