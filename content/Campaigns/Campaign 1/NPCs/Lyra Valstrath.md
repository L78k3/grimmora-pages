**Lyra Valstrath** is the High Priestess of the [[Master of Ash]] and a fanatical adversary seeking the [[Ring of Orris]] to destroy the goddess [[Auriel]].

## Biography & Personality
Lyra is a figure of intense heat and devotion, with dark red skin and eyes that glow like coals. Her cloak and hair often flutter as if they are tongues of flame. She possesses a magically amplified voice that sounds warm and harsh, likened to the sound of a forge. 

She is utterly devoted to the [[Master of Ash]], expressing that her worst form of punishment would be to fail her master. She appears ruthless and transactional, offering the crew of the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]] safety only in exchange for the ring, while threatening to take it from [[Lavender]]'s "charred corpse" if refused.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 41]] (Ambush at Sea):** Intercepted the *[[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]]* aboard a massive ship of dark wood thrice the size of the party's vessel. Claimed to be the successor to [[Magnus]]. 
* **[[Entry 41]] (Battle of the Tidecaller):** Teleported onto the *[[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]]* bow after a failed parley. She utilized ray of enfeeblement on [[Lavender]] and unleashed necrotic blasts and a fiery whip. She was eventually knocked unconscious by [[Gage]]'s bow.
* **[[Entry 41]] (Interrogation):** Bound and sat upon by [[Temerity]], she revealed the [[Master of Ash]] seeks to destroy his "competition" - the entity of balance inside the ring - to tip the cosmic scales in his favour. She recognized the name [[Sargonnas]] but refused to comment on [[Pellor]].
* **[[Entry 41]] (Arrival at Port Adal):** Upon reaching the docks, she was picked up and thrown overboard by [[Captain Voss]].

## Key Relationships
* **[[Master of Ash]]:** Her master and deity.
* **[[Magnus]]:** Her predecessor in service to the Master.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Target of her pursuit due to the [[Ring of Orris]].
* **[[Sargonnas]]:** A figure she recognized during interrogation.

## Notable Equipment
* **Large Staff:** A focus she raised into the sky to command the initial pursuit.
* **Ember-Patterned Robes:** Robes from which heat visibly emanates.
