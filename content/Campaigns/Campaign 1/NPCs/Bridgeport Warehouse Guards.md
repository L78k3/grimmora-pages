# Bridgeport Warehouse Guards (Martin & Roy)

> [!info] Status: Alive (Wet)
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 31]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport Warehouse]] in [[Avalon]]

**Martin** and **Roy** are two guards stationed at the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].

## Interactions

* **Martin:** The more active of the two during our encounter. He stopped [[Temerity]] at the door with his pike. Despite being charmed by Temerity's magic, he remained conflicted about disobeying his orders to keep the warehouse secure.
* **Roy:** The other guard on duty, whom Martin went to consult when he became confused by the conflicting orders and the charm spell.

## Our Experience

While Martin and Roy were distracted discussing whether to let the "inspectors" in, [[Gage]] and [[Lavender]] sneaked up behind them and shoved both guards into the river, allowing the party to pick the lock and enter the warehouse unobserved.
