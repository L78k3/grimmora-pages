# Dave

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** Highwayman / Survivor
> **Affiliation:** [[Bandits]]
> **Location:** [[Bandit Clearing (Avalon Woods)]]
> **Status:** Deceased

**Dave** was a member of the bandit raiding party encountered by the party in the woods north of [[Avalon]]. He was the sole survivor of the initial skirmish in the forest clearing.

## Biography & Personality
Dave was a highwayman serving under an experienced [[bandit captain]]. During the ambush against the party, he demonstrated notable competence by successfully de-summoning the pseudodragon [[Runcible]] before the rest of his crew was decimated by [[Lavender]]'s rage. Unlike his companions, Dave was initially spared when [[Temerity]] intervened to calm the paladin.

During his interrogation, Dave revealed a code of conduct, claiming that while he had hurt people and stolen from the wealthy, he had never killed anyone. He provided the party with the critical lead that his companion [[Brian]] had seen a "large, flying, squawking thing" heading north. Despite being offered a chance to return to Avalon and live an honest life, Dave refused to go back. To spare [[Lavender]] the moral burden of another kill, [[Temerity]] executed Dave with a light crossbow bolt to the base of the skull.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Temerity]]:** His interrogator and eventual executioner.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Spared his life initially, though he ultimately refused her offer of mercy.
* **[[Brian]]:** A fellow bandit whose sighting of the [[griffin]] Dave relayed to the party.

## Notable Equipment
* **Bandit Gear:** Equipped with standard highwayman equipment, including a scimitar, daggers, and a light crossbow.
