# Zadhir's Lieutenant

> [!info] Status: Active
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 25]]
> **Role:** Caravan Master for [[Zadhir]]

**Soliana** is a severe, high-ranking elf in [[Zadhir]]'s employ who oversaw the caravan departure from [[Adalens]].

## Appearance

She is described as a beautiful, pale elf with long black hair and high cheekbones.

## Personality

She appears professional, brusque, and authoritative. When [[Lavender]] introduced herself, the lieutenant wasted no time on pleasantries, simply demanding the party be ready in exactly 23 minutes.

## History

She organized the logistical departure of Zadhir's caravan from [[Adalens]] to [[Avalon]].
