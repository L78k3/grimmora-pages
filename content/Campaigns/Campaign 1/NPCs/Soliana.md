**Soliana** is an officious elf who serves as a high-level manager for [[Zadhir]], overseeing the logistics and security of his cross-continental caravans.

## Biography & Personality
Soliana is described as a beautiful and pale elf with long black hair and high cheekbones. She is characterized by an extremely brusque and authoritative demeanour, frequently seen checking clipboards and shouting orders to her drivers and security personnel. She is highly focused on efficiency, once demanding that the party be ready for departure in exactly twenty-three minutes.

Despite her professional coldness, she showed a degree of concern for her subordinates, such as when she actively sought out a missing driver in [[Durendal]]. Her current status is a point of significant concern for the party, as she vanished under mysterious circumstances during the caravan's stop in [[Avalon]].

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 25]] (The Caravan Assignment):** Soliana greets the party at the south-east gate of [[Midward]]. She acts as the primary authority for the convoy, introducing herself to [[Lavender]] and setting a strict timeline for their departure.
* **[[Entry 27]] (The Missing Driver):** Upon arriving in [[Durendal]], Soliana expresses distress over the absence of a halfling driver named [[Oliver]]. She coordinates with the party to ensure he is returned to his post before the caravan continues south to [[Avalon]].
* **[[Entry 29]] (Disappearance in Avalon):** When the party reports for duty at the caravan site in Avalon, they find the operation in total disarray. Soliana and all of the caravan's horses have gone missing, with no immediate sign of their destination or the party responsible.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Zadhir]]:** Her employer; Soliana operates with his authority and manages his high-value assets across the [[Avalonean Empire]].
* **[[Lavender]]:** Soliana maintained a professional relationship with Lavender, though she often treated the noble warrior with the same brusqueness she afforded common laborers.
* **[[Oliver]]:** A halfling driver whose absence in Durendal was Soliana's primary concern until he was recovered by the party.

## Notable Equipment
* **Management Clipboard:** An ever-present item used to check off unlabelled boxes and track her drivers.
