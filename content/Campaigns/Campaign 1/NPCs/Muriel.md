# Muriel

> [!info] Status: Alive
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 31]]
> **Location:** [[Silvanus' Beard]]

**Muriel** is an elderly woman who tends to the communal cooking pot at [[Silvanus' Beard]].

## Appearance

She is an aged woman who is blind, though she navigates her tasks with ease.

## Interactions

She is friendly and welcoming, offering stew to [[Gage]], [[Theren]], and [[Lavender]] upon their arrival.

She possesses subtle but potent magic; when [[Temerity]] was threatened by a vision or magical pull, she tapped his arm with her ladle, and the band around his neck tightened to "anchor" him in reality. When the party inquired about the missing horses from [[Zadhir]]'s caravan, she directed them to speak with [[Halsen]].
