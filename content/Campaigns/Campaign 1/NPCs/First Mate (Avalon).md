# First Mate (Avalon)

> [!info] Quick Info
> **Race:** Humanoid
> **Role:** First Mate
> **Affiliation:** Independent
> **Location:** [[Docks (Avalon)]]
> **Status:** Alive

The **First Mate** is a mariner serving aboard the [[River Barge (Avalon)]], working directly under the [[Barge Captain (Avalon)]].

## Biography & Personality
The First Mate is a professional sailor responsible for assisting in the operation of a large river transport vessel. He operates with high efficiency and discretion, ensuring that cargo is processed and offloaded promptly upon arrival at major ports. Like his captain, he appears to avoid unnecessary interactions with those investigating his vessel's contents.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 03]] (Arrival at Avalon):** The First Mate arrived at the [[Avalon]] docks aboard the river barge that had previously purchased stolen magical items from a group of [[bandits]].
* **[[Entry 03]] (Disembarkation):** By the time the party reached the quayside to investigate the vessel, the First Mate and the [[Barge Captain (Avalon)|captain]] had already disembarked and disappeared into the city, leaving only a skeleton crew behind to guard the nearly empty hold.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Barge Captain (Avalon)]]:** His direct superior and constant companion during their time at the docks.

## Notable Equipment
* **[[River Barge (Avalon)]]:** The vessel he helped navigate downriver to [[Avalon]].
