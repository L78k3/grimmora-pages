**Gregor Withwhile** is a guard of the city watch stationed at the main gate of the metropolis of [[Adalens]].

## Biography & Personality
Gregor is a dutiful member of the [[Adalens]] city watch, recognizable by his blue and gold uniform. Stationed at the city's wrought metal main gate, his primary responsibility appears to be processing incoming travellers and collecting the mandatory entry tax. Despite the party's initial scepticism regarding the fee, his demeanour and intent are honest, focused entirely on fulfilling his civic duties. 

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 42]] (The Entry Tax):** Stopped the party as they attempted to enter [[Adalens]] through the main gate, demanding a one-gold entry tax per person. 

## Key Relationships
* **[[Mary-Ann]]:** Unimpressed by the sudden tax, she boldly demanded a receipt and his full name for later verification. 
* **[[Theren]]:** Used his insight to intuitively confirm that Gregor was being entirely truthful about the official nature of the entry tax.
