# Temerity

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Tiefling
> 
> **Class:** Bard / Warlock
> 
> **Player:** Paul
> 
> **Location:** [[Onasea]]

**Temerity** acts as the face of the group, a born performer and chaotic force of nature. He travels with a menagerie of animals and is known for his musical prowess, illusions, and complicated romantic history.

## Biography & Personality
Temerity is flamboyant, irreverent, and deeply devoted to his animals. He often irritates his companions with his antics (such as polymorphing into demon rabbits) but acts as the party's social lubricant. He possesses healing abilities and hellish rebuke magic.

**Identity**
* **True Name:** [[Marjorie]] and [[Anaphel]] have both addressed him by his birth name, **Morthos**.

* **[[Entry 31]] (Warehouse Bluff):** Uses *charm person* on a warehouse guard to gain entry to the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].

During a magical storm, he dramatically activated his [Cloak of Billowing](https://2014.5e.tools/items.html#cloak%20of%20billowing_xge) to inspire the crew and stabilized an unconscious Theren.

## The Menagerie
* **[[Korako]]:** Remains his primary companion.
* **[[Chikatika]]:** A raccoon familiar summoned in the [[Spider Cave (The Frost)|Spider Cave]]. She actively assists in combat (e.g., helping cast Eldritch Blast).
* **[[Aurius]]:** A snow fox cub adopted near [[Mightrest]].
* **[[Guido]]:** The rat, who has developed a severe alcohol problem.

## Notable Equipment & Conditions
* **[[Temerity's Choker]]:** A vine-like accessory that manifested after a vision of a "gnome-tree" in a dark forest. The item appears to feed on his blood (thorns stabbed him during intimacy).
* **[[Black Cloak with Red Lining]]:** A magical cloak found in the [[Bandit Lake House]]. Temerity wears it despite thinking it makes him look odd.
* **[[Pulverised Gem Dust]]:** He carries a small box of gem dust looted from the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].
