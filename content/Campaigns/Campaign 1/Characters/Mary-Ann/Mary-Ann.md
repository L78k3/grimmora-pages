# Mary-Ann

> [!info] Character Overview
> **Race:** Halfling
> 
> **Class:** Rogue (Inquisitor)
> 
> **Background:** Noble / Widow
> 
> **Player:** Suzanne
> 
> **Location:** [[Onasea]]

**Mary-Ann** is an elderly halfling woman and the widow of **Clarence Mayfield**, the former Governor of [[Avalon]]. She joined the party in [[Durvish City]] to travel to [[Adalens]].

## Biography & Personality
Mary-Ann maintains the demeanor of a high-society noblewoman, though she is belligerent when slighted. She is generous with her companions but ruthless with her enemies. Her hobbies include knitting (often in taverns) and utilizing her unassuming appearance to catch opponents off guard.

**Combat Style**
She utilizes a shortbow and sneak attacks, often hiding behind the taller party members or scuttling underneath larger foes to strike with her knitting needles (improvised daggers).

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 35]] (High-Maintenance Introduction):** Accused a ship worker of theft upon disembarking in [[Durvish City]], joined the party to [[The Blushing Manatee]], and demanded a private suite before buying rounds of drinks for the group.
* **[[Entry 36]] (Cryptanalysis & Investigation):** Used local knowledge to decipher the encoded letters, identifying a rejuvenated district and locating [[The Gilded Swan]] tavern mentioned in the text.
* **[[Entry 36]] (The Chocolate Interrogation):** Lured [[Jimmy]] out of the bushes at [[The Gilded Swan]] using chocolate and interrogated him to uncover a plot involving a gang and a "prank" with rotten eggs.
* **[[Entry 36]] (Fog Ambush Tactics):** Sneak attacked a marauder with a shortbow during the ambush on the road to [[Onasea]] and slashed a brute with her dagger after scuttling underneath it.
* **[[Entry 36]] (Combat Maneuvers):** Escaped a net trap, disengaged to cover, and successfully sneak attacked the [[bandit captain|Captain]] with her bow before forcing the final marauder to surrender.
* **[[Entry 37]] (Knitting Needle Justice):** Executed the captured bandits by stabbing them in the stomach with her knitting needles after the party deferred the decision of their fate to her.
* **[[Entry 37]] (Cautious Looting):** Refused to wear a magical black cloak found at the [[Bandit Lake House]] due to undefined risks.
* **[[Entry 38]] (Tracking Corbin):** Spotted suspicious crates with feather symbols at the [[Onasea]] docks and tracked footprints to discover the entrance to the [[Onasea Sea Cave]].
* **[[Entry 38]] (The Sea Cave Escape):** Negotiated entry into the cave with [[Corbin]] using a code phrase, then escaped a necromancer attack by being carried by [[Anaphel]] through a submerged tunnel.
* **[[Entry 38]] (Aid to the Displaced):** Provided [[Corbin]] with a bag of gold to help him restart his life after he refused further assistance from the party.
* **[[Entry 40]] (The Frozen Wraith):** After a bout of seasickness, Mary-Ann helped defend the *[[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]]* by [sneak-attacking](https://2014.5e.tools/classes.html#rogue_phb,state:feature=s0-1) the undead captain from behind the gunwale. Following the battle, she meddled in the galley before proving vital during a supernatural storm, successfully repairing the ship and remaining on her feet when massive waves knocked the others down.

## The Adalens Hit
During the journey to [[Onasea]], the party was ambushed by bandits led by **[[Greg (Bandit Captain)|Greg]]**. Under interrogation, Greg revealed that Mary-Ann was the specific target of this hit, ordered by the Thieves' Guild in [[Adalens]].

## Notable Equipment
* **Knitting Needles:** Her signature improvised melee weapons.
* **Shortbow:** Her primary ranged weapon.
