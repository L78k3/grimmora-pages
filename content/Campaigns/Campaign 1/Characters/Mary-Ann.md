# Mary-Ann

> [!info] Character Info
> **Player:** Suzanne
> **Class:** Rogue (Implied by Sneak Attack)
> **Background:** Noble / Widow

**Mary-Ann** (née Mayfield) is an elderly halfling woman who joined the party in [[Durvish City]].

## Background

She is the widow of **Clarence Mayfield**, the former Governor of [[Avalon]]. She is well-acquainted with the nobility of the region, specifically recognizing [[Lavender]] and asking after her grandmother.

## Current Mission

She is traveling to [[Adalens]] to collect her goddaughter's child to take them on a tour. She asked to accompany the party as an escort to the city.

## Personality & Skills

* **Demeanor:** She can be belligerent when slighted (as seen when her bag went "missing") but is generous with her companions, buying rounds of drinks and paying for a luxury suite.
* **Hobbies:** She knits frequently, even bringing her knitting to the tavern bar.
* **Combat:** Despite her age, she is capable in a fight. During the bandit ambush on the road to [[Onasea]], she hid behind Lavender and utilized a shortbow to deliver a precise sneak attack.
