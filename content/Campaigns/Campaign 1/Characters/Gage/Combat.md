## The "Nova" Turn (First Round)
*If you take the Attack action on your first turn:*
1.  **Movement:** +10 ft (35 ft total).
2.  **Attacks:** 2 Attacks (Standard + Dread Ambusher Extra).
3.  **Bonus Damage:** If the extra attack hits, add **1d8**(`dice: 1d8`).
4.  **Assassinate:** If target hasn't acted yet -> **Advantage**. If target is surprised -> **Auto-Crit**.

## Class Features & Feats
* **Sneak Attack:** +**3d6** (`dice: 3d6`) damage (Once/turn if Adv or Ally within 5ft).
* **Umbral Sight:** Invisible to creatures using Darkvision to see you in darkness.
* **Cunning Action:** Bonus Action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide.
