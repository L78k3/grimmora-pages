# Session Debrief: The Alchemist's Trail

> [!summary] The Catch-Up
> The party successfully rescued the sharp-minded clerk, Petra Greenfield, from the smuggler Captain Holloway at the derelict Old Mill. After securing a handsome 500-gold reward from Marvis Colton, the group retired for the evening, but not before Gage requested a Mages' Guild disguise. The stage is now set for tomorrow morning's high-stakes, 8:00 AM rendezvous with Lady Elara Mythin at the Arch-mage's Sanctum, where the party hopes to finally unmask "The Alchemist."

We started at 8 am sharp, the tailor did not have the mages clothes ready overnight, so we will have to return for these. We met with [[Elara Mythin|Lady Mythin]], and followed her to the top of a 6-storey academy, each level increasing in terms of secrecy, and therefore secure access, requiring different levels of clearance for each one. Lady Mythin escorted us to the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]], which was the whole top floor if I remember correctly. There we found 3 researchers, a lady at a cauldron with a squirrel, a dwarf with bejewelled beard studying gems, and a skinnier older gentleman studying the colour reactions of ingredients shown to open flame. [[Theren]] asked the cauldron lady about his curse with his name, and she suggested that going dry on the life force presumable drained by the damage he takes would prompt the fey to investigate the reason their curse isn't working as it did previously.

Also, does Theren's lingering desire to confront Theron Vex over the kidnapping plot cause any friction or delays before the meeting?
