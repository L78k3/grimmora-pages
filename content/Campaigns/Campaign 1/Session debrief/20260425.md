# Session Debrief: The Alchemist's Trail

> [!summary] The Catch-Up
> The party successfully rescued the sharp-minded clerk, Petra Greenfield, from the smuggler Captain Holloway at the derelict Old Mill. After securing a handsome 500-gold reward from Marvis Colton, the group retired for the evening, but not before Gage requested a Mages' Guild disguise. The stage is now set for tomorrow morning's high-stakes, 8:00 AM rendezvous with Lady Elara Mythin at the Arch-mage's Sanctum, where the party hopes to finally unmask "The Alchemist."

We started at 8 am sharp, the tailor did not have the mages clothes ready for Gage overnight, so we will have to return for these. We gave all the gold to Lavender as the usual party treasurer. [[Mary-Ann]] received a letter from her god daughter welcoming her to the city of [[Adalens]] and inviting her to a ball letter that evening, requesting she be chaperoned.

> [!question] The Main Event
> How smoothly does the infiltration of the Mages' Guild go with Gage's new disguise? Does Elara Mythin manage to guide the party to the Alchemist without drawing the attention of the ruling families' pervasive spies, or does the Sanctum hold its own set of magical security traps? Also, does Theren's lingering desire to confront Theron Vex over the kidnapping plot cause any friction or delays before the meeting?

Theren does not confront [[Theron Vex]]. We simply made our way straight there. We met with [[Elara Mythin|Lady Mythin]], and followed her to the top of a 6-storey academy, each level increasing in terms of secrecy, and therefore secure access, requiring different levels of clearance for each one. Lady Mythin escorted us to the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]], which was the whole top floor if I remember correctly. There we found 3 researchers, a lady at a cauldron with a squirrel, a dwarf with bejewelled beard studying gems, and a skinnier older gentleman studying the colour reactions of ingredients shown to open flame. 

[[Theren]] asked the cauldron lady about his curse with his name, and she suggested that going dry on the life force presumable drained by the damage he takes would prompt the fey to investigate the reason their curse isn't working as it did previously. 

[[Temerity|Temerity/Diffidence]] Used his ability to speak with animals to converse with the squirrel who had been rejuvenated by the [[Miriam]]'s concoction from the cauldron. She notes that she'd like to improve the formula to crate some kind of cure-all.

[[Gage]] asked the [[Thomas]], gem-crafting dwarven alchemist firstly about the gem he was working on, suggesting it could be some form of amethyst. Conversation moved to the letters, as well as how information is passed around an environment like this, given the secrecy. Gage wondered aloud if commission work was the model of business, to which the dwarf responded that their work serves all five families equally.

As [[Lavender]] inquired about her brother to the [[Felix]] the fire alchemist, a fight broke out, with the mage dishing out many powerful attacks, including a 56 damage nuke in a 15ft radius, catching half of the combatants (which included Miriam as a healer and Thomas as a support) in it's blaze.

The fight subsumed after a few rounds of combat and we interrogated Felix for information. He worships Sargonnas, and is a member of [[Cult of the Master of Ash]]. He knows [[Orris Locke]] met with the Arch Mage 3 days prior, meaning the party was late by a very short time.

> [!thought] Internal Monologue: Gage
> Gage has made a conscious choice to hide his past connections to the Adalens Thieves' Guild while in the capital. How is the stress of keeping this secret affecting his normally pragmatic, methodical mindset? As he puts on his Mages' Guild disguise to infiltrate the Sanctum, does slipping back into the deceptive role of a spy bring up any lingering guilt from his days serving the necromancers and the Greengather Blades?

I think he's completely okay with using any tools necessary to gain the upper hand - he also suggested that we could possibly recruit some of the mages practising item identification to have some of out relics identified such as the [[Chronometer Pocket Watch]] and the [[Wand of Strange Glyphs]] amongst others. I think he would notice the parallels of the previous role he had, but he can appreciate using the skills and the things he is good at to bring about positive change, from a place of one's own chosen destiny.
