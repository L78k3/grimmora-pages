## Scene Overview

The party arrives at a farm on the outskirts of Uwendale, which is under attack by a wyvern or drake. Amidst the chaos - burning hay, fleeing livestock, and terrified villagers - the party must drive off the beast and secure the area. In the aftermath, they discover signs that several villagers have been abducted and taken toward the mountains, revealing the attack was a diversion for a kidnapping.

## Objectives

- Primary: Defend the farm, protect or rescue as many villagers as possible, and drive off or slay the wyvern/drake.
- Secondary: Assess the damage, calm survivors, and investigate for clues about the missing villagers.

## Key NPCs & Creatures

- Wyvern or drake: The immediate threat, aggressive and territorial.
- Villagers: Some hiding, some fleeing, a few possibly wounded or trapped.
- Survivors: May provide information about what happened and who was taken.

## Setting & Atmosphere

- The farm is set in the rugged, rocky highlands near Uwendale, with fields, barns, and livestock pens.
- Smoke and fire from the attack; the air is filled with the sounds of panicked animals and shouting.
- The landscape is scarred by claw marks, scattered belongings, and signs of a hasty struggle.

## Challenges & Obstacles

- Combat: Face off against the wyvern or drake, using terrain for cover or advantage.
- Rescue: Saving villagers trapped in burning buildings or threatened by the beast.
- Investigation: Spotting tracks, broken fences, or other evidence pointing to the kidnappers’ route.

## Clues & Rewards

- Clues: Drag marks, foreign boot prints, or a dropped token suggesting Noxian or Freljordian involvement.
- Rewards: Grateful survivors may offer supplies, information, or minor boons; the party may salvage useful gear from the farm.

## Complications & Twists

- The wyvern/drake may retreat into the mountains if not slain, potentially reappearing later.
- Some villagers are missing - taken alive, suggesting a coordinated enemy plot.
- The attack was clearly a diversion; evidence points toward a cave in the mountains as the kidnappers’ destination.

## Transition

With the farm secured and clues in hand, the party must decide whether to immediately pursue the kidnappers into the mountains or regroup at the outpost. Their actions here set up the next phase: a rescue mission and cave exploration, with the fate of the villagers - and perhaps the whole outpost - at stake.
