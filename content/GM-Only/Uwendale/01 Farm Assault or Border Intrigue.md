## Scene Overview

The party faces a crucial decision: respond immediately to a nearby farm under attack (Farm Assault), or pursue intelligence suggesting Noxian or Freljordian agents are operating near the border (Border Intrigue). The outpost is stretched thin, and the choice will shape the day's events and reveal different facets of the threats facing Uwendale.

## Objectives

- Primary: Decide as a unit which crisis to prioritize—defend the farm or investigate the border.
- Secondary: Demonstrate teamwork, leadership, and adaptability under pressure; gather as much information as possible about both threats before acting.

## Key NPCs & Creatures

- Lieutenant Magnus Crownguard: Offers briefing and expects swift, disciplined action.
- Messenger or survivor: Provides details about the farm attack or suspicious border activity.
- Possible villagers or scouts: May be present to answer questions or plead for help.
- (Depending on choice) Wyvern or drake at the farm, Noxian/Freljordian operatives at the border.

## Setting & Atmosphere

- The outpost’s command tent or central yard, tense with urgency and the clangour of boots and armour.
- The distant sound of alarm bells or shouts from the farm, and the cold wind carrying rumours of movement on the border.
- The sense of being watched—by both villagers and possible enemy scouts.

## Challenges & Obstacles

- Time pressure: Both threats are urgent, and hesitation could mean loss of life or strategic disadvantage.
- Social challenge: Convincing the squad (and possibly the lieutenant) of the best course of action.
- Limited resources: The outpost cannot respond to both threats at full strength.
- Potential for division: Some NPCs or even PCs may disagree on priorities.

## Clues & Rewards

- Clues: Eyewitness accounts, tracks, or coded messages hinting at a larger plot.
- Rewards: Early respect or suspicion from the lieutenant and villagers based on the party’s decision; potential for surprise advantage (or disadvantage) in the next encounter depending on preparedness and information gathered.

## Complications & Twists

- Whichever crisis is not chosen may escalate or have consequences later (e.g., more villagers taken, enemy agents gaining ground).
- Evidence may suggest the two events are connected—a coordinated distraction or multi-pronged attack.

## Transition

The party splits or commits to one path, moving rapidly toward the farm or the border. Their decision launches them into the next encounter—either the Farm Assault (combat, rescue, and investigation) or Border Intrigue (stealth, social, and intelligence gathering)—and sets up repercussions for the rest of the adventure
