# The Frost

> [!info] Status
> **Exploration:** Partially explored
> **Major Settlement:** [[Mightrest]]

## Overview

The harsh northern territories of Grimmora, characterized by snow-covered landscapes, freezing temperatures, and dangerous mountain passes. The Frost is a land of hardy survivors, ancient mysteries, and deadly creatures.

## Geography

The Frost dominates the northern reaches of the known world, featuring:
- Snow-covered mountain ranges with treacherous passes
- Dense pine forests inhabited by dangerous creatures
- High-altitude settlements built to withstand extreme cold
- Mountain caves and hidden valleys

The climate is harsh year-round, with bitter cold, heavy snowfall, and dangerous weather conditions. Travelers need proper winter gear and supplies to survive here.

## Major Locations

### Towns & Villages
- [[Mightrest]] - Northern town that suffered from a magical plague (now resolved); base for mountain expeditions
- [[Frostpeak Pass]] - Small village serving as a waystation for travelers heading deeper into the mountains
- [[Arcadia]] - City to the northwest, reportedly taken over by necromancers (not yet visited)

### Landmarks
- [[Alpine Forest]] - Forested area northwest of Mightrest where we hunted ice wolves
- [[Dragon's Nest]] - Mountain lair where we defeated an ice dragon
- [[Auril's Church]] - Hidden church to the goddess of duality, guarded by dangerous vines

### Dungeons & Dangerous Sites
- [[Vampire Mansion]] - Former vampire lair on the hill above Mightrest
- [[Vampire Mansion]] - Multi-story mansion connected to the vampire threat
- [[The Trial Chamber]] - Underground trial system related to [[Auriel]]
- [[The Gladiator's Amphitheatre]] - Magical arena within the trial complex

## What We Know

The Frost is a region where survival is the primary concern. Settlements are small and isolated, with inhabitants accustomed to harsh conditions and constant threats from the environment and creatures.

The region has been touched by ancient magic and supernatural forces. We've encountered:
- A magical plague that turned out to be vampiric feeding
- Ancient trial systems left by the goddess Auril
- Ice dragons and other cold-adapted monsters
- [[Ice Wolves]] hunting in packs

Despite the dangers, there are resources here worth braving the cold for - we've found winter gear, potions, and other supplies necessary for mountain expeditions.

## People & Culture

The people of The Frost are hardy and resilient, used to hardship and isolation. They:
- Maintain small communities focused on survival
- Trade in winter supplies, cold-weather gear, and mountain-specific resources
- Have knowledge of local dangers (griffins, ice wolves, etc.)
- Show remarkable toughness in the face of supernatural threats

## Dangers

- **Extreme cold** requiring proper gear and supplies
- **Ice Wolves** hunting in the forests
- **Dragons** in mountain lairs
- **Vampires** and other undead (threat recently eliminated in Mightrest)
- **Magical curses** and ancient trials
- **Avalanches and treacherous terrain**
- **Snow-blindness** from the constant glare

## Connected Regions

- **South:** [[Avalonean Empire]] - Connected via roads from Avalon
- **West:** [[Arcadia]] and beyond
- **East:** Mountain ranges continuing toward [[Cenorillon]]

## Our Experience

We were teleported to [[Mightrest]] by the fey mage [[Millicent]] and spent significant time here dealing with the vampire plague. The region tested us with extreme cold, dangerous creatures, and supernatural threats.

Key events:
- [[Plague Investigation]] - Uncovering the vampire lord's scheme
- [[Battle at the Big House]] - Confronting the vampire
- [[Vampire Defeat]] - Ending the plague
- [[Dragon Battle]] - Defeating the ice dragon
- Various encounters with ice wolves and other creatures

The Frost has proven to be one of the most challenging regions we've explored, but also rewarding in terms of experience and loot.
