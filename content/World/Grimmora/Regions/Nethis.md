# Nethis

> [!info] Status
> **Exploration:** Unexplored
> **Capital/Major City:** [[Adalens]]

## Overview

Nethis is a region on the northeastern coast of the continent, visually defined by its massive island-city of [[Adalens]] and the floating structure of [[Adalens Ul-Maht]] connected to it.

## Geography

The region is centered on a large island or peninsula. It appears temperate and coastal, bordered to the north by the [[Golden Mountains]] and to the south and east by the ocean. Its most prominent feature is the large magical bridge connecting [[Adalens]] to the floating [[Adalens Ul-Maht]].

## Major Locations

### Cities
* [[Adalens]] - The apparent capital, a large walled city on an island.

### Towns & Villages
* [[Crystalmine Village]] - A village to the northwest, near the mountains.
* [[Dorado]] - A village just north of [[Adalens]], at the foot of the [[Golden Mountains]].
* [[Rampion Village]] - A village to the east of [[Adalens]].
* [[Port Adal]] - A coastal village south of [[Adalens]], likely its main port.

### Landmarks
* [[Adalens Ul-Maht]] - A massive, floating structure connected to [[Adalens]] by a skybridge.

## What We Know

[[Orris Locke]] has instructed [[Lavender]] to meet him in [[Adalens]], implying it may be a safe or neutral location away from the [[necromancers]] in [[Arcadia]].
