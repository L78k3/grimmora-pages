# The Spell Scar

> [!info] Status
> **Exploration:** Unexplored
> **Capital/Major City:** Unknown

## Overview

**The Spell Scar** is a desolate region located on the large landmass to the southwest of the [[Avalonean Empire]]. It is visibly distinct from the surrounding verdant lands, appearing barren and ruined.

## Geography

Based on the map, the terrain appears rugged, brown, and lifeless, contrasting sharply with the green forests of Avalon to the east. It features jagged rock formations or ruins scattered throughout the landscape.

## Major Locations

### Cities
* **Scarbrink Bastion** - A large fortress located on the border between The Spell Scar and the Avalonean Empire, likely serving as a defensive line.

### Landmarks
* **Agni Isle** - An island off the northern coast of the region.
* **Lake Soa** - A body of water located near the border with [[Durvish City]].

## What We Know

We have not yet visited this region, but [[Mary-Ann]] mentioned that a past attack on [[Durvish City]] originated from the direction of The Spell Scar.

## Dangers

The region implies a history of conflict or magical catastrophe given its name and the defensive nature of "Scarbrink Bastion." The attack mentioned by Mary-Ann suggests hostile forces reside or originate here.
