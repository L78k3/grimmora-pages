# The Bazaar

> [!info] Quick Facts
> **City:** [[Adalens]]
> **Character:** Commercial hub
> **First Visited:** [[Entry 42]]

## Overview

The Bazaar is the primary commercial hub for goods within the metropolis of [[Adalens]].

## Character & Atmosphere

As the central marketplace for the city, it is a location dedicated to commerce and the acquisition of various supplies. 

## Buildings & Points of Interest

- **Ancient Apothecary** - A shop that sells healing draughts, minor health potions, and snacks.

## People

- **The Ancient Apothecary Owner** - An extremely elderly man (described by Gage as the oldest man he has ever seen) who runs the apothecary.

## Notable Features

Unknown.

## Our Experience

During **[[Entry 42]]**, [[Gage]] visited The Bazaar while scouting the city for [[Lavender]]. He visited the ancient apothecary and successfully purchased two minor healing potions and snacks from the elderly proprietor.
