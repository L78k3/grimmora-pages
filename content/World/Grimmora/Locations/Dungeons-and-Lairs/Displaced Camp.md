# Displaced Camp

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 11]]
> **Region:** [[The Frost]]

The **Displaced Camp** is a hidden, subterranean settlement located deep within the mountains of [[The Frost]]. It currently serves as the base of operations for [[The Displaced]].

## Overview

We discovered this location after being captured in the [[Spider Cave (The Frost)|spider tunnels]] north of the mountains. It is a massive main cavern described as a "mini underground city". Despite being deep underground, the area is brilliantly lit with torches. The architecture is chaotic and "hodge-podge," suggesting a settlement built quickly out of necessity, yet it functions effectively as both a home and a military base.

One of the guards referred to the cavern as "the place of wandering souls".

## Layout & Facilities

The cavern is divided into several functional areas:

* **The Cages:** Located in a dismal corner of the cavern, these holding cells are reinforced with magical protections to contain prisoners.
* **Training Grounds:**
    * **Martial Training:** An area where soldiers practice with twin blades, claymores, axes, and halberds.
    * **Arcane Research:** A section dedicated to magic, containing alchemy stations and research instruments. [[Theren]] observed sorcerers training and halflings working at alchemy benches here.
* **Command Centre:**
    * **Bromelia's Study:** A modest room with a sofa where [[Lady Bromelia]] conducts private meetings.
    * **War Room:** Contains a large map tracking the locations of the few allies and many enemies of [[The Displaced]].
* **Living Areas:** The camp includes sleeping quarters and a canteen serving simple provisions like bread, cheese, and cured meats.

## Inhabitants

The camp hosts a diverse population of races including orcs, half-orcs, dragonborn, humans, halflings, gnomes, and elves. The demographic is strikingly young—mostly young adults and some babies, with a complete absence of elderly people.

## Notable NPCs

* **[[Lady Bromelia]]**: The leader of [[The Displaced]].
* **Elven Wizard**: A confident magic user in the research area who identified the nature of [[Theren]]'s patron.
