# Dragon's Nest

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 7#Events|Day 6]], [[Entry 8#Day 7 (continued)|Day 7]]
> **Location:** Mountains in [[The Frost]]

## Overview

The mountain lair of the ice dragon we were contracted to hunt. It is a high-altitude cave exposed to the harsh weather of [[The Frost]].

## Features

The nest was filled with the refuse of a predator:
- Piles of bones
- Scraps of fur and skin
- Scattered debris
- A makeshift shelter built by [[Theren]]

Based on the signs, [[Theren]] determined that the lair was occupied by only a single creature.

## Our Experience

We tracked the ice dragon to this lair on Day 6. After a long climb, we engaged the dragon in a ferocious battle. The creature's icy breath and powerful attacks nearly overwhelmed us, but we managed to exploit its moments of rage. The fight culminated with [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] jumping onto the dragon and plunging her sword into its eye, killing it.

The following morning (Day 7), we carved up the carcass. [[Theren]] retrieved some of its teeth, and [[Temerity]] hacked off the tail, proclaiming it the best part to eat. He cooked the [[Dragon Meat]] for the party, and we feasted on the dragon steaks.

To protect us from the exposed, high-altitude conditions, [[Theren]] constructed a makeshift shelter from the debris in the nest while [[Orion]] built a small fire.
