# Mausoleum (Alpine Forest)

> [!danger] Danger Level: High
> This location is guarded by [will o' the wisps](https://2014.5e.tools/bestiary.html#will-o'-wisp_mm), drow, and other dark-robed figures. It uses magical compulsion to force captives into slave labour.

## What We Know

We discovered this location by accident after [[Gage]] wandered from our camp (near [[Pinechill]]) while following strange green lights.

## Location

The mausoleum is hidden deep within an alpine forest off the road south of [[Pinechill]], within [[The Frost]] region. The area is marked by a proliferation of [will o' the wisps](https://2014.5e.tools/bestiary.html#will-o'-wisp_mm).

## Description

The exterior is an old, weathered, and mossy mausoleum standing in a dark courtyard. The ground is littered with small animal bones. The door was sealed with a small arcane lock, which [[Lavender]] broke open.

## Interior

* **Entry:** A long staircase descends from the mausoleum into a cavern below.
* **Main Cavern:** The stairs open into a large cavern with multiple passages. We heard the distant sound of pickaxes.
    * **Left Passage:** Smelled of food and had voices.
    * **Right Passage:** Was much quieter.
* **Side Corridors:** The right passage led to a branching corridor. We took a right turn down another set of dark stairs.
* **Mine:** The sounds of labour suggest a large mine is in operation.
* **Cells:** There is a cell block for prisoners. [[Gage]] was held here after being captured. The walls are etched with the same skull-with-diamond-eyes symbol seen outside.

## Inhabitants

* **[[will o' the wisps]]**: Lure travellers to the mausoleum.
* **Dark-Robed Guards:** Figures with torches and whips who oversee the slave labourers.
* **Drow Elf:** A guard we encountered in the lower corridors.
* **Dwarf:** A guard accompanying the drow.
* **Prisoners:** A large group of captives, including [[Gage]], who are magically compelled to work in the mine.

## Treasures & Items

* **Humanoid Skull with Diamond Eyes:** Seen outside in the courtyard, surrounded by wisps. This seems to be the source of the magical lure.

## Hazards & Traps

* **Magical Lure:** The [will o' the wisps](https://2014.5e.tools/bestiary.html#will-o'-wisp_mm) and the diamond-eyed skull create an illusion or compulsion that draws victims in. [[Gage]] was drawn to it and knocked unconscious.
* **Magical Compulsion:** Prisoners are magically forced to labour, as [[Gage]] was.
* **Arcane Lock:** The main door was magically locked.

## History

The structure is old, but is currently being used as a necromancer-run slave mine. The skull symbol etched in the cells matches the one in the courtyard.

## Our Experience

[[Gage]] was lured away from camp, captured, and forced to work in the mine. He faked passing out and was dragged to a cell. The rest of the party followed his rope, found the mausoleum, and broke in.

We navigated the tunnels, avoiding the mess hall, and encountered a drow and dwarf guard. [[Theren]] hit the drow with a [psychic lance](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#raulothim's%20psychic%20lance_ftd), [[Anaphel]] froze the drow, and [[Temerity]] successfully charmed the dwarf.
