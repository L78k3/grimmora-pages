# Vampire Mansion

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 4#Day 5|Day 5]], [[Entry 5#Day 5 (continued)|Day 5 (continued)]]
> **Location:** On a hill at the top of [[Mightrest]] 

## Overview

A multi-story mansion on a hill overlooking [[Mightrest]]. It was presented to the village as an infirmary where victims of the "plague" were taken to recover. In truth, it was the lair of the [[Vampire Lord]] responsible for the plague, who was using the patients as a food source.

After defeating the vampire, the party used the mansion as a temporary base to rest.

## Layout & Features

-   **Grand Foyer:** A massive, silent entrance hall with stone columns, a dusty grand piano, and a large central staircase.
-   **Organ Dais:** A magnificent pipe organ sits on a raised dais on the staircase landing. [[Temerity]] played this during our infiltration.
-   **Infirmary / Larder:** An upstairs room filled with rows of hospital beds. We discovered all the patients within were deceased, turning the room into a morgue.
-   **Library:** A side room with a hearth where the party rested after the battle. It contains many books, including volumes on celestial and abyssal beings, gods, and god-servants, which [[Theren]] studied.

## Our Experience

We infiltrated the mansion on Day 5, with [[Lavender]] posing as a plague victim. We were led inside by the [[Vampire Lord]], who intended to feed on her.

[[Temerity]] created a distraction by playing the organ, allowing [[Theren]] to attack and triggering the [[Battle at the Big House]]. The party, including [[Orion]], fought the [[Vampire Lord]] and his spawn throughout the foyer and up the stairs. The battle ended when [[Lavender]], after breathing fire from a potion, plunged her sword through the vampire's chest.

After the fight, we rested in the library. We collected the belongings of the victims, including children's toys, and returned them to [[Gorg]] at [[The Tavern]] for redistribution.
