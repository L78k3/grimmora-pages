# The Gladiator's Amphitheatre

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 8#Day 7 (continued)|Day 7-8]]
> **Location:** Part of [[The Trial Chamber]] complex

## Overview

A magical arena located within [[Auriel]]'s trial complex in [[The Frost]]. This amphitheatre is where the party faced [[The Trial of the Gladiator]].

## Features

-   **Magical Audience:** The stands of the amphitheatre are empty, yet they produce the sound of a roaring, cheering crowd, creating an oppressive and surreal atmosphere.
-   **Combat Arena:** The central floor is a designated combat zone, home to [[The Gladiator Wight]].
-   **Portcullis Exits:** Entry and exit from the arena are controlled by portcullises that open and close magically.

## Our Experience

After a long trek, we entered this arena to face our next trial. A single, powerful [[The Gladiator Wight]] emerged to challenge us.

[[Lavender]] engaged the wight in single combat while the rest of us watched from the stands, stunned. The wight was incredibly powerful, and the battle went poorly for Lavender. As she was near death, her intuition told her not to attack.

In a climactic test of faith, she allowed the wight to strike her down with necrotic energy. As she fell, her body vanished. The invisible crowd cheered, and an exit portcullis opened, allowing the rest of us-[[Theren]], [[Temerity]], and [[Orion]]-to leave. This was not a trial of victory, but one of sacrifice.
