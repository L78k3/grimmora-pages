## What We Know

Onasea is a coastal village located south of [[Avalon]]. We learned about it while planning our route to [[Adalens]], as it is known to have a quay. Our plan is to travel from [[Pinechill]] to [[Midward]] and then find a caravan heading south to Onasea to secure sea passage.
