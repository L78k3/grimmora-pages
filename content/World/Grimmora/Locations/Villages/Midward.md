# Midward

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 22|Day 18]]
> **Region:** [[Avalonean Empire]]

## Overview

Midward is a town south of [[Pinechill]] that serves as a waypoint for travelers heading toward the southern coast. It's larger and more developed than typical villages, with a marketplace and various services for travelers.

## Geography

Located in the southern portion of the [[Avalonean Empire]], between [[Pinechill]] to the north and [[Onasea]] to the south. The climate here is milder - no snow this far south, though overcast days are common.

Midward serves as a connection point for caravans heading to [[Onasea]] village and its quay, making it important for trade routes to the coast.

## Character

A nice town with a functional marketplace and services. It has more infrastructure than smaller villages like [[Pinechill]], serving as a proper stopping point for merchants and travelers.

## Points of Interest

### Marketplace
The town has an active marketplace with various merchants:
- **Weapon/armor merchant** - A dusty red-skinned dwarf with jet-black hair who deals in weapons and armor
- **Alchemist shop** - Purchases ingredients and sells potions

### Services
- Lodging (inns available)
- General supplies and provisions
- Caravan connections to [[Onasea]]

## Notable NPCs

- **Red-skinned dwarf merchant** - Jet-black hair, deals in weapons. Purchased [[Theren]]'s shortsword and light crossbow for 13 gold pieces
- **Alchemist** - Purchased spider fangs from [[Theren]] for 10 silver pieces each

## Our Experience

We arrived in Midward mid-afternoon on [[Entry 22|Day 18]], traveling with our new companion [[Anaphel]]. It was an overcast day, and we noted the milder climate compared to the north.

[[Theren]] conducted business in the marketplace:
- Sold a shortsword from a fallen foe and his light crossbow to the dwarf merchant for 13 gold pieces
- Examined the merchant's staves but didn't purchase
- Sold spider fangs to an alchemist for 10 silver pieces each

[[Anaphel]] disguised themselves and followed [[Theren]] during his marketplace activities.

Later, [[Temerity]] spent time meditating just south of town, dealing with personal matters related to his choker and his past before heading toward [[Durendal]] at nightfall.

## Travel Connections

- **North:** [[Pinechill]] village (day and a half walk)
- **South:** [[Onasea]] village with quay access
- **Caravan routes:** Regular caravans to the southern coast

## Strategic Importance

Midward was a key stop on our journey to [[Adalens]]. The town provides:
- Rest and resupply between northern and southern routes
- Marketplace for selling loot and buying supplies
- Caravan connections to coastal areas
