# Temple in Durvish City

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 1#Prologue|Prologue]]
> **Location:** [[Durvish City]]

## Overview

A large, opulent temple in [[Durvish City]] that was the site of [[Lady Bromelia]]'s wedding. The event was a major city-wide celebration, drawing visitors from all over.

## The Wedding of Lady Bromelia

This temple was the venue for the high-profile wedding of [[Lady Bromelia]]. During the ceremony, the temple was attacked, leading to the kidnapping that set our party on its initial quest.

## Our Experience

We infiltrated the wedding ceremony in its final stages. As the officiant was concluding the rites, the temple was plunged into chaos:

- **Spectre Attack:** Several [spectres](https://2014.5e.tools/bestiary.html#specter_mm) materialised within the temple, attacking the wedding guests and guards.
- **The Kidnapping:** Amid the panic, two mysterious figures descended from a hole in the ceiling. They seized [[Lady Bromelia]] and were lifted away by a [[griffin]].
- **The Escape:** The griffin crashed through a stained-glass window to make its escape, flying north and setting us on our path to [[Avalon]].

This chaotic event marked the beginning of our journey together, as we were tasked by [[Captain Arran]] to pursue the kidnappers and rescue the bride.
