# Bridgeport Warehouse

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 31]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport]] district in [[Avalon]]

The **Bridgeport Warehouse** is a large storage facility located near the docks in [[Avalon]].

## Exterior

The building is secured by heavy wooden doors surmounted by a seal insignia depicting a ship with full sails, water, anchors, and a twisted rope border. The windows are located high up near the roof and are obscured by a layer of crystallized salt.

It was guarded by two guards, **Martin** and **Roy**, whom the party shoved into the river to gain entry.

## Interior

The interior is dark and maze-like, filled with shelves of crates and boxes bearing various insignias. Scattered throughout the stacks are statues, some covered in cloth.

* **The Workshop:** Located in the back-left corner of the warehouse. It contains a workspace for a stone mason.
* **Necromantic Presence:** [[Theren]] sensed necromantic energy emanating from the statues—not enough to give full life initially, but enough to cause audible "whispers".

## Inhabitants

* **[[Bridgeport Mason]]:** A possessed stonemason working in the back corner.
* **Constructs:** Four human-sized statues and one larger golem, all of which animated upon the party's approach.
