## Overview
The Rotunda is the central civic building in Avalon where official announcements are made. It serves as a gathering place for citizens to hear news from the king's herald and other important communications. The city’s usual hustle and bustle was muted when the party visited, adding an unusual atmosphere.

---

## Geography
Located at the heart of Avalon, the Rotunda stands as a prominent landmark, serving as the focal point for civic activities and announcements.

## History
During the party’s visit, an announcement declared the continuation of a ball to raise funds for the search for the missing bride [[Requiem la Rouge]] despite recent tragic events. The party’s social interactions around this location revealed tensions and alliances, highlighting the Rotunda’s role as a center of city life and politics.

## Our Experience
The party heard the king’s herald announcement at the Rotunda, experiencing the tense social climate within Avalon. Temerity was eager to attend the upcoming ball planned to finance the search for Lady Bromelia Porthwaite, while others were more reluctant. Social dynamics unfolded here, including the party’s preparations and internal tensions.
