# The Tavern

> [!info] Visited
> **Location:** [[Mightrest]]
> **Owner:** [[Gorg]]
> **Region:** [[The Frost]]

## Description

The main inn in [[Mightrest]], serving as a gathering place, lodging house, and dining establishment for the town. During the vampire plague, it became a hub for information and a base for coordinating relief efforts.

## Layout

### Main Floor
- Common room for dining and socializing
- Bar area run by [[Gorg]]
- Kitchen where the [[Gnome Cook]] works

### Upper Floor
- Guest rooms for travelers
- Lodging for those staying in town

## The Crystal Orbs

The tavern is equipped with a remarkable magical feature: **crystal orbs** that can record and replay events.

### How They Work
- **Double-tap activation** - Tapping the orb twice activates recording or playback
- **Event recording** - Captures what happens around them
- **Playback function** - Can replay recorded events for viewing
- **Unknown range** - Unclear how much they can record or for how long

### Significance
These recording devices were invaluable during the plague investigation, potentially capturing evidence of what was happening in [[Mightrest]].

## Staff & Proprietor

### Gorg (Innkeeper)
[[Gorg]] runs the tavern and was one of the key figures during the plague crisis. He exhibited strange symptoms of memory suppression, particularly around the concept of churches, suggesting he was affected by the vampire's curse or magical influence.

### Gnome Cook
The [[Gnome Cook]] works in the kitchen, preparing meals for guests and locals. They witnessed many of the strange events during the plague period.

## Role During the Plague

During [[Mightrest]]'s six-month ordeal with the vampiric plague, The Tavern served as:
- **Information hub** - Where rumors and reports were shared
- **Coordination center** - Base for organizing aid and investigation
- **Safe gathering place** - Somewhere townsfolk could meet
- **Our headquarters** - Where we based our operations

The crystal orbs may have recorded crucial evidence of the vampire's activities.

## Atmosphere

As the main social venue in [[Mightrest]]:
- Warm refuge from the cold northern climate
- Community gathering spot
- Place where news and gossip are exchanged
- During the plague: tense and worried atmosphere
- After vampire defeat: recovering sense of normalcy

## Services

- Lodging in upstairs rooms
- Meals prepared by the [[Gnome Cook]]
- Drinks at the bar
- Information and local knowledge from [[Gorg]]
- Community space for meetings

## Our Experience

The Tavern served as our primary base during our time in [[Mightrest]]. We:
- Stayed in the upstairs rooms
- Investigated [[Gorg]]'s strange memory issues
- Discovered the crystal orb recording system
- Used it as a base for our plague investigation
- Coordinated with [[Gorg]] on helping plague victims
- Distributed recovered belongings to curse victims from here

The discovery of [[Gorg]]'s memory suppression regarding churches was one of our first clues that something supernatural was affecting the town beyond just the "plague."

## Connection to Village Inn

There's also a [[Village Inn]] mentioned in [[Mightrest]], which may be the same establishment or a separate one. The relationship between these two inns is unclear.
