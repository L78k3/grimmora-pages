# Rotunda

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Avalon]]
> **Type:** Civic Building

## What We Know

The Rotunda is a significant building in [[Avalon]]. It was the site of a major disturbance that resulted in a heightened city-wide guard presence.

## Our Experience

We have not visited the Rotunda directly. We learned of its existence on [[Entry 2#Day 3|Day 3]] when we were questioning an innkeeper about the unusual number of guards posted throughout [[Avalon]]. The innkeeper informed us that a "disturbance" had occurred at the Rotunda during the night, which prompted the increased security.

The specific nature of this disturbance remains unknown to us.
