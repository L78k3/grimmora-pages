# Tidecaller

> [!info] Status

The **Tidecaller** is a ship captained by [[Captain Voss]].

## Purpose

It is known for transporting illicit passengers or those who need to disappear, operating under the radar of the authorities. It is the intended vessel for the party's escape to [[Adalens]].
