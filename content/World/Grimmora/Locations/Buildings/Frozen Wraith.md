# Frozen Wraith

> [!danger] Danger Level: High
> A raised undead pirate vessel powered by an unnatural wind.

## Description
The *Frozen Wraith* is constructed of dark wood that is entirely covered in ice. It features torn sails that remain perpetually filled by a supernatural gale. While structurally similar to the pristine, light-absorbing vessels later encountered by the party, the *Frozen Wraith* appeared in a state of rot and decay prior to its second sinking.

## History
The ship was originally a pirate vessel that terrorized the region. According to [[Captain Voss]], it was supposedly brought down three weeks prior to the party's encounter by a kraken, a rival ship, or a force "not of the sea".

The vessel was raised from the depths through necromancy to intercept the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]]. It was captained by an entity calling himself the Master of Ash, who sought to retrieve "the ring containing the [[Winter's Core]]". During the engagement, the undead captain was destroyed by a [[Chikatika]]-enhanced guiding bolt from [[Temerity]], causing the ship to sink back into the ocean.

## Inhabitants
* **The Captain:** An undead entity claiming the title "Master of Ash" with a voice described as "colder than the grave".
* **Undead Crew:** Twelve undead figures that moved jerkily around the deck.

## Our Experience
* **[[Entry 40]] (The Frozen Wraith):** The ship ambushed the party on their journey to [[Adalens]]. The undead crew utilized grappling hooks to board the *Tidecaller* and fired cannons that damaged its deck. [[Lavender]] and [[Anaphel]] successfully boarded the *Frozen Wraith*, decimating the crew before the vessel was returned to the depths.
* **[[Entry 41]] (The Comparison):** [[Mary-Ann]] recalled a newspaper article regarding a cult that sank and raised ships in the name of the Master of Ash, noting a clear similarity between the *Frozen Wraith* and the much larger vessel commanded by [[Lyra Valstrath]].
