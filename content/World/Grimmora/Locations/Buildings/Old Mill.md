# Old Mill

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Adalens]], Warehouse District
> **Type:** Hideout / Derelict Mill
> **Status:** Abandoned

## Description
The Old Mill is a derelict structure that has been partially destroyed by fire. All of its windows are boarded up, and it features both a front and a side entrance. Inside, a stone staircase leads up to the second level.

## Purpose
Originally a mill, the ruined building was recently repurposed as a temporary hideout and holding location by a crew of kidnappers.

## NPCs Present
- [[Captain Holloway]] - Smuggler captain hired to hold a kidnapping victim.
- Three Thugs - Subordinates working under Captain Holloway.
- [[Petra Greenfield]] - Young clerk from Silken and Spindle, tied to a chair and held hostage.

## Atmosphere
The atmosphere is oppressive, and as one approaches the building, the smell of smoke and rotting wood is overwhelming.

## Notable Features
During the party's visit, there were three crates stacked outside, suggesting that something had been delivered recently. The boarded-up windows and fire damage make the interior dark and hazardous.

## Events
* **[[Entry 43]]:** Following a tip from local urchins, the party tracked the kidnapped clerk, [[Petra Greenfield]], to the mill. [[Gage]] climbed a tree to hide near the main entrance, while [[Lavender]] and [[Temerity]] entered through the front. [[Anaphel]] guarded the side entrance as [[Mary-Ann]] and [[Theren]] stealthily entered through the side door. After a brief standoff on the ground floor where Lavender incapacitated one thug and Mary-Ann shot another, [[Captain Holloway]] descended the stairs. Mary-Ann haggled with the captain and paid 22 gold for the smugglers to leave the premises without the girl, allowing the party to successfully rescue Petra.
