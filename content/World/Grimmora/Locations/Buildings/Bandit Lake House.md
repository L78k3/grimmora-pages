# Bandit Lake House

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 37]]
> **Location:** Near the road between [[Durvish City]] and [[Onasea]]

The **Bandit Lake House** was a hideout used by [[Greg (Bandit Captain)|Greg]] and his marauders.

## Description

A small, smelly hut located by a lake. It contained a dinghy that [[Lavender]] deemed untrustworthy.

## Loot

Following Greg's directions, [[Korako]] dug up a stash of **70 gold pieces** here. Inside the hut, the party found a **[[Cloak of Billowing]]** and [[Gage]] stole a kettle.
