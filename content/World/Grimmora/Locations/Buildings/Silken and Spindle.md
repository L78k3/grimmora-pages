# Silken and Spindle

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Adalens]], Shopping District
> **Type:** Shop
> **Status:** Open

## Description

Silken and Spindle is a large textile shop offering high-quality clothing. The interior features tools of the tailoring trade, including a dressmaker's dummy used for fitting and display. 

## Purpose

The establishment operates as a high-end clothing and textile purveyor. It is a highly successful business, outperforming its main rival, [[Vex Silks]].

## Services & Prices

| Service | Cost |
|---------|------|
| High-quality clothing | Unknown |
| Custom tailoring/disguises | Unknown |

## NPCs Present

- [[Marvis Colton]] - Owner; an older human man who runs the shop.
- [[Petra Greenfield]] - Clerk; a young half-elf who manages the shop's ledgers and numbers.

## Atmosphere

While the specific atmosphere is unstated, it is a bustling and prosperous business located within the busy, racially diverse shopping district of [[Adalens]].

## Notable Features

The shop maintains detailed business ledgers that track sales and client interactions. Notably, the smuggler [[Captain Holloway]] appeared frequently as the last entry of the day in these books.

## Events

* **[[Entry 43]] (The Missing Clerk):** The party arrived here after seeing a job posting in the shopping district. They met with [[Marvis Colton]], who explained that his clerk, [[Petra Greenfield]], had been abducted, pointing them toward the warehouse district and his rival, [[Theren Vex]]. After successfully rescuing Petra from the [[Old Mill]], the party returned her to the shop and collected a 500 gold piece reward. Before leaving, [[Gage]] requested clothes that would make him look like he belonged to the [[Mages' Guild]].
