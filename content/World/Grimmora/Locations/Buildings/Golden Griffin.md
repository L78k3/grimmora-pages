# Golden Griffin Inn

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Frostpeak Pass]]
> **Owner:** Female innkeeper
> **Rate:** 1 gp per night
> **Speciality:** Local knowledge of griffins

## Description

A welcoming inn located in the village of [[Frostpeak Pass]], serving as the primary lodging for travelers heading north into [[The Frost]]. The inn takes its name from the [[griffin|griffins]] that inhabit the nearby mountains.

## Services & Prices

| Service | Cost |
|---------|------|
| Room | 1 gp/night |
| Meals | Likely included or minimal extra cost |
| Stable | Available (price unknown) |

## The Innkeeper

The female innkeeper is a valuable source of information:
- **Knowledgeable about local griffins** - Has extensive knowledge about the [[griffin|griffins]] living in the nearby mountains
- **Helpful to travelers** - Willing to share information with guests
- **Experienced** - Clearly has been running the inn for some time and knows the area well

She can provide:
- Directions to griffin territories
- Information about griffin behavior
- Warnings about mountain dangers
- General advice for travelers heading north

## Atmosphere

As the main inn in [[Frostpeak Pass]], the Golden Griffin serves as:
- A warm refuge from the cold northern climate
- A gathering place for travelers' stories and information
- The last comfortable lodging before heading deeper into the mountains
- A hub for local knowledge and gossip

## Strategic Importance

Located at the threshold between civilized lands and the harsh northern territories, the Golden Griffin is:
- The last chance to gather supplies and information
- A place to hire guides or join groups
- Where travelers can learn about conditions ahead
- A safe haven before dangerous mountain journeys

## Our Experience

We stayed at the Golden Griffin Inn on [[Entry 03#Day 3 (continued)|Day 3]] during our journey north. The innkeeper provided valuable information about the [[griffin|griffins]] in the nearby mountains, which proved useful for our travels.

The inn lived up to its reputation as a comfortable waystation, and the 1 gold piece rate was reasonable for the quality and location.
