# The Snoring Jackal

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 22]]
> **Location:** [[Midward]]

**The Snoring Jackal** is a lively tavern and inn located in the town of [[Midward]].

## Overview

The establishment has a raucous atmosphere and appears to be a popular spot for locals and travelers alike. It is run by [[Azuul]], a tall blue dragonborn.

## Inhabitants & Staff

* **[[Azuul]]:** The owner and innkeeper.
* **Firbolg Bard:** A musician who performs in the corner. He engaged in a popular duet with [[Temerity]] during our stay.

## Our Experience

Upon arriving in [[Midward]], [[Lavender]] secured five rooms here for the party. While she handled the logistics, [[Temerity]] joined the resident firbolg bard for an impromptu jam session that the audience loved, whipping them into a "frenzy" with the magic pervading the music.

We used the common room as a meeting point to discuss our travel plans to [[Avalon]] and [[Onasea]].
