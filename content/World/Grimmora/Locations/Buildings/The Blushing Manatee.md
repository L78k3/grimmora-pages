# The Blushing Manatee

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 35]]
> **Location:** [[Durvish City]]

**The Blushing Manatee** is a large, well-used inn in [[Durvish City]] where the party stayed upon their return to the city.

## Atmosphere

The interior decor is described as muted grey and beige, featuring generic art on the walls. [[Theren]] noted it was "better" than the inn they stayed at previously, though he was disappointed it was a different place.

## Inhabitants & Staff

* **Receptionist:** A half-orc wearing an ill-fitting suit. He appeared bored and was angered when [[Temerity]] used *minor illusion* to alter a painting in the lobby to depict a gnome orgy.
* **Bartender:** A man impressed by Temerity's performance. He served the party a huge, unlabelled bottle of intoxicant "guaranteed to mess up anyone".

## Notable Events

* **The Special Bottle:** The bartender's unlabelled bottle released fumes that intoxicated the party (and [[Chikatika]]) almost instantly. [[Gage]] passed out after a single sip, and [[Theren]] vomited.
