# Arch-mage's Sanctum

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Mages' Guild]], Guild Quarter, [[Adalens]]
> **Type:** Guild Facility / Sanctum
> **Status:** Restricted

## Description

The Arch-mage's Sanctum is a highly secure and specialized area located within the [[Mages' Guild]] headquarters, which itself is a repurposed massive old temple situated in the Guild Quarter of the metropolis of [[Adalens]].

## Purpose

It serves as a private workspace and operational hub for high-ranking members of the [[Mages' Guild]]. According to intelligence gathered by Lady [[Elara Mythin]], it is currently the primary working location of a mysterious individual known as "The Alchemist."
