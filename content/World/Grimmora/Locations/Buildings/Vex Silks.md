**Theron Vex** is a merchant in Adalens and the bitter business rival of Marvis Colton.

## Biography & Personality
Theron Vex is the owner and operator of the textile business Vex Silks. He is a ruthless and jealous competitor whose own business is performing poorly when compared to the highly successful Silken and Spindle. Driven by this financial disparity, Theron is willing to resort to severe criminal measures to sabotage his competition.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 43]] (The Kidnapping Plot):** Identified by [[Marvis Colton]] as the prime suspect behind the abduction of his talented clerk, [[Petra Greenfield]]. The party later confirmed his involvement when [[Captain Holloway]] confessed that Vex Silks had hired her to execute the kidnapping.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Marvis Colton]]:** His primary business rival in the textile trade. Vex's business sales are struggling in comparison to Colton's.
* **[[Captain Holloway]]:** A smuggler ship captain whom Vex Silks hired to kidnap Colton's clerk.
* **[[Petra Greenfield]]:** The young, sharp-minded clerk abducted on his orders to disrupt his rival's operations.
