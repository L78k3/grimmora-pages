## Overview
The North City Gate serves as one of the main entry points to the city. Recently, guards stationed here experienced episodes of memory suppression reminiscent of [[Gorg]]'s inability to process the concept of churches. This pattern suggests a broader magical anomaly or curse affecting multiple beings and sites across Grimmora.

---

## Geography
Located at [[Avalon]]’s northern perimeter, the gate marks the intersection between the city’s outer roads and its inner defensive wall.

---

## Points of Interest

- Guard barracks nearby, reported to be the first affected by memory disturbances
