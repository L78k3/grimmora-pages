# Guild's Rest

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Adalens]], Guild Quarter
> **Type:** Inn / Tavern
> **Status:** Open

## Description

The Guild's Rest is a prominent three-storey wooden building located within the Guild Quarter of the metropolis of Adalens. Inside, it is lit by multiple hearths, providing a comfortable and inviting environment.

## Purpose

It serves as a primary tavern and lodging house, frequented heavily by guild members and respectable travellers visiting the capital.

## NPCs Present

- **Dwarven Bartender** - A weather-worn dwarven woman with facial scars who manages the bar.
- **[[Cornelius]]** - A hairy man encountered breaking rocks in the bathhouse's ice pool.

## Atmosphere

The atmosphere is warm, welcoming, and notably respectable. The attached bathhouse is described as calm and relaxed, offering a quiet respite from the bustling city outside. 

## Notable Features

The establishment features an extensive bathhouse equipped with both a jacuzzi and a freezing ice pool. The third floor is specifically noted for its high level of respectability and security.
