# Jade Zephyr Casino

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 24]]
> **Location:** [[Adalens]]
> **Type:** Casino and business front

## Description

A flashy casino in [[Adalens]] featuring flashing lights and gaming noises. The Jade Zephyr presents itself as an entertainment venue but serves as a meeting place for [[Zadhir]]'s business dealings, both legitimate and "potentially underhand."

## Layout

### Main Floor
- Slot machines and gaming tables
- Relatively quiet mid-morning, presumably busier evenings
- Free drinks offered by servers
- Canapes available

### High-Roller Section
Behind a blue sparkling curtain:
- Private area with guards controlling access
- Plush office with dark green sofa
- Where real business meetings occur
- More exclusive and secure

## Entrance & Security

- **Bejeweled dwarf greeter** - Manages entrance, understands thieves' cant
- **Guards** - Control access to high-roller area
- **Casino chits** - Two complimentary chits given to new visitors
- **No tolerance policy** - Thieves explicitly not tolerated
- Security is professional and selective

## Games Available

### Slot Machines
Simple gambling machines on the main floor. Odds appear typical for casino games (mostly losses).

### Captain's Wheel
Run by a hulking human dressed as a pirate captain. Despite his enthusiastic performance, we noticed his façade slip - he seemed depressed underneath the act.

### Devil's Dice
Another game of chance. Located somewhere accessible on the main floor.

### Taimora's Favour
Mentioned by locals as a popular game (not personally observed).

## Staff & NPCs

- **Bejeweled dwarf greeter** - Professional, uses thieves' cant
- **High-roller guards** - Act as intermediaries for meetings
- **Pirate captain** - Runs Captain's Wheel, hiding depression behind performance
- **Servers** - Offer free drinks and refreshments
- **Old lady** - Regular at the slots, kind enough to share a copper

## Connection to Zadhir

[[Zadhir]], dressed in a beautiful green velvet cloak, uses the high-roller section as his base of operations. He:
- Conducts business meetings here
- Owns an inn in [[Adalens]]
- Engages in "potentially underhand" dealings
- Hires mercenaries and security through contacts made here
- Uses the casino as a semi-public yet private space

## Our Experience

[[Gage]] learned about the casino from a halfling bartender at another establishment ([[Entry 23]]), who mentioned [[Zadhir]]'s presence here and that there might be drugs involved. Initially planning to investigate with [[Temerity]], the party eventually visited together in [[Entry 24]].

### Arrival & First Impressions
Upon arrival mid-morning:
- Greeted by the bejeweled dwarf at entrance
- Each received two complimentary casino chits
- [[Gage]] tested security by asking about magic in thieves' cant
- Warned that thieves will not be tolerated
- The casino was relatively quiet at this hour

Party reactions varied:
- [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] - Guarded and watchful
- [[Theren]] - Awkward and overwhelmed
- [[Gage]] - Relaxed and observant
- [[Anaphel]] - Inscrutable as always

### Gambling Attempts
**Slot Machines:**
- [[Gage]] borrowed 3 silver from [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] and lost it all
- An old lady playing slots gave him a copper; he won back 3 coppers
- [[Theren]] also gambled and broke even with 5 coppers profit

**Captain's Wheel:**
- Run by a hulking human dressed as a pirate captain
- [[Gage]] and [[Theren]] both lost their remaining chits
- Noticed the pirate's façade slip - he seemed depressed underneath
- A waitress offered free drinks but [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]]'s glare scared her off

**Devil's Dice:**
- [[Gage]] asked [[Anaphel]] to play for him (for luck)
- Both lost their rolls

### The High-Roller Section
[[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] approached a guard at the high-roller area (behind a blue sparkling curtain) and offered the party's services as security for a journey south. After [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] warned us to be on best behaviour, the guard investigated and eventually invited us through.

### Meeting Zadhir
We were led to a plush office with a dark green sofa. [[Zadhir]], dressed in a beautiful green velvet cloak, met with us:

**The Interview:**
- [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] claimed they'd never met (unconvincing)
- [[Zadhir]] agreed to "play their game"
- He admitted needing a security detail for a caravan to [[Adalens]]
- Warned it would be a perilous journey
- [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] promised utmost discretion

**Complications:**
- [[Zadhir]] began softly taunting us
- [[Theren]]'s rage caused his magicka to surge, accidentally summoning two copies of [[Runcible]]
- [[Zadhir]] ordered [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] to make [[Theren]] calm down
- [[Theren]] dispelled the pseudodragons, looking abashed

**The Deal:**
- [[Zadhir]] revealed his business dealings are "potentially underhand"
- He owns an inn in [[Adalens]]
- Meeting time set for 4:30am the following morning
- [[content/Campaigns/Campaign 1/Characters/Lavender]] offered her left hand (without the ring) to shake
- [[Zadhir]] pulled her close and made a barb about noble etiquette
- [[Gage]] searched the office visually but found nothing important

### Impressions
The casino served its purpose - [[Zadhir]] uses it as a base of operations where he can meet people and conduct business in a semi-public setting. The high-roller section provides privacy for sensitive discussions. While we didn't uncover the full extent of his operations, we secured a job and learned he deals in questionable activities.

## What We Learned

- [[Zadhir]] owns an inn in [[Adalens]]
- He conducts "potentially underhand" business dealings
- The casino's high-roller area is where real business happens
- He has enough resources to hire mercenary security
- He operates with a mix of legitimate business and shadier activities
- His caravan route is dangerous enough to require armed guards
