# The Slippery Eel

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 37]]
> **Location:** [[Onasea]]

**The Slippery Eel** is a tavern in the coastal village of [[Onasea]].

## Atmosphere

The tavern is described as quiet and suspicious. The locals were unwelcoming and looked at the party with suspicion upon their entry.

## Notable Interactions

[[Lavender]] questioned the patrons about [[Corbin]]. A fisherman revealed that Corbin had been asking too many questions before disappearing, though the fisherman appeared confused and possibly drunk.
