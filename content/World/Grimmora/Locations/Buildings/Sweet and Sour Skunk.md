# The Sweet and Sour Skunk

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 27]]
> **Location:** [[Durendal]]

**The Sweet and Sour Skunk** is an inn located in the town of [[Durendal]].

## Overview
It appears to be a budget-friendly establishment, though it does offer expensive luxury suites. It is run by a goblin with violently green skin.

## Notable Events
* **Stay:** The party stayed here after their excursion into the [[Fairy Garden (Durendal)]]. [[Lavender]] insisted on cheap rooms despite the goblin's attempts to upsell them.
* **Oliver's Bender:** The missing caravan driver, [[Oliver]], was found in one of the luxury rooms here.
* **Thumpy:** The harengon/almiraj [[Thumpy]] was seen sitting on the bar drinking beer from a saucer.
