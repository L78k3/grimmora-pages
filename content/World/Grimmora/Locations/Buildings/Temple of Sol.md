# Temple of Sol

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 28]]
> **Location:** [[Avalon]]

The **Temple of Sol** is a large religious building in [[Avalon]] dedicated to [[Sol]], the god of the sun.

## Layout

* **Main Hall:** A large area for the congregation where sermons are held. It does not appear to have any overt necromantic symbols.
* **Staff Area:** Accessible via a back door behind the altar or stealthily from the side. It contains a corridor with six rooms:
    * **Dormitories:** Two rooms containing basic sleeping quarters.
    * **Study:** Contains desks and manuscripts. [[Gage]] found books on [[Sol]] and one on [[Auriel]] here, along with a cloudy dark gem hidden in a drawer.
    * **Refectory:** A dining area with a long table and benches.
    * **Privy:** A standard toilet.

## Notable Events

[[Gage]] and [[Theren]] investigated this temple suspecting necromantic activity. Gage infiltrated the staff area while Theren created a (failed) distraction. They were eventually caught and brought before the head priest. It was revealed that the clergy is being coerced by the [[necromancers]], and the priest is acting against his will.

## Notable NPCs

* **[[Priest of Sol (Avalon)]]:** The head priest who is currently being blackmailed by necromancers.
