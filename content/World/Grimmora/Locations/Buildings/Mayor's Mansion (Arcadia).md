# Mayor's Mansion (Arcadia)

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 16]]
> **Location:** [[Arcadia]]

The **Mayor's Mansion** is a large estate in [[Arcadia]] currently being used as a headquarters by the [[necromancers]] operating in the city.

## Overview

We infiltrated this location believing the leadership of the local necromancer cell resides here. The interior is surprisingly unkempt and in a state of disrepair, with indifferent servants who appear confused or magically compelled.

## Layout

* **Exterior/Courtyard:** Guarded by pale, unresponsive sentries who appear to be undead or heavily charmed.
* **Servants' Quarters:** Accessible via a balcony on the upper levels.
* **Office/Study:** A room occupied by [[Frolot]], a contact for the necromancers' spy network.
* **Library:** Contains historical records of the manor. [[Theren]] discovered a historian's log here that ends abruptly, but reveals the manor was significantly extended *downwards*.
* **The Cellar:** Implied to be the true heart of the operation, accessible via stairs found after deducing the building's downward extension.

## Events

Our party infiltrated the mansion using a multi-pronged approach:
* **Distraction:** [[Temerity]] distracted the front guard with a performance.
* **Infiltration:** [[Gage]] entered via the balcony and helped disguise [[Theren]] as a servant.
* **Encounter:** Gage physically tackled a servant into [[Frolot]]'s office to maintain their cover.
