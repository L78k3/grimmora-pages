# The Gilded Swan

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 35]]
> **Location:** [[Durvish City]]

**The Gilded Swan** is an upmarket tavern in [[Durvish City]] known for its ostentatious display of wealth.

## Description

The building is covered in finery, including gold leaf and stained glass windows. The rear features a garden with a pond containing waterfowl.

## The Investigation

The party investigated this location based on a clue from the [[Encoded Letters]] ("Location the third bush. East of the gilded swan’s nest").

* **The Magical Bush:** A non-native bush was found growing rapidly up the side of the building, blocking windows. It regenerated instantly when cut or damaged. [[Temerity]] eventually polymorphed into a giant ape to uproot it, revealing an extensive root system.
* **The Prank:** [[Mary-Ann]] discovered a group of children hiding in the bushes. They revealed they had been hired by a cloaked figure to "egg the place" once the plants covered the windows.
