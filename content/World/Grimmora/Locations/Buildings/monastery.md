# The Monastery

> [!warning] Unvisited
> We have not yet traveled to this location. All information comes from our companion, [[Orion]].

## What We Know

This is the hidden monastery belonging to [[Orion]]'s monastic order. It is extremely difficult for outsiders to find and is protected by powerful magic.

## Current Situation

The monastery is in a state of crisis. According to [[Orion]]:
- They recently lost their abbess and their [[First Fist]].
- Two new members were expected to arrive, but they never made it.
- This suggests the order is under attack from an unknown enemy.

## Security & Practices

- **Memory Wiping:** [[Orion]] mentioned that visitors to the monastery have their memories wiped before they depart, ensuring its location remains secret.

## Our Experience

We have not been to the monastery ourselves. Our connection to this location is through our companion, [[Orion]], whom we met in [[The Tavern (Mightrest)|The Tavern]] in [[Mightrest]].

He was investigating the disappearance of his order's members when our paths crossed. Believing the necromancers in [[Arcadia]] may be connected to the disappearances of his abbess and [[First Fist]], he decided to join our party. The fate of his order is a driving force behind his motivations.
