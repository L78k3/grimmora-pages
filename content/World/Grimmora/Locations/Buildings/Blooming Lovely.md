# Blooming Lovely

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[Adalens]]
> **Type:** Florist Shop
> **Status:** Open

## Description

Blooming Lovely is a florist shop located within the metropolis of [[Adalens]]. 

## Purpose

While functioning primarily as a florist, the location served as a designated rendezvous point for a covert meeting between the party and [[Orris Locke]].

## Atmosphere

During the party's visit, the immediate vicinity of the shop was tense and heavily surveilled. [[Temerity]] observed at least six different individuals in the area poorly attempting to remain inconspicuous while keeping watch.
