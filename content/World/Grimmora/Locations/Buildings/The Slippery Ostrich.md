# The Slippery Ostrich

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 30]]
> **Location:** [[Avalon]]

**The Slippery Ostrich** is a "touristy" and fairly non-descript tavern located in [[Avalon]].

## Inhabitants & Staff

* **Bartender:** A faerie with a surprisingly deep voice.

## Notable Events

* **The Missing Guards:** The new guard, [[Kevin]], deposited the unconscious night-shift guards outside this tavern to make it look like they had passed out drunk.
* **The Gauntlet:** [[Gage]] discovered a gauntlet near the door containing a fragment of a seal (rope and letters "NAV"), which led the party to the port.
* **Temerity's Visit:** The bartender reported that a nondescript man in his underpants (later confirmed to be [[Temerity]]) wandered in around 1:00 AM, asked for his location, and left.
