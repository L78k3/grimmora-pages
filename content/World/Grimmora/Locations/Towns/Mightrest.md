A town to the north of [[Frostpeak Pass]], suggested as a place to find griffin experts. The party was teleported here by the fey mage [[Millicent]]. Currently suffering from some kind of plague or ague.

A northern city afflicted by a magical plague for six months, accompanied by persistent thick cloud cover. Citizens who contract the plague become weak, pale, cold, and lethargic before being taken to the [[big house]] and never returning.

The party can acquire winter gear, healing potions, and other supplies here. Contains a clothier's shop and an alchemist, serving as a base for mountain expeditions.
