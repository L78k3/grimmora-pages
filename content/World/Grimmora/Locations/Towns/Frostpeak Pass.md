# Frostpeak Pass

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 3#Day 3 (continued)|Day 3]]
> **Region:** [[The Frost]]

## Overview

Frostpeak Pass serves dual purposes - it's both a mountain pass route through [[The Frost]] and a small village built around that route. The settlement acts as a crucial waystation for travellers heading north into the harsh mountain territories.

## Geography

Located at the southern edge of [[The Frost]], Frostpeak Pass marks the transition from the temperate lands of the [[Avalonean Empire]] to the frozen north. The pass itself cuts through the mountains, providing one of the few accessible routes deeper into the northern territories.

Accessible by road from [[Avalon]], making it a key stopping point for anyone travelling north.

## Character

A hardy frontier settlement built around the needs of travellers. The village exists primarily to serve those brave (or foolish) enough to venture into the mountains beyond. Despite the harsh climate, it maintains a welcoming atmosphere for visitors.

## Points of Interest

### The Golden Griffin Inn
The [[Golden Griffin]] serves as the main lodging in the village. The innkeeper is knowledgeable about the local area, particularly regarding the [[griffin|griffins]] that live in the nearby mountains.

## Services & Supplies

As the last real settlement before the deep mountains:
- Lodging at the [[Golden Griffin]] (1 gp per night)
- Cold weather supplies
- Local knowledge and guides
- Basic provisions

## Notable NPCs

- Female innkeeper at the [[Golden Griffin]] - Helpful and knowledgeable about local griffins and mountain conditions

## Wildlife

The area is known for [[griffin|griffins]] living in the nearby mountains. The innkeeper at the [[Golden Griffin]] has extensive knowledge about these creatures and their habits.

## Travel Connections

- **South:** [[Avalon]] via main road
- **North:** [[Mightrest]] and deeper into [[The Frost]]
- **Mountains:** Various mountain routes, including paths to griffin nesting areas

## Our Experience

We stayed at the [[Golden Griffin]] on [[Entry 3#Day 3 (continued)|Day 3]] of our journey. The village served as a perfect waystation as we pursued our quarry northward from [[Avalon]].

The innkeeper provided valuable information about the griffins in the area, which proved useful for our travels. Frostpeak Pass marked our transition from civilized lands into the harsher territories of [[The Frost]].
