# Adalens

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 41]] / [[Entry 42]]
> **Region:** [[Nethis]]

## What We Know

Adalens is a major constructed metropolis that did not grow organically. It is governed by a council of powerful guilds - including Mages, Merchants, Artisans, Scholars, Navigators, and Hospitallers - overseen by an elected Arch-mage. The city is characterized by its 30-foot white walls and a colossal floating island, [[Adalens Ul-Maht]], which dominates the skyline. While the city allows freedom of religion, most citizens view it as a frivolous pursuit. 

The city’s power is balanced between five ruling families who have "eyes everywhere": [[House Nightbrook]] (Information), [[House Silverwind]] (Artisans), [[House Crestwood]] (Merchants), [[House Mythin]] (Scholars), and [[House Kinver]] (Military).

## Geography

Adalens is situated on the northeastern coast of Grimmora's main continent on a massive island or peninsula. It serves as the primary hub for the [[Nethis]] region. The city features a massive bridge connecting the mainland metropolis to the floating structure of [[Adalens Ul-Maht]].

## Districts & Points of Interest

### Guild Quarter
The center of city governance and professional life.
- **[[Mages' Guild]] Headquarters**: A massive, old temple repurposed as the headquarters for the Arch-mage.
- **[[Guild's Rest]]**: A warm, three-storey wooden tavern frequented by guild members.

### The Bazaar
The primary commercial hub for goods.
- **Ancient Apothecary**: Run by an extremely elderly man; sells healing draughts and snacks.

### Temple District
Contains a variety of religious sites, though they are often marginalized by the general populace.
- **Shrine to [[Pellor]]**: A large shrine located within a church-style building.
- **The Iced Door**: An unremarkable closed door in the district found with ice crystals forming at its base.

### Affluent Area
The location of the massive estates belonging to the five ruling families.
- **[[House Mythin]] Estate**: A large estate where corridors are protected by shimmering magical wards.

### Other Points of Interest
- **[[Adalens Ul-Maht]]**: A floating island island with a colossal tower reaching into the clouds.
- **The Saltwater Exchange**: Recommended by [[Captain Voss]] as a primary location for gathering information.

## Notable NPCs

- [[Orris Locke]]: [[Lavender]]'s brother and a member of the [[Mages' Guild]] who is currently laying low after being discovered.
- [[Elara Mythin]]: Mistress of [[House Mythin]] and correspondent of [[Orris Locke]] who is "on the side of knowledge".
- [[Gregor Withwhile]]: A guard of the city watch stationed at the wrought metal main gate.
- [[Cornelius]]: A hairy man encountered in the city bathhouse known for "breaking rocks".
- [[Grawlock]]: The goblin butler of the [[House Mythin]] estate.

## History

Adalens is a constructed metropolis, designed and built rather than growing naturally over time. Its most prominent historical structure, a massive old temple, was seized and converted into the [[Mages' Guild]] headquarters to reflect the city's shift toward guild-based governance.

## Current Events & Rumors

- **Security Alerts**: [[Orris Locke]] has recently been forced to flee his usual haunts as "someone is onto him".
- **Entry Tax**: The city watch currently enforces a 1 gold entry tax for all visitors.
- **Family Vigilance**: Residents are wary of the ruling families, as they are rumored to have spies and "eyes" across all districts.

## Services & Commerce

- **Lodging**: [[Guild's Rest]] provides rooms, with the 3rd floor being notably respectable.
- **Hygiene**: A local bathhouse features jacuzzis and an ice pool.
- **Refreshments**: A coffee shop is located near the affluent family estates.
- **Medical**: Apothecary services are available in [[The Bazaar]].

## Travel Connections

- **Skybridge**: [[Adalens Ul-Maht]].
- **North**: [[Dorado]] (At the foot of the [[Golden Mountains]]).
- **East**: [[Rampion Village]].
