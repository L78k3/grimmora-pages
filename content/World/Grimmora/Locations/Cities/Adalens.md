# Adalens

> [!warning] Unvisited
> The party has not yet traveled to this location.

## What We Know

Adalens is a major city located on a large island. It is visually dominated by a massive bridge connecting it to the floating structure of [[Adalens Ul-Maht]].

[[Orris]] instructed [[Lavender]] to travel here for a safe, private conversation, providing her with a [[sending stone]] to make contact upon her arrival.

## Geography

Adalens is a large, walled city on the northeastern coast of Grimmora's main continent, situated on a massive island or peninsula. It appears to be the central hub for the **[[Nethis]]** region, which also includes the floating city of [[Adalens Ul-Maht]].

The city is surrounded by three smaller villages:
* [[Dorado]] to the north, at the foot of the [[Golden Mountains]].
* [[Rampion Village]] to the west.
* [[Port Adal]] to the south, which likely serves as its main port.

## Districts & Points of Interest

* **[[Adalens Ul-Maht]]**: A massive, separate structure that floats in the air, connected to the main city by a large, magical-looking bridge.
* **[[Port Adal]]**: A coastal village to the south, presumably the city's primary port.
