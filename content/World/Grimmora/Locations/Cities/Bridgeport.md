# Bridgeport

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** Taken here by the [[eladrin]]
> **Location:** [[fey realm]]

## Overview

Bridgeport is the fey realm equivalent of [[Avalon]] in the mortal world. This bustling magical city exists in the [[fey realm]] and serves as a major hub for fey creatures and their activities.

## Geography

Located in the [[fey realm]], an alternate dimension that mirrors certain locations in the mortal world. Bridgeport corresponds geographically to [[Avalon]].

## Architecture & Atmosphere

The city features stunning magical architecture:
- Buildings adorned with living ivy that represents the four seasons
- Structures that shift and change with fey magic
- A blend of natural and constructed elements
- Vibrant colors and impossible geometries

## Inhabitants

Bridgeport is filled with various fey creatures:
- Pixies - Numerous and bustling throughout the city
- [[eladrin]] - Fey elves who brought us here
- Various other fey beings
- Creatures of magic and nature

## Notable Features

- Seasonal ivy decorating buildings - representing spring, summer, autumn, and winter
- Magical architecture that defies normal physics
- Bustling markets and gathering places for fey
- Connection to the mortal world's [[Avalon]]

## Connection to Courts

As a major fey city, Bridgeport likely has connections to various fey courts, including the [[Court of Summer and Winter]].

## Our Experience

We were transported to Bridgeport by the [[eladrin]]. The city was overwhelming in its magical nature - filled with pixies darting about and architecture that seemed alive with seasonal magic.

The experience of being in a fey version of [[Avalon]] was both familiar and utterly alien. Everything had a dreamlike, intensified quality to it.

## Travel

Unlike mortal cities, travel to and from Bridgeport requires magical means - either through portals, fey gates, or being transported by powerful fey like the [[eladrin]].
