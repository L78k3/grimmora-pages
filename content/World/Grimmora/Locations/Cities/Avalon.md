# Avalon

> [!info] Visited
> **First Visit:** Early in our journey
> **Region:** [[Avalonean Empire]]

## Overview

Avalon is the capital city of the [[Avalonean Empire]], serving as the political and cultural heart of the kingdom. A major city with multiple districts, it's a hub for trade, governance, and diverse populations. The party has stayed here multiple times during our adventures.

## Map

![[City_of_Avalon.jpg]]

## Geography

Located in the western part of the empire, Avalon sits along major trade routes. The city is well-fortified with multiple gates providing controlled access.

## Districts & Points of Interest

### The Elvish Quarter
The [[elvish quarter]] is a district designated for elven residents and visitors, featuring distinctive architecture and culture.

### South District
Home to a [[Silvanus' Beard|Druid enclave]] led by [[Halsen]].

### Central District
Features the [[Rotunda]], the city's civic centre where official announcements are made by the king's herald.

## Notable Buildings

- [[Rotunda]] - Central civic building for public announcements and gatherings

## Gates & Fortifications

- [[North City Gate]] - Northern entrance with guard presence
- South-West Gate - Secondary entrance with increased security

## Notable NPCs

- [[Halsen]] - Leader of the southern druid enclave
- King's Herald - Makes official announcements at the Rotunda
- Various city guards and officials

## Services & Commerce

As the capital city, Avalon offers extensive services:
- Multiple inns and taverns
- Shopping districts with various merchants
- Religious establishments
- Magical services
- Government facilities

## Current Events

The party has used Avalon as a base of operations multiple times, particularly during the quest to find [[Lady Bromelia]].

## Travel Connections

- **North:** [[Frostpeak Pass]] and [[Mightrest]]
- **South:** [[Durvish City]]
- **East:** Routes toward [[Adalens]] and the [[Golden Mountains]]
- **West:** Coastal regions

## Our Experience

We've passed through Avalon several times:
- Early visits while searching for [[Lady Bromelia]]
- Stopped here on the way to [[Adalens]]
- Met [[Halsen]] at the druid enclave in the south of the city
- Used the city as a waypoint for various quests

The city has proven to be a reliable place for rest, resupply, and gathering information.
