# Arcadia

> [!warning] Unvisited
> The party has not yet traveled to this location.

> [!danger] Occupied
> This city has reportedly been taken over by necromancers.

## What We Know

Arcadia is a city in [[The Frost]] region that has been taken over by [[necromancers]]. This makes it an extremely dangerous location and a potential future destination for our quest.

## Geography

Located in the northwestern territories of [[The Frost]], west of [[Mightrest]] and [[Frostpeak Pass]].

## Current Situation

The city is under the control of necromancers. The exact extent of their control, their goals, and what happened to the original inhabitants is unknown to us.

## Known Locations

## Threats

- Likely undead creatures and dark magic
- Unknown dangers within the occupied city
