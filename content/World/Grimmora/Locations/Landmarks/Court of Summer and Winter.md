# Court of Summer and Winter

> [!info] Visited
> **Location:** [[fey realm]]
> **Rulers:** [[Summer]] and [[Winter]]

## Description

A powerful fey court within the [[fey realm]], ruled jointly by the lord and lady [[Winter]] and [[Summer]]. This court holds significant authority and has involved our party in matters important to the fey.

## Significance

The Court of Summer and Winter is a major power in the [[fey realm]], with enough authority to task mortals with quests and missions. Their interests appear to extend into the mortal world, making them important figures in the broader political landscape.

## Rulers

### Lady Summer and Lord Winter
[[Summer]] and [[Winter]] serve as the joint rulers of this court, their names reflecting the dual seasonal nature of their domain. The balance between these two forces seems central to the court's identity and power.

## Political Standing

The Court of Summer and Winter is:
- **Independent** from the [[Court of Flowers]]
- **Powerful** enough to authority quests for mortals
- **Connected** to the mortal realm through agents like the [[eladrin]]

## Connection to Our Quest

This court has been mentioned as **the authority behind our party's quest**, suggesting they:
- Have specific goals or missions they need accomplished
- Hold enough power to compel or commission mortal adventurers
- Have interests that span both the fey and mortal realms

## Relationship with Other Courts

The Court of Summer and Winter is explicitly **not affiliated** with the [[Court of Flowers]], indicating:
- Multiple independent fey powers exist
- Complex political relationships between courts
- Potential for conflicting interests or territorial disputes

## Location

Located within the [[fey realm]], likely in a place of significant power. We were brought to [[Bridgeport]], the fey equivalent of [[Avalon]], which may be connected to or near this court's seat of power.

## Our Experience

We have encountered representatives of this court during our time in the [[fey realm]]:
- The [[eladrin]] who brought us to [[Bridgeport]] likely serve this court
- The court has commissioned or authorized our party for some quest
- We've learned of their independent status from other fey courts

The exact nature of their quest for us and their ultimate goals remain matters we're still uncovering.
