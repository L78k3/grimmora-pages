# Fairy Garden (Durendal)

> [!danger] Danger Level: High
> This location is a fey pocket dimension used for storing stolen children. It is guarded by hostile fey creatures.

## What We Know

This is a pocket dimension accessible via a circle of daisies just outside [[Durendal]]. It appears to be a holding area for children taken as "favors" by fey vendors in the town market.

## Description

The environment is high-contrast and shifting. From the air, [[Runcible]] observed a maze, fountains, and topiary that constantly changed form.

The entrance leads to a gated area with two main sections:
1.  **The Lawn:** A large empty lawn where we were ambushed.
2.  **The Hedge Maze/Garden:** A section behind a large hedge.

## Inhabitants

* **Dryads:** We encountered three hostile dryads capable of casting charm spells and restraining vines.
* **Redcaps:** Two small, murderous fey wielding barbed sickles.
* **Blink Dogs:** Four teleporting dogs that attacked the party in a pack.

## Our Experience

We entered the garden to rescue [[Merisa]]. Immediately upon passing the gates, we were ambushed by a large group of fey. The battle was brutal; [[Theren]] was mauled to unconsciousness by blink dogs and redcaps, nearly dying before being stabilized by [[Anaphel]].
