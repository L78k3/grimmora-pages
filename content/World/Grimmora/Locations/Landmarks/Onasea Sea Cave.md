# Onasea Sea Cave

> [!danger] Trapped
> **Location:** North end of [[Onasea]] docks
> **Access:** Low tide only

The **Onasea Sea Cave** is a hidden cavern located at the northern end of the [[Onasea]] docks, accessible via a set of stairs and a sandy beach revealed at low tide.

## Investigation

[[Mary-Ann]] spotted footprints leading to this location while searching for [[Corbin]].

## Defenses

The entrance was rigged with a trap: a tripwire connected to a sprinkler system smelling of petroleum—a firebomb trap. [[Gage]] successfully disarmed it before the party entered.
