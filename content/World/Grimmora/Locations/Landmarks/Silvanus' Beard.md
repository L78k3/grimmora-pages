# Silvanus' Beard

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 27]]
> **Location:** Just outside the south-east of [[Avalon]]

**Silvanus' Beard** is a farming enclave and druidic sanctuary located on the outskirts of [[Avalon]].

## Overview
The area appears to be a farming enclave dedicated to nature and sustainability. It is led by the druid [[Halsen]]. The community focuses on practices such as sustainable fishing and animal husbandry.

## Inhabitants
* **[[Halsen]]:** The leader of the enclave.
* **Other Druids:** A community of nature-focused individuals living and working the land.
