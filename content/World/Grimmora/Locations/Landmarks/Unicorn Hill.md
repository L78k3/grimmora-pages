# Unicorn Hill

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 27]]

**Unicorn Hill** is a landmark located near [[Phoenix Hill]] in [[Avalon]].

## Overview
During [[Theren]]'s visit to the area, this hill was occupied by various animals—including a raven, a jackalope, and a weasel—playing together.
