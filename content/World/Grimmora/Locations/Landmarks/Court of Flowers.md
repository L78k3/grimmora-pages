# Court of Flowers

> [!info] Visited
> **Location:** [[fey realm]]
> **Type:** Fey Court

> [!danger] Dangerous
> The fey here have the power to take magic from others.

## Description

A magical clearing within the [[fey realm]] controlled by a distinct fey court. The clearing is characterized by special yellow and purple flowers that hold magical significance.

## Significance

The Court of Flowers is a separate fey power from the [[Court of Summer and Winter]], with its own rules, authority, and territorial claims. The flowers in the clearing appear to be connected to the court's magical nature and power.

## Location

Located somewhere within the [[fey realm]], separate from the territories of the [[Court of Summer and Winter]].

## The Fey Court

### Powers
The fey of this court possess dangerous abilities:
- **Magic absorption** - They have the power to take magic from others, making them particularly dangerous to spellcasters
- **Territorial control** - They maintain dominion over their magical clearing

### Language
The fey here cannot understand Common, making communication extremely difficult and potentially dangerous. This linguistic barrier adds another layer of peril to any encounter.

### Political Status
The Court of Flowers is **not affiliated** with the [[Court of Summer and Winter]], suggesting complex political relationships between different fey powers.

## The Magical Clearing

The clearing contains special yellow and purple flowers that seem central to the court's identity and possibly their power. The exact nature and significance of these flowers remains mysterious.

## Dangers

- **Magic-draining abilities** - The fey can steal magical power
- **Communication barriers** - Cannot speak or understand Common
- **Unpredictable fey nature** - Standard fey unpredictability combined with language barriers
- **Territorial** - Unknown consequences for trespassing or offending the court

## Our Experience

We encountered the Court of Flowers during our time in the [[fey realm]]. The experience was tense:
- Discovered their magical clearing with its distinctive yellow and purple flowers
- Learned they could not understand Common, making negotiation nearly impossible
- Witnessed or learned of their ability to take magic from others
- Recognized they were separate from and unaffiliated with the [[Court of Summer and Winter]]

The encounter reinforced the dangers and complexities of navigating fey politics and territories.
