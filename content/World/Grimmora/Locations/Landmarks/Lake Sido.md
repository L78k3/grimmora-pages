# Lake Sido

> [!info] Visited
> **When:** [[Entry 27]]
> **Location:** Between [[Durendal]] and [[Avalon]]

**Lake Sido** is a body of water located along the main road between [[Durendal]] and [[Avalon]].

## Description

We passed this lake while traveling south with [[Soliana]]'s caravan. [[Gage]] took a specific interest in the lake as they passed, but observed that it did not seem particularly special or magical at the time.
