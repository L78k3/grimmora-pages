# Alpine Forest

> [!info] Visited
> **Location:** Northwest of [[Mightrest]]
> **Region:** [[The Frost]]

## Description

A dense forested area in the northern territories, characterized by alpine pine trees adapted to the cold climate of [[The Frost]]. The forest is thick enough to provide cover for dangerous predators and contains numerous caves carved into the landscape.

## Significance

The Alpine Forest serves as hunting grounds for [[Ice Wolves]] and other cold-adapted predators. It's a dangerous area that has claimed the lives of previous adventuring parties, as evidenced by the remains found within.

## Location

Located northwest of [[Mightrest]], accessible for those brave enough to venture into the wilderness. The forest represents one of the natural barriers and dangers surrounding the town.

## Features

### Caves and Dens
The forest contains multiple caves that serve as dens for [[Ice Wolves]]. These natural formations provide shelter for the predators and are littered with the remnants of their hunts.

### Grim Evidence
Evidence of previous adventuring parties who met unfortunate ends can be found throughout the forest - a sobering reminder of the dangers present in [[The Frost]].

## Dangers

- **[[Ice Wolves]]** - Pack hunters that use the forest as their primary territory
- **Harsh weather** - Cold temperatures and potential snowstorms
- **Difficult terrain** - Dense forest and cave systems that can disorient travelers
- **Other predators** - Likely home to additional cold-adapted dangerous creatures

## Wildlife

- [[Ice Wolves]] - The primary predators in the area, hunting in packs
- Various cold-adapted prey animals
- Potentially other northern creatures

## History & Lore

The forest has claimed multiple adventuring parties over time. The bones and equipment found in wolf dens tell stories of those who underestimated the dangers of [[The Frost]].

## Our Experience

We ventured into the Alpine Forest northwest of [[Mightrest]] to hunt [[Ice Wolves]]. The expedition was dangerous but necessary:
- Explored wolf dens and cave systems
- Encountered packs of ice wolves
- Discovered evidence of previous adventurers who weren't as fortunate
- Successfully hunted the wolves and returned to town

The forest proved to be as dangerous as advertised, but we managed to navigate its perils and complete our objective.
