# Cloak of Billowing

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** cloak
> **Attunement:** Unknown
> **Value:** Unknown

## Visual Description
A black cloak with a red lining. When its magic is activated, it glows brightly with swirling magic and billows dramatically, completely independent of the surrounding weather or wind.

## Mechanics & Effects
The wearer can activate the cloak to make it billow dramatically behind them, regardless of the current wind conditions or weather.

## Lore & History
The party discovered this item while searching for buried loot following the bandit ambush on the road to [[Onasea]]. [[Theren]] detected magic on the item but was initially unable to identify its specific properties. [[Mary-Ann]] refused to wear it due to the unknown risk, but [[Temerity]] eventually agreed to put it on, despite worrying it would make him "look odd."

* **[[Entry 40]] (The Cloak of Billowing):** During a devastating, ash-scented magical storm aboard the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]], [[Temerity]] utilized the strange cloak to shield himself from the rain. The garment glowed brightly with swirling magic and billowed dramatically regardless of the wind, revealing its true nature as a Cloak of Billowing as he used it to rouse and inspire the battered crew.
