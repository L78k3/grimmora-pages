# Pulverised Gem Dust

> [!info] Obtained
> **When:** [[Entry 32]]
> **Where:** [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]
> **Current Owner:** Party (Two large crates), [[Temerity]] (Small box), [[Lavender]] (Small box), [[Bridgeport Mason]] (Small box)

**Pulverised Gem Dust** refers to the valuable material discovered in crates within the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].

## Description

The crates contained gems that had been crushed into fine dust. The dust glistens when viewed.

## Distribution

* **Large Crates:** Two large crates were transported to the ship by [[Theren]] and [[Anaphel]] to be taken to [[Durvish City]].
* **Small Boxes:** Inside one crate were three smaller boxes. [[Temerity]] took one, gave one to [[Lavender]], and gave one to the [[Bridgeport Mason]] to carry. The Mason's box was taken to the druid enclave.
