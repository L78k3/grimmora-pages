# Pulverised Gem Dust

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** Material 
> **Attunement:** None 
> **Value:** High 

## Visual Description
The material consists of gems that have been crushed into a fine dust. The dust is notable for how it glistens when viewed.

## Mechanics & Effects
Unknown. While the dust is considered a valuable material, its specific arcane or practical applications have not yet been determined by the party.

## Lore & History
The dust was discovered in crates within the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]] during the party's raid on the facility. It was found alongside other artifacts like the [[Corrupted Censer]] and the [[Chronometer Pocket Watch]].

**Distribution:**
* **Large Crates:** Two large crates were transported to the ship by [[Theren]] and [[Anaphel]] to be taken to [[Durvish City]].
* **Small Boxes:** Three small boxes were found inside one of the crates. [[Temerity]] took one, gave one to [[Lavender]], and entrusted the third to the [[Bridgeport Mason]] to carry. The mason's box was subsequently taken to the druid enclave at [[Silvanus' Beard]].

## Acquisition
* **Obtained In:** [[Entry 32]] 
* **Method:** Looted from the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]] after the defeat of the possessed artisan and the destruction of the [[Necromantic Idol]].
