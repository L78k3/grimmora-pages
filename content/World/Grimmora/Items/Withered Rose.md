# Withered Rose

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** cursed item
> **Attunement:** None
> **Value:** None

## Visual Description
The item appeared as a rose with withered petals. It emitted a foul odor that [[Korako]] identified as smelling "very bad".

## Mechanics & Effects
The rose acted as a beacon or source for a degenerative necromantic disease that actively drained the life essence of those in its proximity. The affliction primarily targeted the courier [[Loraleia]], but the plague had begun to spread to other passengers on the vessel.

## Lore & History
The Withered Rose was a cursed object discovered hidden inside a crate containing herbs and spell components within the cargo hold of the [[Paddle Steamer]]. During the investigation into the ship's quarantined floor, the party traced the source of the magical plague to this crate. To end the threat, [[Temerity]] destroyed the rose by casting *sacred flame*, immolating it completely and stopping the further spread of the curse.
