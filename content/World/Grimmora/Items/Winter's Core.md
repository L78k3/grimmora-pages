# Winter's Core

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** wondrous
> **Attunement:** Unknown
> **Value:** Priceless / Divine

## Visual Description
The Winter's Core is now confirmed to be the entity trapped inside the [[Ring of Orris]]. During the ambush by the High Priestess [[Lyra Valstrath]], she displayed a magical image of the ring to identify it as the object sought by the [[Master of Ash]].

## Mechanics & Effects
* **Balance vs. Ash:** The entity within represents "she who strives for balance".
* **Intense Cold:** Emits an unnatural absence of heat.
* **Release Procedure:** Releasing the entity from the ring is a "very complex procedure... fraught with danger".

## Lore & History
The Winter's Core is the central "competition" of the [[Master of Ash]]. By seeking the ring, the Master of Ash intends to destroy the entity within to "tip the scales in his favour" and secure his own power and vengeance.

* **[[Entry 38]] (The Smuggler's Secret):** [[Corbin]] revealed that he was hunted by [[necromancers]] who were searching for an artifact known as "Winter's Core".
* **[[Entry 39]] (Moonlight Resonance):** The [[Ring of Orris]] began refracting moonlight and forming rime, leading [[Anaphel]] and [[Lavender]] to theorize that the goddess [[Auriel]] was trapped within.
* **[[Entry 40]] (The Master's Demand):** The undead captain of the *Frozen Wraith* demanded the "ring containing the Winter's Core" in the name of the [[Master of Ash]].
* **[[Entry 41]] (The High Priestess):** [[Lyra Valstrath]] confirmed the Master of Ash has sought the ring to destroy his competition - the entity of balance inside. She identified her predecessor in this hunt as [[Magnus]].
