# Winter's Core

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** wondrous
> **Attunement:** Unknown
> **Value:** Unknown

## Visual Description
Suspected to be a gemstone or artefact. It has now been explicitly linked to the [[Ring of Orris]], suggesting it is either the ring itself or the dark, occluded gem set within it. 

## Mechanics & Effects
Unknown. It is deeply associated with death magic and emits an unnatural, intense absence of heat. 

## Lore & History
An object of immense interest to the [[Necromancers]]. They have been searching for it for months, intercepting ships and smuggling crates in search of it. [[Corbin]] believed it was the primary target of their operations in [[Onasea]]. 

* **[[Entry 38]] (The Smuggler's Secret):** [[Corbin]] revealed to the party that he was hunted by [[necromancers]] after tracking their unusual shipments. He discovered they were desperately searching for a mysterious, powerful artefact known as the Winter's Core, which he suspected was a gemstone or jewellery.
* **[[Entry 39]] (Moonlight Resonance):** While not explicitly referenced by name, the suspected Winter's Core - the [[Ring of Orris]] - grew intensely cold and refracted moonlight during the voyage. This activity spurred [[Lavender]] and [[Anaphel]] to research religious counterparts who might desire the frozen entity trapped within.
* **[[Entry 40]] (The Master's Demand):** The undead captain of the *Frozen Wraith*, the Master of Ash, explicitly demanded the party hand over the ring containing the Winter's Core. This confrontation prompted [[Lavender]] to seriously consider if the ring was indeed the sought-after artifact.
