# Ring of Orris

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** ring
> **Attunement:** Requires Attunement (Currently attuned to [[Lavender]])
> **Value:** Unknown

## Visual Description
The ring is made of silver and set with three dark, occluded jewels. When attuned or active, the smoke within the gems pulses in time with the bearer's heartbeat. It frequently forms rime and frosts the air around it, and it has been known to actively refract moonlight. 

## Mechanics & Effects
* **Necromantic Aura:** The ring glows with a faint blue light and radiates dense necromantic energy.
* **Unnatural Cold:** It does not just feel cold, but actively radiates an "intense absence of heat."
* **Binding Magic:** Identified by [[Halsen]] to contain immensely powerful binding magic.
* **Spells:** It grants the attuned wearer the ability to cast [spirit of death](https://2014.5e.tools/spells/spirit-of-death-bmt.html).

## Lore & History
Formerly belonging to [[Orris]], the ring serves as a tool of immense power and a deep source of tension within the party.

* **[[Entry 16]] (Discovery and Attunement):** Discovered by [[Guido]] inside a mead bottle, the ring was identified by [[Theren]] as a necromantic artifact belonging to [[Orris]]. [[Lavender]] attuned to it, experiencing a harrowing vision of her brother using its power to strike down and reanimate members of their own family.
* **[[Entry 17]] (The Undead Checkpoint):** [[Lavender]] utilized the ring to actively intimidate a defiant zombie clerk stationed in [[Orris' Secret Lair|the secret lair]] beneath the [[Mayor's Mansion (Arcadia)|Mayor's Mansion]]. By leveraging its dark connection to [[Orris]], she successfully forced the undead creature to bow and secured the party an audience with the [[necromancers]].
* **The Void Vision:** During a battle in the [[Mausoleum (Alpine Forest)]], [[Lavender]] was banished to a cold void. The ring's icy blue light filled the darkness, providing her a strange reassurance as a voice questioned her loyalty to her companions versus her goddess.
* **[[Entry 29]] (The Cloudy Gem):** When [[Gage]] presented a [[Cloudy Gem (Sol)|powerful, dark gem]] stolen from the [[Temple of Sol]] to the druid [[Halsen]], [[Lavender]] noted its striking physical resemblance to the stones set within the ring. Unsettled by the visual connection, she defensively concealed her gloved hand to hide the artifact.
* **[[Entry 34]] (A Celestial's Fear):** [[Lavender]] revealed the hidden ring to [[Anaphel]], who reacted with visceral fear and recoiled, sensing a presence trapped within. [[Lavender]] recounted her dark vision of [[Orris]] using the ring's magic, leading to a tense but ultimately healing conversation between the paladin and the aasimar.
* **[[Entry 37]] (The Goddess Trapped):** The ring began to emit a freezing mist and an intense absence of heat. Upon investigating its chilling aura, [[Anaphel]] and [[Lavender]] hypothesized that the goddess [[Auriel]] herself had been imprisoned within the ring by the [[necromancers]] to tip the cosmic scales.
* **The Moon's Grace:** In **[[Entry 39]]**, the ring grew intensely cold and refracted the moonlight at [[Onasea]]. [[Anaphel]] refused to hold it, sensing the entity inside might be the trapped counterpart to [[Auriel]].
* **[[Entry 40]] (The Master's Demand):** The ring was explicitly targeted by the Master of Ash, who commanded the party to hand over "the ring containing the [[Winter's Core]]." Following the battle, Lavender retreated below deck to ponder this terrifying revelation and the ring's true nature.

## Acquisition
* **Obtained In:** [[Entry 16]]
