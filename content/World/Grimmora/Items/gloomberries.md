# Gloomberries

> [!info] Item Info
> **Type:** Magical Fruit
> **Effect:** Creates magical darkness upon impact

**Gloomberries** are a magical fruit used by the druid children at [[Silvanus' Beard]] for pranks and tricks.

## Properties

When thrown or dropped, the fruit explodes on impact, creating a burst of **magical darkness**.

## History

[[Temerity]] witnessed the effects of these berries when the druid children (disguised as birds) dropped them from the great tree in [[Silvanus' Beard]] in an attempt to prank him. The children later asked [[Lavender]] for money specifically to purchase more of them.
