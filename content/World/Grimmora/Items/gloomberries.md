# Gloomberries

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** Consumable
> **Attunement:** No
> **Value:** Low (Sold for copper)

## Visual Description
Gloomberries are magical fruits that grow upon and fall from the great tree located in the [[Silvanus' Beard]] enclave. They appear as ordinary fruit until they are subjected to impact.

## Mechanics & Effects
* **Magical Darkness**: When the fruit is thrown, dropped, or otherwise explodes on impact, it creates a localized burst of **magical darkness**.

## Lore & History
These berries are primarily utilized by the druid children of [[Silvanus' Beard]] to facilitate pranks and tricks. During the party's arrival at the enclave, [[Temerity]] witnessed the effects firsthand when children disguised as birds used [wildshape](https://2014.5e.tools/classes.html#druid_phb,state:feature=s1-0~ishideoutline=b1) to drop the fruit from the canopy. The children later approached [[Lavender]] to request funds specifically to purchase a larger supply of the berries.
