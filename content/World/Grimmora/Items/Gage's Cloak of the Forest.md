# Gage's Cloak of the Forest

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** cloak
> **Attunement:** No
> **Value:** Unique

## Visual Description
It is a very dark green cloak featuring embroidered leaves around the edges.

## Mechanics & Effects
The cloak is magical and provides the wearer with enhancements to their **Nature** (+1) and **Survival** (+1) skills.

## Lore & History
**Gage's Cloak of the Forest** is a magical garment gifted to [[Gage]] by the druid [[Halsen]]. Upon arriving at [[Silvanus' Beard]], [[Gage]] bonded with the leader [[Halsen]] over sustainable practices and the "word of green". Impressed by [[Gage]]'s dedication, [[Halsen]] retrieved this cloak from a small hut and presented it to him as a gift.

* **Obtained In:** [[Entry 27]]
