# Gage's Cloak of the Forest

> [!info] Obtained
> **When:** [[Entry 27]]
> **Where:** [[Silvanus' Beard]] (near [[Avalon]])
> **Current Owner:** [[Gage]]

**Gage's Cloak of the Forest** is a magical garment gifted to [[Gage]] by the druid [[Halsen]].

## Description

It is a very dark green cloak featuring embroidered leaves around the edges.

## Properties

The cloak is magical and provides the wearer with enhancements to their **nature**(+1) and **survival**(+1) skills.

## History

Upon arriving at [[Silvanus' Beard]], Gage bonded with the leader [[Halsen]] over sustainable practices and the "word of green". Impressed by Gage's dedication, Halsen retrieved this cloak from a small hut and presented it to him as a gift.
