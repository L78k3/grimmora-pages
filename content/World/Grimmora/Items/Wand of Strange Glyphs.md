# Wand of Strange Glyphs

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** wand
> **Attunement:** Unknown
> **Value:** Unknown

## Visual Description
The wand is covered in strange glyphs that appear to shift or change when viewed out of the corner of one's eye.

## Mechanics & Effects
* **Charges:** It possesses a set number of charges that replenish after a specific duration.
* **Spellcasting:** The wand casts a non-offensive spell, though its exact function remains unknown to the party.

## Lore & History
The wand was discovered by [[Gage]] while the party was investigating the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]. Upon its discovery, Gage passed the item to [[Theren]] for examination. Theren noted the shifting nature of the glyphs but was unable to identify the specific spell contained within.

## Acquisition
* **Obtained In:** [[Entry 32]]
* **Method:** Discovered by [[Gage]] hidden within a hollow inside a stone statue in the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].
