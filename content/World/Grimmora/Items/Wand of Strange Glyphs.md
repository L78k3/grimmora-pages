# Wand of Strange Glyphs

> [!info] Obtained
> **When:** [[Entry 32]]
> **Where:** Found inside a statue in the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]
> **Current Owner:** [[Theren]]

The **Wand of Strange Glyphs** is a magical wand discovered by [[Gage]] hidden within a hollow inside a stone statue.

## Description

The wand is covered in strange glyphs that appear to shift or change when viewed out of the corner of one's eye.

## Properties

* **Charges:** It possesses a set number of charges that replenish after a specific duration.
* **Spell:** [[Theren]] determined that it casts a non-offensive spell, though its exact function remains unknown.
