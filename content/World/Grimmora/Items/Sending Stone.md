# Sending Stone

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** Wondrous Item
> **Attunement:** No
> **Value:** Unknown

## Visual Description
A small stone utilized for magical communication over vast distances.

## Mechanics & Effects
The stone allows the bearer to send a short, secure message to the holder of the paired stone and receive a brief reply.

## Lore & History
The Sending Stone was given to [[Lavender]] by her brother, [[Orris Locke]], during their tense reunion in [[Orris' Secret Lair|his secret lair]] beneath the [[Mayor's Mansion (Arcadia)|Mayor's Mansion]] in [[Arcadia]]. Knowing they were being watched by other members of the [[Necromancers]], Orris secretly passed the stone to Lavender and instructed her to use it to contact him only after she and the party had safely reached the city of [[Adalens]]. 

Upon arriving in the capital, the party gathered to use the stone. [[Mary-Ann]] declared she was "fully in," and they coordinated a rendezvous with Orris outside the [[Blooming Lovely]] florist, signing the message "LX3". They received the brief reply: "Understood." It was later revealed that the reply was actually sent by Lady [[Elara Mythin]], as Orris had been forced to flee and lay low.
