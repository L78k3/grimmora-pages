# Imperial Courier Bag

> [!info] Obtained
> **When:** [[Entry 34]]
> **Where:** [[Paddle Steamer]] (Loraleia's Cabin)
> **Current Owner:** [[Gage]] (Contents removed/copied)

The **Imperial Courier Bag** is a satchel belonging to [[Loraleia]], found during the investigation of the sickroom aboard the [[Paddle Steamer]].

## Description

The bag bears a specific insignia which [[Theren]] identified as that of an **Imperial Courier**—an official service carrying correspondence for the wealthy and important.

## Contents

When found, the bag contained five **[[Encoded Letters]]**.
* Three letters had no monogram.
* Two letters bore the monogram of the sender (O.L.) and recipient (E.M.).

[[Gage]] carefully pried off the seals, copied the contents, and restored the seals to avoid detection.
