# Grey Pearl

> [!info] Obtained
> **When:** [[Entry 38]]
> **Where:** [[Onasea Sea Cave]]
> **Given By:** [[Corbin]]
> **Current Owner:** [[Lavender]]

The **Grey Pearl** is a small, unassuming gemstone that serves as a token of passage.

## History

[[Corbin]] received this pearl from a "noble-looking man" (revealed in [[Lavender]]'s vision to be [[Orris]]).

## Purpose

Orris instructed Corbin to give the pearl to a woman of "noble background and military bearing" ([[Lavender]]) if she came through his network.

The pearl is a signal for [[Captain Voss]]. Showing her the pearl will secure immediate passage to [[Adalens]] with "no questions asked," ensuring Lavender's safety from the forces hunting her.

## Significance

Orris's message accompanying the pearl warned that Lavender "doesn't know what she has," likely referring to the revelation that the goddess [[Auriel]] may be trapped inside her [[Ring of Orris]].
