# Grey Pearl

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** Key Item
> **Attunement:** No
> **Value:** Unknown

## Visual Description
The Grey Pearl is a small, unassuming gemstone.

## Mechanics & Effects
The pearl serves as a token of passage. It is a signal for [[Captain Voss]]; showing her the pearl secures immediate passage to [[Adalens]] with "no questions asked," ensuring [[Lavender]]'s safety from the forces hunting her.

## Lore & History
[[Corbin]] received this pearl from a "noble-looking man," revealed through [[Lavender]]'s vision to be [[Orris]]. Orris instructed Corbin to deliver the pearl to a woman of "noble background and military bearing" if she came through his network. 

The message accompanying the pearl warned that Lavender "doesn't know what she has," which likely refers to the revelation that the goddess [[Auriel]] may be trapped inside Lavender's [[Ring of Orris]].
