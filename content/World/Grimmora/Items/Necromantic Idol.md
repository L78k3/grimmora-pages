# Necromantic Idol

> [!abstract] Properties
> **Type:** Ritual Focus
> **Attunement:** N/A
> **Value:** 0 gp (Destroyed)

## Visual Description
The idol appeared as a miniature statue positioned on the [[Bridgeport Mason]]'s workbench. It was characterized by a "glowing swirl of energy" that surrounded the object.

## Mechanics & Effects
* **Combat Autonomy**: The idol acted independently during combat as a source of control and offense.
* **Psychic Pulse**: It utilized psychic attacks that successfully stunned [[Theren]] and [[Lavender]] and dispelled [[Runcible]].
* **Immunities**: The object was identified by [[Temerity]] as being immune to psychic damage, poison damage, and all status effects.
* **Weaknesses**: The idol was uniquely vulnerable to thunder damage.

## Lore & History
The Necromantic Idol served as the primary focal point for the necromantic ritual discovered within the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]. Its presence maintained a green glow of possession over the [[Bridgeport Mason]] and provided the energy required to animate various stone statues and a [clay golem](https://2014.5e.tools/bestiary.html#clay%20golem_mm). 

Upon its destruction, the necromantic hold on the room was broken: the animated constructs solidified and the mason was freed from his possessed state.

## Acquisition
* **Obtained In**: [[Entry 32]]
* **Method**: The item was destroyed by [[Theren]], who utilized the **[shatter](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#shatter_phb)** spell to disintegrate the idol into a pile of sand. [[Gage]] subsequently collected the sand remains to be delivered to [[Halsen]] for further study.
