# Necromantic Idol

> [!danger] Destroyed
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 32]]
> **Location:** [[Bridgeport Warehouse]]
> **Status:** Disintegrated into sand

The **Necromantic Idol** was a dangerous magical object that served as the focal point for the necromantic ritual in the [[Bridgeport Warehouse]].

## Description

It appeared as a miniature statue sitting atop the [[Bridgeport Mason]]'s workbench, surrounded by a "glowing swirl of energy".

## Abilities

During combat, the idol acted independently, serving as a source of control and offense:
* **Psychic Pulse:** It launched psychic attacks that stunned [[Theren]] and [[Lavender]] and dispelled [[Runcible]].
* **Resistances:** [[Temerity]] identified that it was immune to psychic and poison damage and could not be inflicted with status effects.
* **Weakness:** It was vulnerable to **thunder** damage.

## Destruction

[[Theren]] destroyed the idol by casting **[shatter](https://2014.5e.tools/spells.html#shatter_phb)**. Upon destruction, it disintegrated into a pile of sand.

The destruction of the idol immediately broke the necromantic hold on the room: the animated statues and golem solidified, and the green glow of possession left the [[Bridgeport Mason]].

## Loot

[[Gage]] collected the **sand** remains of the idol to take back to [[Halsen]] for study.
