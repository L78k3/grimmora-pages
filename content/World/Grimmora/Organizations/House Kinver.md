# House Kinver

> [!info] Status: Neutral
> **Encountered:** Mentioned in [[Entry 42]]
> **Current Goal/Base:** Operating from [[Adalens]] as one of the five ruling families, overseeing the city's military.

## Overview

House Kinver is one of the five powerful ruling families in the metropolis of [[Adalens]]. They hold authority over the military forces of the guild-run city. Notably, House Kinver is an offshoot of [[House Locke]], sharing a noble lineage with [[Lavender]]. Like the other ruling families, they are known to have "eyes everywhere," contributing to the high level of surveillance and political tension within the region.

## Composition

While their exact internal demographics are unknown, House Kinver commands the military might of [[Adalens]]. Their influence naturally extends to the city's armed forces, the city watch, and the guards that patrol locations like the main gates and [[Port Adal]].

## Objectives

To maintain military supremacy and security within the metropolis while balancing political power alongside the other four ruling families: [[House Nightbrook]] (Information), [[House Silverwind]] (Artisans), [[House Crestwood]] (Merchants), and [[House Mythin]] (Scholars).

## Our Interactions

The party has not directly interacted with members of House Kinver. They learned of the family's existence, their dominion over the military, and their historical connection to [[House Locke]] from [[Captain Voss]] and local knowledge upon arriving in the region in [[Entry 42]].
