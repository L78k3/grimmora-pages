# Mages' Guild

> [!info] Status: Neutral
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 42]]
> **Current Goal/Base:** Governing [[Adalens]] from their headquarters in a repurposed massive old temple located in the Guild Quarter.

## Overview

The Mages' Guild is one of the premier ruling bodies of the metropolis of [[Adalens]]. Operating as part of a council of powerful guilds that govern the city, the organization is overseen by an elected Arch-mage. Their headquarters is situated in the heart of the city within a massive, old temple that has been repurposed for their needs.

## Composition

The guild is composed of spellcasters, scholars, and magical researchers who hold significant political power in [[Adalens]]. Their facilities include highly secure and specialized areas such as the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]].

## Objectives

As a ruling guild, their primary directive includes governing the city of [[Adalens]] in conjunction with the Merchants, Artisans, Scholars, Navigators, and Hospitallers guilds, while advancing arcane study and maintaining their political influence over the region of [[Nethis]].

## Notable Members

* **Elected Arch-mage:** The leader of the guild and a primary executive figure in the city's government.
* **[[Orris Locke]]:** A known member who is currently laying low within the city to avoid pursuers.
* **The Alchemist:** A mysterious figure believed to be working within the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]].

## Our Interactions

The party learned of the Mages' Guild's significant authority upon their arrival in [[Adalens]]. During a meeting at the [[House Mythin]] estate, Lady [[Elara Mythin]] revealed that "The Alchemist" could be found within the guild's [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]]. This revelation led [[Lavender]] to the realization that her brother, [[Orris Locke]], is a member of the guild. The party has arranged to meet Elara at the sanctum to locate The Alchemist.
