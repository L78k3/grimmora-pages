# House Crestwood

> [!info] Status: Neutral
> **Encountered:** Mentioned (Unencountered)
> **Current Goal/Base:** Operating from their estate in the affluent area of [[Adalens]], maintaining control over the city's commerce.

## Overview

House Crestwood is one of the five powerful ruling families of the metropolis of [[Adalens]]. They are explicitly associated with the Merchants of the city. Along with the other ruling houses, they are rumored to have "eyes everywhere" and keep a strict, vigilant watch over the districts of the capital. 

## Composition

While the exact composition of their house is currently unknown, they are an affluent noble family heavily tied to the city's merchant guilds and commerce. They likely employ a vast network of traders, informants, and retainers.

## Objectives

As one of the five ruling families, their primary objective is to maintain the balance of power and govern [[Adalens]] alongside the elected Arch-mage. Specifically, House Crestwood focuses on the economic and mercantile stability of the city.
