# Greengather Blades

> [!info] **Status:** Neutral  
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 15]]  
> **Current Goal/Base:** Nomadic; primarily active in forest regions within [[The Frost]] and surrounding territories.

## Overview
The Greengather Blades are a nomadic, forest-based mercenary clan known for selling their martial and scouting services to the highest bidder. They are perceived as highly efficient but ruthless, holding no specific political allegiances. They serve as the former employers and kin of [[Gage]].

## Composition
The clan operates with strict military discipline, training its members from a young age to become masters of ambush, survival, and guerrilla warfare. Their culture is defined by a blend of gnomish ingenuity and extreme pragmatism. The rank-and-file consists of elite scouts and killers who are taught to operate in complete silence and strike without hesitation.

## Objectives
The primary directive of the Greengather Blades is the fulfillment of mercenary contracts for profit. They specialise in long-term surveillance, reconnaissance, and high-efficiency ambushes, prioritising the completion of the contract over the environmental or human cost.

## Notable Members
* **[[Gage]]**: Former elite spy and scout known as "The Greenblade"; now a defector.
* **Gage’s Brother**: An older sibling and mentor who first prompted [[Gage]] to question the clan's mercenary ethics.

## Our Interactions
The party first became aware of the Greengather Blades when [[Gage]] revealed he had been hired by the [[Necromancers]] to monitor [[Lavender]], [[Temerity]], and [[Theren]]. During an encounter outside [[Arcadia]], [[Gage]] witnessed the devastation wrought by his employers and chose to defect from the Blades, skewering a guard to save the party. He now fights against the interests of the clan’s former clients.
