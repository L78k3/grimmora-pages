# House Mythin

> [!info] Status: Allied
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 42]]
> **Current Goal/Base:** Operating from their heavily warded estate in the affluent district of [[Adalens]]. They are currently assisting the party in locating "The Alchemist" within the Arch-mage's Sanctum.

## Overview

House Mythin is one of the five ruling families that balance power within the metropolis of [[Adalens]]. They specifically represent the city's Scholars. Like the other ruling families of the city, they are rumored by the populace to have spies and "eyes everywhere" across all districts. 

## Composition

As a wealthy ruling family, they maintain a massive estate protected by shimmering magical wards in its corridors. Their household staff is diverse, employing individuals such as a goblin butler and a small bugbear guide to attend to guests and manage their sprawling manor.

## Objectives

House Mythin aligns itself "on the side of knowledge." Their current directive involves aiding their correspondent, [[Orris Locke]], who was forced to flee into hiding. They are actively working to uncover the identity of "The Alchemist" within the [[Mages' Guild]] to understand the broader conspiracy threatening the region.

## Notable Members

* **[[Elara Mythin]]:** The Lady and Mistress of the house. A trusted correspondent of [[Orris Locke]] who intercepted the party's [[Sending Stone]] message to ensure their safety.
* **[[Grawlock]]:** The goblin butler who manages the front doors of the estate.

## Our Interactions

The party first interacted with House Mythin in **[[Entry 42]]**. After attempting to contact [[Orris Locke]] using their [[Sending Stone]], the party was redirected to the House Mythin estate by a halfling messenger. [[Lavender]], [[Mary-Ann]], and [[Anaphel]] secured an audience with Lady [[Elara Mythin]], who revealed that Orris had fled because "someone is onto him." She pledged her allegiance to their cause and agreed to meet the party the following morning to search the [[Arch-mage's Sanctum]] for "The Alchemist."
