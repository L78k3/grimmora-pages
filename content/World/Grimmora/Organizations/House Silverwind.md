# House Silverwind

> [!info] Status: Neutral
> **Encountered:** Mentioned in [[Entry 42]]
> **Current Goal/Base:** Operating out of the city of [[Adalens]], overseeing the artisan sector.

## Overview

House Silverwind is one of the five powerful ruling families that hold power in the constructed metropolis of [[Adalens]]. In the city's complex, guild-run government overseen by the Arch-mage, House Silverwind specifically governs the domain of Artisans.

## Composition

While the exact internal composition of the family is unknown, they are intrinsically tied to the artisan guilds of the city. According to warnings from [[Captain Voss]], House Silverwind, like the other ruling families, has "eyes everywhere," indicating they employ an extensive and highly effective network of spies and informants throughout [[Adalens]] and [[Port Adal]].

## Objectives

Their primary objective is maintaining their balance of power alongside the other four ruling families (House Nightbrook, House Crestwood, House Mythin, and House Kinver) while governing the artisans and protecting their political interests through surveillance.

## Our Interactions

The party has not yet interacted directly with House Silverwind. They learned of the family from [[Captain Voss]] upon arriving in [[Port Adal]]. The warning regarding the family's pervasive spy network prompted the party to adopt aliases before entering the capital.
