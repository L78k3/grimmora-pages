# Adalens Thieves' Guild

> [!info] Status: Hostile
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 37]] (via hired agents)
> **Current Goal/Base:** Operating out of [[Adalens]], issuing contracts, robberies, and hits for gold.

## Overview

The Adalens Thieves' Guild is a criminal syndicate based in the constructed metropolis of [[Adalens]]. They operate a wide-reaching network capable of organizing hits and ambushes far beyond the city walls, frequently utilizing external bandit groups and highwaymen to execute their contracts without directly implicating the guild's core members.

## Composition

While the internal structure, leadership, and exact demographics of the guild are currently unknown, they possess significant resources and influence to hire independent crews. It is also established that the rogue [[Gage]] has some past or existing links to the organization, which he keeps carefully hidden.

## Objectives

The guild seeks profit through robbery, coordinated crime, and targeted assassinations. Recently, they issued a specific contract to acquire gold by targeting the affluent noblewoman and party member, [[Mary-Ann Mayfield|Mary-Ann]].

## Notable Members

* **Guildmaster (Unknown):** The unidentified head of the Thieves' Guild who personally ordered the hit on Mary-Ann.
* **[[Greg (Bandit Captain)|Greg]]:** An external bandit captain hired by the guild to execute the ambush on the road to [[Onasea]] (Deceased).

## Our Interactions

The party first became aware of the guild after surviving an ambush on the fog-shrouded road to [[Onasea]]. After capturing the bandit leader, [[Greg (Bandit Captain)|Greg]], the party interrogated him using *zone of truth* and *detect thoughts* in [[Entry 37]]. Greg confessed that he was hired via a letter from the head of the Thieves' Guild in [[Adalens]] to specifically target [[Mary-Ann|Mary-Ann]]. 

Later, upon the party's arrival in [[Adalens]] and their visit to the respectable [[Guild’s Rest]] tavern in [[Entry 42]], [[Gage]] intuited the establishment's strict nature and deliberately decided to keep his personal links to the Thieves' Guild a closely guarded secret from both the locals and his party.
