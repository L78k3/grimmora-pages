# House Nightbrook

> [!info] Status: Neutral
> **Encountered:** Mentioned in [[Entry 42]]
> **Current Goal/Base:** Gathering intelligence and maintaining power from their estate in the affluent area of [[Adalens]].

## Overview

House Nightbrook is one of the five ruling families that balance power within the guild-run metropolis of [[Adalens]]. They specialize in the domain of "Information" and are known to have "eyes everywhere" throughout the city's districts. 

## Composition

The specific composition of the house is unknown, though their reputation heavily implies they employ a vast network of spies, informants, and surveillance agents across [[Adalens]] and its surrounding territories like [[Port Adal]].

## Objectives

While their explicit goals remain unconfirmed, they actively work to maintain their influence and balance of power within the city's complex political landscape through constant vigilance and intelligence gathering.

## Our Interactions

The party has not yet interacted directly with House Nightbrook. However, upon arriving in the region, [[Captain Voss]] warned them that the city "has eyes," prompting the party to use aliases - such as "Eloise Rosewood" and "Littlerock Sprockett" - to avoid detection by the extensive surveillance networks maintained by families like Nightbrook.
