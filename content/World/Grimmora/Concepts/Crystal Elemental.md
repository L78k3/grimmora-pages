# Crystal Elemental

> [!info] Creature Info
> **Type:** Elemental
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 24]]
> **Location:** Cave near [[Midward]]

The **Crystal Elemental** is a unique elemental entity composed of jagged crystals and swirling dust.

## Description

We encountered this creature in a dark cave on the outskirts of [[Midward]]. It manifested as a huge formation of crystals surrounded by a "whirlwind of crystal dust". It emits a menacing screech described as sounding like "fingernails on a blackboard".

## Abilities & Traits

* **Crystal Shards:** It can fire sharp crystal shards at its enemies as a ranged attack.
* **Slam:** It possesses a powerful slam attack.
* **Defences:** It appears highly resistant to force damage; [[Theren]]'s [[agonising blast]] merely "pinged off" its crystals.
* **Weaknesses:** It is vulnerable to thunder damage. A [[shatter]] spell caused several of its crystals to explode, inflicting significant pain and damage.

## Our Experience

We tracked this elemental to its lair after a local farmer reported his rams were being savaged. The corpses showed jagged cuts inconsistent with normal animal attacks.

During the battle, the elemental proved dangerous, damaging [[Gage]] and [[Lavender]] with both slam attacks and fired shards. We discovered that shattering its form was key to defeating it. [[Anaphel]] finished it off by freezing the crystals with [toll the dead](), after which a tap from their sword caused the entity to shatter completely.

We harvested **crystal shards** and **crystal dust** from its remains.
