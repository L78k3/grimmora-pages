### Pellor

[[House Locke]]'s family deity, mentioned as her primary knowledge of extra-planar entities. Lavender's family's moral framework is largely based on their virtues.

## History
* **[[Entry 39]]:** Discussed by [[Theren]], [[Anaphel]], and [[Lavender]] as a sun/light/healing god during their research into potential adversaries of [[Auriel]]. Lavender confirmed that Pellor is worshipped by [[House Locke]].
