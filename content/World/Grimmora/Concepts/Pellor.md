# Pellor

> [!info] Deity
> **Domain:** Sun, Light, Healing
> **Status:** Worshipped by [[House Locke]]

## Overview
Pellor is a god of the sun, light, and healing. He is the family deity of [[House Locke]], and [[Lavender]]'s family bases their moral framework largely on his virtues. Consequently, Pellor constitutes Lavender's primary point of reference regarding extra-planar entities.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 05]] (Extra-Planar Knowledge):** Mentioned as [[Lavender]]'s sole source of knowledge regarding extra-planar beings when discussing the topic with [[Temerity]] in the [[Vampire Mansion]].
* **[[Entry 39]] (Divine Research):** Discussed by [[Theren]], [[Anaphel]], and [[Lavender]] as a sun, light, and healing god during their research aboard the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]] to identify potential divine adversaries of [[Auriel]]. Lavender confirmed to the group that Pellor is actively worshipped by [[House Locke]].
* **[[Entry 41]] (Interrogation of Lyra):** During the interrogation of the captive high priestess Lyra Valstrath, Lavender specifically asked her about Pellor to determine if he was the entity opposing the Master of Ash. However, Lyra offered no response to the deity's name.
