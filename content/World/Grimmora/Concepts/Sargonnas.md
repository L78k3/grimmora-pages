**Sargonnas** is the God of Vengeance and Fire, suspected by the party to be the divine patron or identity of the [[Master of Ash]].

## Biography & Personality
Sargonnas is a deity associated with the domains of fire, heat, and vengeance. While his direct presence has not been manifested to the party, his influence is felt through his high priestess, Lyra Valstrath, and his followers who operate under the title of the [[Master of Ash]]. 

According to Lyra, Sargonnas (as the Master of Ash) seeks to destroy his competition - specifically the goddess [[Auriel]] - to tip the cosmic scales of balance in his favour. He is described as being more powerful than can be imagined, a being who seeks total control and the fulfilment of his vengeance. His followers utilize fire-based weaponry, such as flame cannons and fiery whips, and are surrounded by an intense heat that reflects his divine domain.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 39]] (The Divine Counterpart):** During the voyage on the [[Tidecaller (Ship)|Tidecaller]], [[Theren]] identifies Sargonnas during religious research as a potential divine adversary to [[Auriel]], noting his association with fire and vengeance.
* **[[Entry 41]] (The Name Confirmed):** During the interrogation of Lyra Valstrath, [[Theren]] mentions the name "Sargonis" (Sargonnas), which elicits a visible flash of recognition from the high priestess, confirming his relevance to their enemies. Lyra reveals that her master intends to destroy the one inside the [[Ring of Orris]] to ensure his own power.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Auriel]]:** His primary divine rival. Sargonnas seeks to destroy her to eliminate his competition for cosmic balance.
* **[[Lavender]]:** Currently holds the [[Ring of Orris]] containing the "competition" he seeks to destroy; his followers have orders to take it by force.
* **[[Lyra Valstrath]]:** His High Priestess, who acts as his voice and enforcer in the mortal realm.
* **[[Necromancers]]:** The mortal organization and cult-like following that operates in his name to secure artefacts like the [[Winter's Core]].

## Notable Equipment
* **[[Winter's Core]] (Related):** The powerful artefact (suspected to be [[Auriel]] or her essence) that his followers are desperately searching for to destroy.
