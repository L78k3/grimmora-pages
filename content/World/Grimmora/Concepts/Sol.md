# Sol

> [!info] Deity
> **Domain:** Sun
> **Status:** Major Religion

**Sol** is the god of the sun. His worship represents one of the "big, old religions" in the world of Grimmora.

## Worship

A major temple dedicated to Sol is located in [[Avalon]].
