# Necromancer Symbol (Diamond Eyes)

> [!info] Faction Symbol
> **Organization:** [[necromancers]]
> **Location:** [[Arcadia]]

We have encountered a recurring symbol associated with the necromancers in [[Arcadia]]: a **skull with diamond eyes**.

However, we have observed at least two variations:

* **Cavern Symbol:** The symbol seen on the walls inside [[Orris' Secret Lair]].
* **Corpse Symbol:** A second version found on the arms of the necromancer corpses in the [[Arcadia]] town square. This symbol was **inverted** and used **dark blue and green** colours.

The distinction between these two symbols is currently unknown, but they may represent different ranks, factions, or specializations within the necromancer organization.
