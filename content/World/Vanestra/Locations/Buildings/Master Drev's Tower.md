# Master Drev's Tower

> [!info] Quick Reference
> **Location:** [[The Northern Forest]], North of Daggatt
> **Type:** Wizard's Tower
> **Status:** Restricted

## Description

An isolated wizard's tower located deep in a forest a few hours north of the town of Daggatt. It stands as a solitary structure surrounded by the wilderness, far from the immediate and watchful eye of the central Triumvirate.

## Purpose

It serves as the private residence, research facility, and sanctuary for Master Drev and his apprentice. It is used primarily to study magical wards and shields in relative isolation.

## Atmosphere

Dusty, quiet, and filled with academic tension. The atmosphere is strained and oppressive due to Drev's lingering grief over his former apprentice and his ongoing resentment of Gwilym.

## Notable Features

The Restricted Library: A repository containing high-level texts on wards, magical shields, and the unredacted, esoteric histories of the Triumvirate's purges.

## Events

Pre-Campaign: Gwilym was brought here to begin his apprenticeship after Master Drev pulled rank to claim him during an Inquisitor sweep in Hjemmen.
