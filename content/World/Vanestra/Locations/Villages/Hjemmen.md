# Hjemmen

> [!warning] Unvisited
> The party has not yet traveled to this location. Remove this callout when visited.

## What We Know
Hjemmen is a town located to the northwest of Master Drev's tower. It is Gwilym's birthplace and the site of the brutal Inquisitor sweeps that defined his childhood.

## Character
The general feel is oppressive and paranoid. Due to the presence of Bishop Greggan, the town is heavily policed by the Disciples of War. Citizens keep their heads down, and worshippers of unofficial deities rely heavily on secret symbols to communicate.

## Points of Interest
* **The Alleys:** The former stomping grounds of Gwilym's found family. 
* **The Bishop's Seat:** The local headquarters for the Disciples of War.
