# Disciples of War

> [!info] Status: Hostile
> **Encountered:** Pre-Campaign
> **Current Goal:** Conducting street sweeps, hunting heretics, and rounding up unregistered dissidents in Hjemmen and surrounding settlements.

## Overview

The Disciples of War serve as the militant, inquisitorial arm of [[The Triumvirate]]. They are the boots on the ground that enforce the state's totalitarian will. They police major towns and villages, operating under a strict and highly complex set of esoteric rules that govern how they identify, hunt, and process enemies of the state.

## Composition

They are heavily armed zealots, comprised primarily of War Clerics, inquisitors, and royal soldiers. When conducting major operations, they are often protected by state-trained Abjuration wizards who act as magical shields. 

## Objectives

To root out and destroy hidden worshippers (such as those of Sune, Mystra, or Oghma) and to round up vulnerable populations - like street urchins - to be institutionalized and brainwashed into serving the state.

## Notable Members

* **[[Bishop Greggan]]:** The fanatical Bishop who rules the town of Hjemmen with an iron fist. 

## Our Interactions

The Disciples of War are directly responsible for the trauma of [[Gwilym|Gwilym's]] past. They conducted the brutal alleyway sweeps in Hjemmen that scattered his found family and led to his capture. Gwilym is currently fixated on decoding their esoteric patrol rules so he can intercept their ledgers, forge documents, and warn their targets before a sweep happens.
