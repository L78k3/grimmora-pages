# The Triumvirate

> [!info] Status: Hostile
> **Encountered:** Pre-Campaign
> **Current Goal:** Maintaining totalitarian control over the realm and enforcing a strict, unified religious and political hierarchy.

## Overview

The Triumvirate is the overarching totalitarian state and religious authority that rules the land. They are a massive, suffocating bureaucracy that maintains a strict monopoly on spiritual and civic life. They do not tolerate dissent, open rebellion, or the worship of unapproved deities, moving swiftly to make heretics and dissidents "disappear."

## Composition

A vast network of politicians, clerics, state-sanctioned scholars, and military inquisitors. They maintain elite state academies where they train captured youths to become clerics. While they largely distrust arcane magic, they tolerate a select few powerful wizards (like Master Drev) who serve the state's interests.

## Objectives

Absolute control and the eradication of "free association." They seek to stamp out all underground resistance, private shrines, and independent thought through a complex system of esoteric laws and brutal enforcement.

## Notable Members

* **[[Bishop Greggan]]:** A high-ranking, fanatical leader within their military arm.
* **[[Master Drev]]:** A sanctioned Abjuration Master (though he prefers to remain isolated from central politics).

## Our Interactions

[[Gwilym]] holds a deep, quiet hatred for the Triumvirate. They are responsible for the constant threat that loomed over his childhood, the eventual capture that forced him into a life of academic servitude, and the disappearance of his found family. He actively uses his position as a Cloistered Scholar to subvert their rules from the inside.
