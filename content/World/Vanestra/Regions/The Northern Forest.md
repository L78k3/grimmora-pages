# The Northern Forest

> [!info] Status
> **Exploration:** Partially explored
> **Capital/Major City:** Unknown

## Overview

A dense, isolated forested area located a few hours north of the town of Daggatt. Its primary defining feature is its remote nature, offering a buffer from the immediate and watchful eye of the central Triumvirate bureaucracy.

## Geography

Heavily forested terrain. Climate and specific geographical features (like rivers or mountain ranges) are currently unknown.

## Major Locations

### Cities
- None known.

### Towns & Villages
- None known within the forest itself (Daggatt lies to the south).

### Landmarks
- [[Master Drev's Tower]] - An isolated wizard's tower hidden deep within the trees.

## What We Know

It is secluded enough that a known, powerful wizard like Master Drev can maintain a residence and a restricted library without constant, daily harassment from the Disciples of War.

## People & Culture

Largely uninhabited, save for recluses and scholars seeking isolation. 

## Dangers

Currently unknown, aside from the ever-present threat of Triumvirate Inquisitors or sweeps that might pass through the area.

## Connected Regions

- **South:** The region surrounding [[Daggatt]]

## Our Experience

[[Gwilym]] currently resides here, deep in the forest at Master Drev's Tower. It serves as his relatively safe base of operations to study abjuration magic and prepare for scouting runs into nearby towns.
