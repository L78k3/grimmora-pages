# Withered Rose

> [!danger] Destroyed
> **Encountered:** [[Entry 34]]
> **Location:** Cargo hold of the [[Paddle Steamer]]
> **Effect:** Caused a degenerative necromantic plague.

The **Withered Rose** was a cursed object hidden inside a crate of herbs and spell components in the cargo hold of the [[Paddle Steamer]].

## Properties

* **Necromantic Disease:** The rose acted as a beacon or source for a degenerative disease that drained the life essence of those nearby. It affected [[Loraleia]] most severely but had begun spreading to other passengers.
* **Appearance:** A rose with withered petals that smelled "very bad" to [[Korako]].

## Destruction

[[Temerity]] destroyed the rose by casting **[[sacred flame]]**, immolating it completely. Its destruction immediately stopped the spread of the curse, allowing the victims to be healed.
