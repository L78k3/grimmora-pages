**Garratt** is a stocky, dwarven guard stationed in the warehouse district of [[Adalens]] who accepted a bribe to look the other way during a kidnapping.

## Biography & Personality
Garratt serves as a watchman in the warehouse district of the capital city. Despite his duty to protect the area, he is corruptible and willing to turn a blind eye to criminal activities if the price is right. He is initially evasive and attempts to lie to cover up his indiscretions, but he lacks the fortitude to maintain his deception when pressured by intimidating figures or subjected to magical scrutiny.

## Campaign History
* **[[Entry 43]] (The Bribed Guard):** The party tracked down Garratt while investigating the abduction of [[Petra Greenfield]]. Although he initially denied seeing anything, [[Temerity]] used *detect thoughts* to uncover memories of money changing hands. Under intimidation from [[Lavender]], Garratt confessed that he was paid by a hooded, cloaked figure with a scarred hand to ignore the kidnapping and the sound of a smashing bottle.

## Key Relationships
* **[[Lavender]]:** She easily saw through his lies and successfully intimidated him into breaking his silence to reveal the truth about the bribe.
* **[[Temerity]]:** Used magic to scan the surface of Garratt's mind, bypassing his lies to uncover the visual memory of the bribe taking place.
