# ⚔️ Grimmora: Rime of the Soul-Stolen

Welcome to the central archive for the **Grimmora** D&D campaign. This vault serves as a player-facing repository documenting the party's journey, the evolving world atlas, and the mechanics of our ongoing narrative.

## 📜 Campaign Overview
**Current Campaign:** *Rime of the Soul-Stolen*
The story follows a continent-spanning struggle against a rising necromantic order marked by the skull-and-diamond-eye symbol. What began as a simple rescue mission for the "kidnapped" Lady Bromelia has evolved into a quest to prevent environmental sabotage and liberate the goddess Auriel, who is believed to be trapped within a magical ring.

**Current Status:** The party is currently hiding at the **Bandit Lake House** near Onasea. The immediate goal is to secure passage on the *Tidecaller* and escape the region for the island city of Adalens.

## 🛡️ The Party
* **Gage**: Rock Gnome Rogue/Ranger; a former spy for the necromancers turned "Greenblade" freedom fighter.
* **Lavender**: Human Barbarian/Paladin; the chosen Champion of Auriel and eldest child of House Locke.
* **Temerity**: Tiefling Bard/Warlock; a flamboyant performer and caretaker of a magical menagerie.
* **Theren**: Half-Elf Warlock; a former vagabond bound to an enigmatic Golden Patron.
* **Mary-Ann**: Halfling Rogue; a high-ranking noble widow from Avalon with a ruthless protective streak.
* **Anaphel**: Celestial Aasimar; the direct offspring of Auriel, currently cut off from her mother's light.

## 📂 Vault Structure
This project uses **Quartz 4.0** to transform Obsidian markdown files into a searchable web interface. The core data is organized within the `content/` directory:

* **Campaigns**: Contains the Chronicles (session-by-session logs), character biographies, and active quest tracking.
* **World**: The Atlas. Detailed documentation of regions like the Avalonean Empire and The Frost, as well as planes like the Fey Realm.
* **System**: Internal templates, automation scripts, and workflow documentation.

## 🛠️ Technical Details
This vault is optimized for **Obsidian** and published via **Quartz**.
* **Perspective:** Player-facing.
* **Language:** British English.
* **Automation:** The root directory includes `generatePages.py`, a utility script for processing raw session notes into structured wiki updates using a custom architect pipeline.
